Today, the Emperor Penguin is listed by the IUCN as a species that is of Least Concern from becoming extinct in the wild in the near future.In fact, their southerly-dwelling nature may mean that they are the least vulnerable of the eighteen different species of penguin.mperor Penguin Diet and Prey. The Emperor Penguin is a carnivorous animal that only hunts and eats animals in the surrounding water in order to survive. Fish and krill make up the bulk of their diet along with squid and crustaceans.
