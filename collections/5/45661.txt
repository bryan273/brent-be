This surname recorded in the spellings of Van Arsdale, Van Arsdalen, Van Arsdall, etc. seems to originate from one settler Symon Janse Van Arsdale, and all nameholders, however the name is spelt, are relatives of this one man.ater recordings include George Van Arsdall, who married Zervia Andrews, at Pine Bush, Orange County, New York, on February 13th 1849. He may have been the son of Henry Vanarsdaal, of Montgomery, Orange County, New York state, who was recorded there on December 29th 1812.
