Courses that prepare students for veterinary school tend to include: Microbiology ; Animal management ; Anatomy and physiology ; Animal nutrition ; Equine care ; Doctor of Veterinary Medicine. DVM programs take four years to complete and are generally divided into two-year segments. The first segment typically consists of classroom instruction while the last two years focus more on clinical practicums. Coursework typically includes: Animal behavior ; Veterinary pharmacology ; Animal nutrition ; Clinical pathology
