First offense possession of marijuana/synthetic marijuana is a misdemeanor and is punishable by up to six months in jail. Second offence marijuana/synthetic marijuana is a felony that is punishable by up to 5 years in jail, and third offense is also a felony punishable by up to 20 years. The laws in Louisiana are very strict for marijuana and synthetic marijuana. It is very important to be represented by an attorney when charged with any type of marijuana offense to make sure your rights are protected.
