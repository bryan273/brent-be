Malay Apple. Syzygium malaccense. Common names Jambu merah, jambu bar, jambu bol, jambu melaka, jambu. kling jambu kapal Malay rose-apple, mountain apple, water. apple, Otaheite apple, Otaheite cashew, French Cashew. Origin Native of Malaysia, Sumatra and Java in Indonesia and. Vietnam. Belongs to Myrtaceae family.
