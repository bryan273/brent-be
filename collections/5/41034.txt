FEMA To Open A Mobile Disaster Recovery Center For Lake County Residents And Businesses October 18, 2017 The Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) has announced it will be opening a mobile Disaster Recovery Center (DRC) for residents and business owners affected by Hurricane Irma. The DRC will be open beginning Thursday, Oct. 19 at 1 pm. at the Lake County Extension Office, located at 1951 Woodlea Road, Tavares.
