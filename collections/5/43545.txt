It is the responsibility of your supervisors to guide you through the academic and administrative requirements of a PhD. Supervisors will: 1  meet with you regularly. 2  provide you with academic guidance and scholarly direction. 3  assess your progress and give you written feedback. 4  act as guides to University facilities.our supervisors will provide guidance on a range of academic matters. These include: 1  the standards required for a PhD. 2  planning your research. 3  skills you will need to acquire. 4  research resources. 5  methodology. 6  undertaking a literature review. 7  ethical, legal, professional and safety issues.
