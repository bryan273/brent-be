Cujo is a small ghostly dog that appears in the episode Shades of Gray.. Though seemingly harmless, he can change from a cute little puppy into an enormous, rabid dog monster. Cujo is the ghost of a guard dog for Axion Industries. He came back to the human world in order to reclaim a squeaky toy he had left in the dog kennels. Cujo first appeared at Danny's school and, after transforming, ran off to Axion Industries, with Danny in hot pursuit.
