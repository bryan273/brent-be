Theodore Roosevelt. For other people named Theodore Roosevelt, see Theodore Roosevelt (disambiguation). Theodore Roosevelt Jr. (/ˈroʊzəvɛlt/ ROH-zə-velt; October 27, 1858 – January 6, 1919) was an American statesman, author, explorer, soldier, naturalist, and reformer who served as the 26th president of the United States from 1901 to 1909.
