Stalking, is a crime, like domestic violence, that gives the perpetrator a feeling of power and control over his or her victim. Though all stalking definitions have the same meaning, they are worded very differently. Let’s take a look at the legal definition of stalking and other close interpretations of the laws. Legal definition of stalking The legal definition of stalking, according to the Department of Justice, is a “pattern of repeated threatening or harassing behaviors that directly or indirectly communicate a threat, or place the victim in fear.”
