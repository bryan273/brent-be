Totem poles are sculptures carved from large trees, such as the Western Red Cedar. In North America, totem poles are part of the cultures of many indigenous peoples of Alaska, British Columbia and the Pacific Northwest.Totem poles serve many purposes beyond their beauty, and their meanings are as varied as the cultures that make them.
