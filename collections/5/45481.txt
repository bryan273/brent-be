About Birds And Blooms. Birds and Blooms magazine was founded in 1995. Birds and Blooms magazine contains a dose of photos, reader tips and advices. There are six pages of expert advice in each issue. The magazine publishes are mail-order catalog offering a wide variety of country-oriented products, calendars and best-selling books including cookbooks, scenic photo books, nostalgia books and more with photos of birds, gardens and butterflies.
