‘The Sinner’ Anthology Drama Starring Jessica Biel Picked Up To Series By USA. USA Network has picked up its drama pilot The Sinner to series. The crime thriller anthology drama, from Universal Cable Prods., stars and is executive produced by Jessica Biel and also stars Bill Pullman. The Sinner, written by Derek Simonds (When We Rise), is part of a three-pilot batch at USA that also includes Damnation and The Tap.
