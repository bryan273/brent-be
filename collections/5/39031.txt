Holland-America's newest and most luxurious cruise ship, the ms Koningsdam, is homeported at Port Everglades for the 2017 Cruise Season. Crystal Cruises returns to Port Everglades in 2016 with the Nov. 8th arrival of the Crystal Serenity. Port Everglades is consistently ranked among the top three cruise ports in the world and welcomes approximately 4 million cruise guests annually. Port Everglades offers cruise guests more choices with a wide variety of itineraries ranging from 3 to 14-plus days.
