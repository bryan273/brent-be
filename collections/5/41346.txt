QuickBooks tracks the quantity and value of items that have a type of Inventory and Assembly. It uses an inventory sub-ledger to track individual items then updates the various general ledger accounts as required. The following accounts are used by QuickBooks to track inventory. Sample Name/Type of Account. What's its used for. Inventory Asset /. Other Current Asset. This account is used to track the values of inventory on hand. QuickBooks will create this account the first time an Inventory Part item is entered. This account should be used exclusively for Items.
