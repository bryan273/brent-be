Other Useful Guides: Using the apostrophe, Using the comma, Sentence structure. The semi-colon. The semi-colon represents a break within a sentence that is stronger than a comma, but less final than a full stop. It enables the writer to avoid over use of the comma and preserves the finality of the full stop. Semi-colons are used to separate items in a list and to link closely related sentences.
