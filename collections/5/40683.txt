How To Thaw. Here are a few safe methods to thaw frozen foods: 1. In The Refrigerator: 1  Plan ahead because it takes about one day to thaw most foods. 2  Place frozen food on a plate or in any container to catch the juices that may leak. 3  Place in bottom of refrigerator. 4  You may refreeze food that has been thawed in the refrigerator before or after cooking.
