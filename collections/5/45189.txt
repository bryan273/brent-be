Novack, a former stripper, married Novack Jr. in 1991. He was the son of Ben Novack Sr., builder of the storied Fontainebleau on Miami Beach. Novack Sr. died in 1985, and left most of his assets to his ex-wife, Bernice and his son. When Bernice Novack was killed in April 2009, her estate, valued at about $2 million, went to Novack Jr., her only son.
