Answer Wiki. The cost of replacing the wheel bearings definitely depends on your vehicle, but also if you’re looking to replace the front or rear wheel bearings. The national average for front wheel bearing replacement is $471 to $620 and for rear wheel bearing replacement, it’s $351 to $545 (quality parts + labor). You can go to RepairPal (.com) and enter in your car’s information + your zip code for a more accurate quote if it’s helpful!
