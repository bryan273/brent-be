A HISTORY of LEHIGH COUNTY PENNSYLVANIA Published by James J. Haurer 1902. Lower Macungie is bounded on the northeast by Salisbury township, southeast by Upper Milford township, northwest by Upper Macungie township, southwest by Berks county. Population in 1900 was 2,920. It is one of the richest townships in the county, soil is very fertile and productive and is of limestone formation.
