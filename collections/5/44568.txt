It was founded as a not-for-profit company in 1895 but was later re-incorporated by a private Act of Parliament, the National Trust Act 1907. Subsequent acts of Parliament between 1919 and 1978 amended and extended the Trust's powers and remit.or the year ended 28 February 2013, the Trust's total income was £475 million, up from £406 million in 2010. The largest sources of income were still membership subscriptions (£140.1 million), direct property income (£124.6 million) and legacies (£50.2 million).
