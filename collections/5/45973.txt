Then: When he joined Carey's American spin-off, Mochrie was already an established improv star, having appeared in over 70 episodes of the British version of 'Whose Line.' He went on to appear in every episode of the American version, and also earned a recurring role on 'The Drew Carey Show' as Eugene.Whose Line' followed two years later, then in 2007 Carey took to hosting a real game show, taking over for Bob Barker on the price-guessing game 'The Price Is Right.' In 2011, a slimmed down Carey called much of the 'Whose Line' gang back together for the short-lived comedy series 'Drew Carey's Improv-A-Ganza.'.
