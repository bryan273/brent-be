Lodi /ˈloʊ.daɪ/ LOH-daɪ is a city located in San Joaquin County, California, in the northern portion of California's Central Valley. The population was 62,134 at the 2010 census. Its estimated population as of July 1, 2013 was 64,338. Lodi is best known for being a center of winegrape production, although its vintages have traditionally been less prestigious than those of Sonoma and Napa counties.
