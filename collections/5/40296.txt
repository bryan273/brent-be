Foods that may trigger migraines: 1  Aged, canned, cured or processed meat, including bologna, game, ham, herring, hot dogs, pepperoni and sausage. 2  Aged cheese. 3  Alcoholic beverages, especially red wine. 4  Aspartame. 5  Avocados. 6  Beans, including pole, broad, lima, Italian, navy, pinto and garbanzo.
