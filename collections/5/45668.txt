Examples of early recordings include Cornelius Simonse Van Ardsdale, the son of the first nameholder, baptised at Flatlands, Kings County, on July 1st 1662, and Marretje Van Arsdale, christened at the same place on April 4 1678.ater recordings include George Van Arsdall, who married Zervia Andrews, at Pine Bush, Orange County, New York, on February 13th 1849. He may have been the son of Henry Vanarsdaal, of Montgomery, Orange County, New York state, who was recorded there on December 29th 1812.
