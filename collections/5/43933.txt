Ohio recently raised the state sales tax by 0.25% from 5.5% to 5.75%. Counties and cities as well as mass transit districts in Ohio are allowed to charge an additional local sales tax on top of the Ohio state sales tax (up to a total sales tax rate of 8.5%). All non-prepared food (groceries) is exempt from the Ohio sales tax with the exemption of soda and alcohol, which is taxed at a rate of 7%.
