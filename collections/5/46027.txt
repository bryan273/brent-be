Born Padmé Naberrie, Padmé Amidala served as the Queen and later Senator of the planet Naboo. She and secretly married the Jedi Anakin Skywalker and had two children, Luke and Leia. Padmé played an important role in the politics of the Clone Wars and, before her tragic early death, planted the seeds for the Rebellion that would eventually overthrow Palpatine's Empire.
