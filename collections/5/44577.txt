1 In a large pot, bring 1 quart of water to a boil. 2  Place shrimp in a steamer basket and place on top of the pot; cover. 3  Do not submerge shrimp. 4  Remove some water if necessary. 5  Season the shrimp with Old Bay seasoning. 6  Steam shrimp until pink. 7  Eat by removing shells as you go and dipping in cocktail sauce.
