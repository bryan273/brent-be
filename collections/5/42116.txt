Leucine is an essential amino acid which assists to regulate blood sugar and energy levels, production of the human growth hormone, wound healing as well as the growth and repair of muscle tissue. Leucine can also assist in the prevention of the breakdown of muscle proteins that may occur after severe stress or trauma. Important leucine facts. 1  Leucine is part of the three branched chain amino acids (BCAA) - the other two are valine and isoleucine.
