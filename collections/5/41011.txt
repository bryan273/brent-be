This is because everyone loves tomatoes. They are great in salads and sauces and even make a great gift. However, with these beautiful and tasty beauties comes a problem. Sometimes, right in the middle of thinking everything is alright with your crop, you will find splitting tomatoes or tomato cracking.n order to prevent splitting tomatoes, you need to make sure you water your tomato plants once a week with about one to two inches of water. To keep tomato cracking to a minimum, be sure to keep your tomato plants watered evenly on a regular basis.
