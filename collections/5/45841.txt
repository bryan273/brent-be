From Middle French soleil, from Old French soleil, from Vulgar Latin *soliclus < *soliculus, from Latin sōl, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sóh₂wl̥. Compare Friulian soreli, Italian sole, Ladin surëdl, Occitan solelh, Romanian soare, Romansch sulegl, Venetian sołe. Pronunciation . IPA : /sɔ.lɛj/
