How to Clean Ivory. Ivory comes from elephants. Please don't buy new ivory as our elephants need it more than we do. This is for cleaning antique ivory, for the elephants who already gave up their lives for our trinkets.ake a lemon rinse for yellowed ivory. Make a lemon rinse of 2 parts lemon juice and 2 parts water. Rub over the yellow to remove it. This will work for faint yellowing.
