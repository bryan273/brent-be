To a passing motorist unaware of the events taking place in the area, it could easily have seemed as if an entire town had mysteriously disappeared into thin air. Legends of Hell Town. Mutant Town and Mutane Town are two alternate names for the Hell Town area. Research on the stories has led us to conclude that the area was first called Mutant Town when the federal government bought up the land in 1974 to create the Cuyahoga Valley National Recreation Area.
