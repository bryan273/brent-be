Understanding the Training to be an Osteopathic Physician. An osteopathic physician is a licensed medical practitioner who was trained in the field of osteopathic medicine.. A Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine has the initials DO after his or her name. Like an MD, an osteopathic physician is a fully trained and licensed physician. DOs and MDs are considered equal, legally, and in terms of their authority to diagnose and treat various health conditions, prescribe medications, and perform surgery.
