The Warring States was a great period for military strategy; of the Seven Military Classics of China, four were written during this period: 1  The Art of War, attributed to Sun Tzu, a highly influential study of strategy and tactics.n 230 BC, Qin conquered Han. Han, the weakest of the Seven Warring States, was adjacent to the much stronger Qin, and had suffered continuous assaults by Qin in earlier years of the Warring States period.
