II. Connecting Your TV To Your Network. To begin connecting your TV to your wireless network, follow these steps: 1  Connect the wireless adapter to the USB port on your TV. 2  Press the MENU button, and then select Setup. 3  Select Network.4  If Network Type is set to Wired, select Network Type, and then select Wireless. 5  Select Network Setup. 6  Select Select a network.I.
