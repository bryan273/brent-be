Proper mowing can make the grass more tolerant to chinch bugs and greatly improve the appearance of the lawn. St. Augustine grass should be mowed to a height of 3-4 inches (as high as possible with a home mower) and it is very important to keep the mower blade sharpened (sharpen once per month).The grass should be mowed often enough so that no more than 1/3 of the leaf blade is removed at each mowing.t.
