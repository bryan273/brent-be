Thus the Strand of Cleena's Wave. South is at a land/sea intersect on the eastern side of the lowest dip of the land. North is near a high point but detail is obscured by vegetation. Drombeg stone circle is well known for having a winter solstice sunset alignment. Those wishing to see it should be there by 3:45pm at the latest. It is a popular gathering. The prominent notch indicated by the circle's axis supposedly marks winter solstice sunset but this is not true.
