To be called a pegmatite, a rock should be composed almost entirely of crystals that are at least one centimeter in diameter. The name pegmatite has nothing to do with the mineral composition of the rock. Most pegmatites have a composition that is similar to granite with abundant quartz, feldspar and mica.These are sometimes called granite pegmatites to indicate their mineralogical composition.he name pegmatite has nothing to do with the mineral composition of the rock.
