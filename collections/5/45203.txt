Thirty-seven percent said they consider feminist to be a negative term, compared to only 26 percent who consider it a positive term. Twenty-nine percent said it's a neutral term. Among Republicans, 58 percent said the term is mostly negative, compared to 40 percent of independents and 20 percent of Democrats.ut the vast majority fit the basic definition of the word.
