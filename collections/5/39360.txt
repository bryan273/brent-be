​What does AMRAP mean? Don’t worry, you are not alone in asking these questions. Both beginners, and even experienced CrossFitter’s, struggle with some of the acronyms. Here are some of the most common acronyms used in CrossFit and at CrossFit Power Grid. CFT:CrossFit Total – consisting of max squat, press, and deadlift. GHR(D): Glute ham raise (developer). Posterior chain exercise, like a back extension.
