To help answer your questions, we’d like to give a little further explanation about something called “Regulation D,” also known as “Reg D.” Regulation D is a Federal Reserve Board regulation that limits the number of transfers and withdrawals that can be made from savings and money market accounts through specific transaction channels. All financial institutions must comply with this regulation.
