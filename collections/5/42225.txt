Appearance and Anatomy of Bobcats. 1  One and a half to two times larger than a typical house cat. 2  Average body length including tale is 36. 3  Average height of adult bobcat is 14-15 from ground to shoulder.  Males range from 16 to 30 lbs, females average 20 1  lbs. Tufted ears and a tufted face both marked with bold bands.
