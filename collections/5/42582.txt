Leedey, OK. Leedey is a town in Dewey County, Oklahoma, United States. The population was 435 at the 2010 census. Leedey is located at 35°52′7″N 99°20′39″W / 35.86861°N 99.34417°W / 35.86861; -99.34417 (35.868518, -99.344124). According to the United States Census Bureau, the town has a total area of 0.4 square miles (1.0 km²), all of it land.
