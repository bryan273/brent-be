The filter Indicator will be Red when you have used your water filter for 6 months. If you change the filter, press the Ice Type / Filter Change button for 3 seconds to reset the filter schedule.When you do not want to use the water filter, press the Ice Type button for more than 5 seconds, the Filter Indicator and the Filter Change display will be switched off.o give you time to get a new filter the red light comes on just before the capacity of the current filter runs out. Follow these steps to change the water filter. 1. Shut off the water supply. Then turn the water filter (1) about 1/4 turn anti-clockwise. 2. Pull the water filter (1) from the case filter (2).
