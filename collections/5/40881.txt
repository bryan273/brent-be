Once it is in the uterus implantation occurs – typically around 7-10 days after ovulation. HCG, the “pregnancy hormone”, is present in your blood right after conception, but it won’t be detectable on a pregnancy test until after the egg attaches to the uterus during implantation. At the earliest, a pregnancy test will not show up positive until around 9 or 10 days past ovulation.
