Many people complain to their eye doctor of pain behind the eyes. Pain behind the eye can feel somewhat like a deep headache. Pain, headache or achiness that occurs behind the eye is a common complaint and can be concerning. However, a proper diagnosis is not always easy. Your eye doctor will need to evaluate your eye health to determine a possible source of pain. Different Types of Eye Pain. Pain behind your eye can feel like a dull ache or sharp, intense pain.
