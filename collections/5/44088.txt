1st publication. Chicago is a poem by Carl Sandburg, about the U.S. city of Chicago. It first appeared in Poetry, March 1914, the first of nine poems collectively titled Chicago Poems. It was republished in 1916 in Sandburg's first mainstream collection of poems, also titled Chicago Poems. Sandburg moved to Chicago in 1912 after living in Milwaukee, where he had served as secretary to Emil Seidel, Milwaukee's Socialist mayor.
