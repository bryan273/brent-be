Evergreen Rosemary Propagation. Rosemary plants are usually propagated by cuttings, as it can be tricky getting evergreen rosemary seeds to germinate. Successfully growing rosemary plants from seeds comes only when the seeds are very fresh and when planted in optimum growing conditions.Start new rosemary plants with cuttings from existing evergreen plants.
