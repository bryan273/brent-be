The demographic breakdown of cell and smartphone owners (January 2014): As of May 2013, 63% of adult cell owners use their phones to go online. 34% of cell internet users go online mostly using their phones, and not using some other device such as a desktop or laptop computer.4% of adult smartphone owners ages 18 and older say they use their phone to get directions or other information based on their current location. Among adult social media users ages 18 and older, 30% say that at least one of their accounts is currently set up to include their location in their posts.
