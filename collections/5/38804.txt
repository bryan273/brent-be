Granite & Pegmatite. Both granite and pegmatite are made from the same minerals (pink zone in chart). These are silicates including potassium and plagioclase feldspars, quartz, biotite and amphibole.Both are intrusive igneous rocks meaning that the magma from which they formed never reached the earth's surface.ranite & Pegmatite.
