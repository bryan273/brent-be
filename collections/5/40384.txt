School Profile for Maiden Middle School. Maiden Middle School is a middle school located in Maiden, North Carolina and is one of 7 middle schools in the Catawba County Schools. There are 500 students from 7th grade to 8th grade and a student/teacher ratio of 13:1. Maiden Middle School currently has a rating of 7.2 out of 10.
