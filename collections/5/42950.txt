The Great American Eclipse is coming to the USA on August 21, 2017. Learn where to view the eclipse, how to view it safely, and all about solar eclipses. On August 21, 2017, millions of people across the United States will see nature's most wondrous spectacle — a total eclipse of the Sun. It is a scene of unimaginable beauty; the Moon completely blocks the Sun, daytime becomes a deep twilight, and the Sun’s corona shimmers in the darkened sky.
