Blocks in the leg or knee: • Femoral nerve block, for surgery on the knee and. the front of the thigh. • Sciatic nerve block, for surgery on the knee, ankle, or foot. • Popliteal Fossa nerve block, for surgery on the lower. leg or foot. Blocks given in the upper arm or shoulder: • Interscalene block, for surgery on the shoulder, arm, or elbow. • Brachial Plexus nerve block, for surgery on the arm, elbow, or hand. Talking with your doctor. Before your surgery, your doctor will meet with you to. describe the peripheral nerve block.
