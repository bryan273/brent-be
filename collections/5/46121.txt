Lucy /lucy/ [2 sylls.] as a girls' name is pronounced LOO-see. It is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Lucy is light. Feminine of Lucius; vernacular form of Lucia.The feast day of Saint Lucy (fourth century), patroness of sight, is called the Festival of Light in Sweden.Lucy is also a bossy little girl in Charles Schulz's popular Peanuts comic strip.
