The growth rate of the Japanese Blueberry tree is slow to moderate. The trees take up to 2 years to begin growing when planted from seed. Japanese Blueberries make excellent screening trees, but considering the growth rate, look for a more mature Japanese Blueberry at a nursery or garden center.During the first two years of growth, the tree reaches 10 to 12 feet in height.he trees take up to 2 years to begin growing when planted from seed.
