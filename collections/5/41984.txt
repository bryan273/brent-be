Preheat oven to 475 degrees. Melt 2 Tbsp. of butter, and brush the inside of about 22 gold bar or brioche molds. (The recipe also fits in a combination of 13 financier molds and 5 brioche molds .) Molds can be found here. Set buttered tins on a cookie sheet and place in freezer to harden.Melt and cool 3/4 cup salted butter. and 1/3 cup confectioners (powdered) sugar. 1/2 cup whole wheat pastry flour OR unbleached all-purpose flour. Separate 5-6 eggs to make 3/4 to 7/8 cup of egg whites. Mix egg whites lightly.
