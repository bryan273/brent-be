Inside the Basket: Cheri White, Balloon Fiesta Pilot. Every year during the Balloon Fiesta hundreds of hot air balloons take flight. Controlling those colorful giants are the dedicated men and women who make each Balloon Fiesta as memorable as the last. Cheri White, a commercial hot air balloon pilot with over 2,300 pilot hours, is one of many pilots participating in the 2016 Balloon Fiesta. Flying since the age of 14, Cheri has flown around the county, over 40 USA states and the world. With multiple medals and honors under her belt, Cheri is currently the Southwest Region Director and Vice President of the Balloon Federation of America.
