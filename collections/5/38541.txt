About Lodi Wine Lodi is known as the Zinfandel Capital of the World because our local vines produce about 40 percent of the premium Zinfandel in California. With old growth vines dating back to the late 1800s, and wine-making traditions dating back generations, today Lodi is home to more than 80 wineries.
