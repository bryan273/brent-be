The first recorded spelling of the family name is shown to be that of Symon Janse Van Arsdale, which was dated June 1st 1660, a baptism witness at Flatlands, Kings County, New York, during the reign of King Charles 11 of England, known as 'The merry monarch', 1660 - 1685.ater recordings include George Van Arsdall, who married Zervia Andrews, at Pine Bush, Orange County, New York, on February 13th 1849. He may have been the son of Henry Vanarsdaal, of Montgomery, Orange County, New York state, who was recorded there on December 29th 1812.
