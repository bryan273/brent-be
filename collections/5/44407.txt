This feature is new to Unity 5, and allows you to make games that run in a browser using HTML 5, without the player even needing a plugin. Here's how to convert your Unity project to a web build. 1  Open your project, or download ours. 2  Click to bring up the platform build settings. 3  Add the Game scene by either clicking Add Current, or dragging it from the Project tab into the Scenes In Build panel. 4  Under Platform chose WebGL, and click Switch Platform.
