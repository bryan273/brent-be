The number of days of stay in Cambodian on a tourist visa vary according to the country that issued the passport being held by a foreign national. The shortest number of days is 14 and the longest is 30 days. Click here to see chart listing number of days allowed to stay in Cambodia on a tourist visa. You will need to exit Cambodia before the maximum number of days allowed is consumed, otherwise you will be charged a fine of US$5.00 per day after the last day of the allowable number of days.
