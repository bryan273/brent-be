There are different breeds of Bulldogs! English Bulldog lives to be about 8 years. French Bulldog lives to be about 10-12 years. American Bulldog lives to be about 15-17 years. Olde English Bulldogge lives to be about 12 years old. Alpha Blue Blood Bulldog lives to be about 12-16 years. Banter Bulldogge lives to about 12-13 years.
