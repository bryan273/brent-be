Cooling down after exercise restores your breathing and heart rate back to normal. Photo Credit Polka Dot Images/Polka Dot/Getty Images. Most people are familiar with how their bodies feel during exercise; the heart beats faster and it becomes harder to breathe.our heart rate will still increase while exercising, but not as sharply because the heart is strengthened; your breathing will intensify, but you may be less winded. Once your exercise routine is complete, breathing and heart rate will return to normal more quickly.
