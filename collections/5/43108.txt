INCREASING FINANCIAL SUPPORT FOR OUR NATION'S CHILDREN. The Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity Reconciliation Act of 1996 (PRWORA) ALL EMPLOYERS TO REPORT ALL NEW HIRES. The Personal Responsibility and Work Opportunity Reconciliation Act (PRWORA) of 1996 was signed into law on August 22, 1996. This legislation provides for a much strengthened Child Support Enforcement Program.
