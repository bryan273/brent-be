Exhibit 2 shows the demographic characteristics of the uninsured currently and post-ACA under our best and worst case scenarios. Overall, the ACA will minimally alter the demographic composition of the uninsured, regardless of whether undecided states opt-in or out. While Blacks and Hispanics will continue to be overrepresented among the uninsured, the majority will be non-Hispanic, white, low-income, working-age adults, many of them employed.
