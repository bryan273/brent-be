Generally 1, but 2 days at the most. (In most cases.) 1  Using a gas torch burn the hairs from the floor prior to coating.  Coat the floor with first coat of Performance Epoxy, wait 24 hours and sand vigorously with an industrial sander (80 grit), apply a second coat and flake the floor. Note: If you dont flake the floor you may still see traces of hairs so its important to apply at least the minimum of flakes provided in a normal kit.
