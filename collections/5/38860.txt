Detailed Medicare cost information for 2017. Monthly premium: Most people don't pay a monthly premium for Part A (sometimes called premium-free Part A). If you buy Part A, you'll pay up to $413 each month in 2017. If you paid Medicare taxes for less than 30 quarters, the standard Part A premium is $413. If you paid Medicare taxes for 30-39 quarters, the standard Part A premium is $227. Learn more about Part A costs.
