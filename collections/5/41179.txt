7. No visit to the Isle of Man is complete without a trip to Great Laxey Wheel, named ‘Lady Isabella’ after Lieutenant Governor Hope’s wife. Built in 1854, the world’s largest working water wheel has an impressive beauty that belies its industrial heritage. It has a diameter of 72ft and a circumference of 227ft.. Spot the Isle of Man icons. There are two very famous symbols of the Isle of Man – the Manx Cat and the Legs of Man. The cat is ‘a breed of domestic cat with a naturally occurring mutation that shortens the tail.
