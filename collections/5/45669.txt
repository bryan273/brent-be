Nov. 1697 /1698 at Flatbush, Kings County, New York. He was the son of Cornelius Symonse Van Arsdalen and Marike Dirckse Ammerman. On the paternal side Simon was the grandson of Sijmon Janzen and the great grandson of Jan Paulwelsen Van Aersdale. should have said, there is rotation in the office of both Elder and Deacon in the Reformed (Dutch) Church. The following names I findied David Cossart, Garret Van Arsdalen, John Van Dyck, Henry Commingore, Isaac Van Arsdalen, Luke Brinkerhoff, John Cownover, Thomas Johnson and Ralph Brinkerhoff.
