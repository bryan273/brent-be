Directions. 1  Heat oven to 350ºF. Melt butter in a small skillet over medium-high heat. 2  Add ground turkey, breadcrumbs, egg, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and salt, if desired to turkey mixture; mix well. 3  Bake 1 hour or until the internal temperature of meatloaf is well-done, 165ºF as measured by a meat thermometer.
