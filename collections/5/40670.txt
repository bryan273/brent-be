2015 Federal Income Tax Rates - Tax Year 2014. Updated with tax rates for tax year 2014 (due April 2015) Compare the tax year 2014 tax brackets (above) with the federal brackets for tax year 2013 (below): The Federal Income Tax is a marginal income tax collected by the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) on most types of personal and business income. The federal income tax consists of six marginal tax brackets, ranging from a minimum of 10% to a maximum of 39.6%. Your income tax must be paid throughout the year through tax withholding or quarterly payments, and reconciled yearly by filing a Federal Income Tax Return by April 15th.
