Virginia LPN Requirements. RNs in Virginia are required to complete 30 CE hours or 15 CE hours and 640 active practice hours every two years. LPNs in Virginia are required to complete 30 CE hours or 15 CE hours and 640 active practice hours every two years. For more Information about Virginia's CE Requirements for RNs and LPNs and the various hour requirements, please visit the Virginia Board of Nursing website.
