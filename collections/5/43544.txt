Academic Supervisors. You will normally have two academic supervisors during your studies. Your supervisors will have considerable research experience and knowledge of your discipline. At least one, the primary supervisor, will have experience supervising PhD students to completion.our supervisors will provide guidance on a range of academic matters. These include: 1  the standards required for a PhD. 2  planning your research. 3  skills you will need to acquire. 4  research resources. 5  methodology. 6  undertaking a literature review. 7  ethical, legal, professional and safety issues.
