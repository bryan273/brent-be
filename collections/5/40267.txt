14-Year-Old Jerika Bolen, Who Suffers From Rare Disease, Has Chosen To End Her Life After One Final Summer. Jerika Bolen, 14, is enjoying her final summer, fulfilling lifelong wishes after telling her mother Jen to unplug her life-sustaining ventilator at the end of August. Bolen, who spends her days in unbearable pain, has opted to forgo yet another surgery for Spinal Muscular Atrophy Type 2 disease.
