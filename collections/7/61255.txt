A commercial grade of fuming nitric acid contains 90% HNO 3 and has a density of 1.50 g/mL. This grade is much used in the explosives industry. It is not as volatile nor as corrosive as the anhydrous acid and has the approximate concentration of 21.4 molar.ed fuming nitric acid, or RFNA, contains substantial quantities of dissolved nitrogen dioxide (NO 2) leaving the solution with a reddish-brown color. Due to the dissolved nitrogen dioxide, the density of red fuming nitric acid is lower at 1.490 g/mL.
