Difference Between Flu and H1N1. The word flu is the shortened form of influenza. Influenza virus causes the flu. There are three main types of virus; Influenza virus A (can infects human and birds), Influenza virus B (infects only human) and Influenza virus C (can infect Human, dogs and pigs). These viruses are called RNA viruses, that means they have the genetic matter in the RNA.
