Here are a few words and saying in Cajun you may hear when visiting Louisiana. 1  bouder [bou-deh] verb: To pout or sulk. 2  envie [ahn-vee] noun: A longing or hunger to do or eat something.  gris gris [gree-gree] noun: To put a curse on someone. Frequently used in jest, not in reference to actual black magic.
