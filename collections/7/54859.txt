The Hemingray 42, a telegraph insulator produced by the Hemingray Glass Company, is widely found in North America. The Hemingray Glass Company was an American glass manufacturing company. The company was founded by Robert Hemingray and Ralph Gray in 1848.n 1933, the company was sold to the Owens-Illinois Glass Company but production remained in Muncie under the Hemingray name. The main plant in Muncie shut down in 1966 and insulator production ceased.
