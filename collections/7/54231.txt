Dimensions: 45.5 cm × 41 cm (17 7⁄8 in × 16 1⁄8 in) Location: Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam, Netherlands The Milkmaid (Dutch: De Melkmeid or Het Melkmeisje), sometimes called The Kitchen Maid, is an oil-on-canvas painting of a milkmaid, in fact a domestic kitchen maid, by the Dutch artist Johannes Vermeer.
