The TLP can be used in all forms of communication, whether written or oral. This Glossary Entry presents the TLP and its possible variants, and proposes some considerations on its use and its limitations. The Traffic Light Protocol (TLP) The TLP is in principle easy to use: the sharer of information tags the information with a colour. Tagging information consists simply of adding “TLP:COLOUR” on a document or part of it. The meaning of the colour indicates the possibilities for further spreading of the information.
