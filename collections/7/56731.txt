Lifeline Nets - Product Overview. Our lifeline netting is white, weather resistant, high strength nylon. Lifeline netting comes in 2 X 2 knotted diamond mesh with double strands top and bottom for durability. It is easy to install and fits boat rail from 18 to 36 high. When new, our lifeline netting has a tensile strength of 340 pounds per strand! That's plenty of holding power to keep gear on board! Our lifeline netting is sold by the foot with all of the meshes (diamonds) collapsed, stretched tight and unopened.
