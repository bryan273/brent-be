Registered Nurse Salary in Pennsylvania. In May 2013, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, Pennsylvania was the fifth state with the highest employment level, i.e. 124,750, for the RNs which represented 22.210 per 1000 jobs.he average RN salary in PA was $64,000 as stated by indeed.com. The salary figures were 4% less than the mean salaries of these workers for job postings at national level.
