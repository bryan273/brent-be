1. Belowground portions of the potato plant are often infected by the potato wart pathogen. Following infection by the potato wart pathogen, meristematic tissues are transformed into large warty galls on the lower stems and tubers. The diagnostic symptoms of potato wart are galls produced on several plant parts.
