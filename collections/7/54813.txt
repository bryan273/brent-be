Late May and early June are alive with colour and temperatures usually range from 8 to 22 degrees C (46 to 71 degrees F). Summer is hot, but rarely humid. We wear short sleeves. Daytime temperatures are usually in the 20s (70s) and can go as high as 34 degrees C (93 degrees F). Autumn is clear and bright. September afternoons can be quite warm, evenings cool.
