A vertical town where instead of roads visitors explore along steep flights of steps. How to get to Positano If you're driving from the west, eastbound, Positano is the first town you'll come across on the Amalfi Coast. The town is approximately a 75 minute drive away from Naples and twenty minutes away from Sorrento.You can also get to Positano by one of the buses run by the Sita coach company and, in the summer months, by boat.
