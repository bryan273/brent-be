Immunoglobulin Therapy & Other Medical Therapies for Antibody Deficiencies. There are several specific medical therapies available for patients with primary immunodeficiency diseases involving the humoral immune system. These illnesses include X-Linked Agammaglobulinemia (XLA) and Common Variable Immune Deficiency (CVID), among others, and are characterized by a lack of and/or impaired antibody function.
