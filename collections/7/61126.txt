Pectoral (Shoulder) Girdle. The pectoral (shoulder) girdle attaches the bones of the. upper limbs to the axial skeleton. •Consists of scapula and clavicle. •Clavicle articulates with sternum (sternoclavicular joint) •Clavicle articulates with scapula (acromioclavicular joint) •Upper limb attached to pectoral girdle at shoulder. (glenohumeral joint)
