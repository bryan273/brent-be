The Sabin Lock is the fourth lock in a four parallel lock complex. The north canal services the Sabin and Davis Locks and has a minimum maintained depth of 24.0 feet upstream and 23.5 feet downstream of the locks. The normal upper pool for the Sabin Lock is elevation 601.3 ft (IGLD 85) and the normal lower pool is elevation 580.8 ft (IGLD 85).
