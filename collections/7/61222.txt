Worthy Is the Lamb - SATB Anthem (Min. Rhodes, Gary (Arranged by) , Zschech, Darlene (Composer) , Cranfill, Jeff (Orchestrated by) We believe you will find this to be the definitive arrangement of this classic praise and worship song by Darlene Zschech. The arrangement is true to the feel of Zschech's original recording, yet with rhythms that are enjoyable and workable for choirs.
