The prescription medicine Lyrica is a brand of the generic drug pregabalin and is used totreat seizures, relieve nerve pain and prevent anxiety.This drug is manufactured by Pfizer and there are currently no generic forms of it available.It was the first FDA-approved medication for fibromyalgia patients.yrica is normally prescribed to patients as an additional treatment when other medications have been unsuccessful in controlling their seizures. Because the Pregabalin is able to stabilize electrical nerve activity, it is also used to treat neuropathic pain in patients who have suffered spinal cord injuries.
