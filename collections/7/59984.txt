Experience beautiful beaches, luxury amenities and an easygoing lifestyle in Jupiter. Take the family on a turtle hatchling walk, hike or bike the many park and beach trails, paddle a kayak along the Loxahatchee River or climb the Jupiter Lighthouse for the view. Jupiter's Roger Dean Stadium is Spring Training home to both the Florida Marlins and the St. Louis Cardinals. Nearby Abacoa Town Center features specialty shops, sidewalk dining, weekly events and live concerts. And don't forget Jupiter's beaches, which have served as retreats for surfers and beachgoers for decades.
