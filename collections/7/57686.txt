The most common overcharging error we see is matching a battery to a charger that is not designed for use with that battery’s capacity even if the voltage is the same. For instance, our 12 volt 3 Amp charger should not, in general, be used on 12 volt batteries that have a capacity below 10Ah.he most common overcharging error we see is matching a battery to a charger that is not designed for use with that battery’s capacity even if the voltage is the same.
