Steelers announce minicamp, OTAs schedule for 2016. The Pittsburgh Steelers released their offseason schedule, including dates for rookie minicamp, OTAs and the full-team minicamp. The NFL Draft is scheduled for April 28-30 in Chicago. The rookies will have their first official work with the Steelers the following weekend, which typically consists of non-contact drills to give the rookies a chance to see the kinds of things coaches will look at during the full-team minicamp in June.
