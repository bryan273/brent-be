Vampires Dark Rising is a free to play, fresh and exciting take on text-based MMORPG gaming. VDR introduces the story, characters and universe of a newly rising, vampire inhabited Darkside world. Using the native functions of Android phones, VDR players will find gameplay aspects and interaction features never-before-seen in this genre that push the game to a whole new level! WELCOME TO THE RISING. Long ago a Great Purge befell our vampire kind.
