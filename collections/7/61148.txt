What is a field approval? A field approval is a maintenance performance approval for a major repair or major alteration that is performed by a Flight Standards Service, Aviation Safety Inspector. The process is used for one serial numbered aircraft in accordance with Order 8300.10, Vol. 2, Chapter 1. The field approval process has been in use for over 40 years and represents a collaboration between the Flight Standards Service, Aircraft Certification Service, designees, and applicants.
