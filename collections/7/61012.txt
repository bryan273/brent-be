Here are a few words and saying in Cajun you may hear when visiting Louisiana. 1  bouder [bou-deh] verb: To pout or sulk. 2  envie [ahn-vee] noun: A longing or hunger to do or eat something. 3  gris gris [gree-gree] noun: To put a curse on someone.  fais do do [faydoe doe] expression: A Cajun dance party.
