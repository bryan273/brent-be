Dental Implants Supported Dentures. If you are missing all of your teeth either on the upper or on the lower jaw you may be a great candidate for what is called dental implant dentures. There are two major reasons to consider dental implant dentures. First, dental implants used in this type of dentures provide tremendous support and fixation of the teeth. This denture fixation method guarantees that your dentures will never slip and thus prevent embarrassing situations.
