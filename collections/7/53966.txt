Fritillaria (Fritillaria) is a perennial growing in U.S. Department of Agriculture plant hardiness zones 5 to 9, producing bell-shape blooms that dangle downward from the tall flower stalk. This attractive plant will keep cuddly kittens away from your garden. Scaredy cat plant (Coleus canina) grows to about 1 to 2 feet tall as an annual bedding plant. It has deep green foliage and delicate blue blooms. The smell of the scaredy cat plant keeps dogs and cats away from the area.
