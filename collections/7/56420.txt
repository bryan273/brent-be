Men are often riveted by the beauty of a Sabra. Intelligent and well read Sabra's are not often swayed by the ways of the world. Most Sabra's are artistic and can dance. ie: Sabra Johnson of So you think you can dance  Witty and sometimes sarcastic Sabra's see humor in the ordinary. Mesmerizing and fulll of joy , they are often seen as the life of the party.
