The furnace itself usually costs $1,000 to $1,500, while labor adds an additional $1,000 to $2,000. Very high-efficiency models can cost $5,000 or more to buy and install. Removal and haul away of the old furnace usually costs $300 to $1,000, depending on size and difficulty.As with boilers, a new chimney liner for a high efficiency unit will cost anywhere from $500 to $1,500, with liners for gas units priced on the lower end of that range and liners for oil units on the higher end.id-efficiency boilers cost anywhere from $2,500 to $4,000, while high-efficiency boilers run $5,000 to $10,000. Those prices include installation, but keep in mind that installing a boiler is more expensive than installing a furnace because the process is more complex.
