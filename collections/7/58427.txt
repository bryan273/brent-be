Income by State. Respiratory therapists practicing in California reported higher incomes than those in any other state as of May 2011, earning an average of $69,930 per year. Nevada therapists earned $67,270 per year on average, and those in Hawaii reported average incomes of $66,890 per year. New Jersey and Connecticut were also among the top-paying states. Respiratory therapists working in Kentucky reported the lowest average wages, $44,790 per year.
