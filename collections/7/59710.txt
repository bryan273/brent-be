Back to Top Why the Test Is Performed. This test is done to see if you have high levels of uric acid in your blood. High levels of uric acid can cause gout or kidney disease. Your doctor may also order this test if you have had or are about to have certain types of chemotherapy.f your body produces too much uric acid or doesn't remove enough if it, you can get sick. A high level of uric acid in the blood is called hyperuricemia. This test checks to see how much uric acid you have in your blood. Another test can be used to check the level of uric acid in your urine.
