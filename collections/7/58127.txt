Professional boundaries in social work and other helping professions are limits in the therapeutic relationship that the provider is responsible for establishing and maintaining. Social workers must understand what professional boundaries are and identify poor ones. You are making the session about you if you discuss parts of your personal life that are not relevant to the client. 2  When a client crosses the professional boundaries, redirect them by immediately clarifying your role and the limits of your relationship with the client. 3  Avoid having a dual relationship with a client.
