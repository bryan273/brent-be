Pregnancy: Dropping (Lightening) Pregnancy: Dropping (Lightening) Pregnancy: Dropping (Lightening) At the end of the third trimester, the baby settles, or drops lower, into the mother's pelvis. This is known as dropping or lightening. Dropping is not a good predictor of when labor will begin. In first-time mothers, dropping usually occurs 2 to 4 weeks before delivery, but it can happen earlier.
