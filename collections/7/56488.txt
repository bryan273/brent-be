Kidney Failure patients are suggested to keep a healthy diet, however, can they eat garlic? Apart from medications and surgery, garlic may also help manage kidney diseases. Then how does garlic benefit Kidney Failure patients? Garlic can lower the reabsorption of salts by the kidneys and increase urine production. The biologically active components of garlic can inhibit the production of blood pressure-enhancing hormones by the kidneys. All these mechanisms may help Kidney Failure patients reduce high blood pressure.
