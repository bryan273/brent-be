(bɒbəl ) Word forms: plural , 3rd person singular present tense , plural bobbles , present participle bobbling, past tense , past participle bobbled. 1. countable noun. A bobble is a small ball of material, usually made of wool, which is used for decorating clothes. ...the bobble on his nightcap. If a ball bobbles, it moves in an irregular and uncontrolled way. The ball bobbled into the back of the net.
