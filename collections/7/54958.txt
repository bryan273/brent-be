Water the dwarf mondo grass thoroughly when the top 1 to 2 inches of soil becomes dry, usually every 10 to 14 days. Never allow the soil to become soggy, instead water to keep it evenly moist.Stop applying water during the winter when new growth has slowed or stopped.ow the dwarf mondo grass' foliage once per year, in the late winter or early spring just before the first flush of new growth. Adjust the blade height setting on the lawn mower to its highest setting. Rake up and discard the clippings.
