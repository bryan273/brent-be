The Nutrition of Lentils. Lentils are some of the most nutritious, and at the same time economical, foods in the world. These legumes that are grown like peas in a pod, and come in a wide array of colors including brown, pink, green and orange. They are widely popular in Eastern Europe, India and in some parts of Asia.his is because the high amount of soluble fiber in the lentils help trap carbohydrates, which can slow digestion and absorption and help reduce the risks of spikes in the blood sugar levels. Boosts Digestion. With the high levels of fiber in lentils, they can expedite the detoxification of the body.
