The Oregon Clinic - Surgical and Burn Specialists offers many services, including general surgery as well as burn and complex wound management treatment. General surgery focuses on abdominal organs, e.g., intestines including esophagus, stomach, small bowel, colon, liver, pancreas, gallbladder and bile ducts, and often the thyroid gland. We also specialize in burn treatment; wound care, skin grafting, in addition to general surgery services.
