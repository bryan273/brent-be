Generation No. 1. 1. William Austin Greer, born 15 Sep 1920 in Hart County, Kentucky. He was a bulldozer operator; died 23 Dec 1991 in LaRue County, Kentucky. He is buried in Red Hill Cemetery in Hodgenville. He was the son of 2. William Taylor Greer and 3. Eula (Beulah) Frances Rock. He married (1) Patricia LaVerne Laha 04 Sep 1947 in Kentucky.
