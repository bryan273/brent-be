Soil pH decreases as the acidity increases because the pH measurement expresses acidity as. the negative logarithm of H+ ion concentration. Alfalfa is the most sensitive crop to low pH grown in Iowa, while. forage grasses are the least sensitive and corn and soybean are intermediate. Soil acidity can affect plant growth directly. or indirectly by affecting the plant-availability of several nutrients, increasing levels of some elements to phytotoxic.
