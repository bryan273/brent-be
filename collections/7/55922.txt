2015 Pittsburgh Steelers OTAs: Day 1 Recap. The Pittsburgh Steelers' first day of OTAs in the books. Time to take a look back at what happened at the team's South Side facility Tuesday. When NFL fans have been deprived of any football substance since the 2015 NFL Draft, they are starving for any bits of information they may receive from their favorite teams. Pittsburgh Steelers fans were one of 26 NFL fan bases who got just that Tuesday as those teams began their Organized Team Activities (OTAs).
