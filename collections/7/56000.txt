Best Answer: The song was originally recorded by Kasey Cisyk (as the title song for a movie of the same name) ... then re-recorded as a record single by Debbie Boone. You Light Up My Life is a ballad written by Joe Brooks and originally recorded by Kasey Cisyk for the soundtrack to the film of the same name. The song was performed in the film by its lead, Didi Conn, who lip-synched Cisyk's version.
