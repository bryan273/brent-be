Ginger tea is often recommended for adults to treat nausea, indigestion or stomach upset, but it is rarely used for infants and young children. Mild herbal teas are a way to treat minor discomfort and illness in children, according to Dr. Lisa Watson, ND, and are very safe when used as directed.
