Theatre Research 1 Assignment One Caroline Ranson Consider how 'Hamlet' conforms to Aristotle's definition of tragedy according to his Poetics, and how 'Hamletmachine' resists this conformity. Aristotle's Poetics is considered a guide to a well-written tragedy. In Aristotle's opinion, plot is the most important aspect of the tragedy, all other parts such as character, diction, and thought stem from the plot.
