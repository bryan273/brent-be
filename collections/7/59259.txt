Military retirees and / or their spouses must be enrolled in Medicare Part A, which is Medicare’s hospital insurance, and Medicare Part B, which is Medicare’s medical insurance, to be eligible for TRICARE for Life. Benefits. The types of care covered by TRICARE for Life are discussed in the Overview section above. In addition to care, TFL provides for durable medical equipment (DME) provided the items are 1) medically necessary and 2) prescribed by a physician. One can find greater details about the program’s DME policy here.
