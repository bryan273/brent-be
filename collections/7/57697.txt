Often referred to as cardio, endurance or aerobic exercise, cardiorespiratory activity relies on your body's ability to provide your muscles with enough oxygen to carry out sustained exercise. In addition to helping manage your weight, engaging in cardio offers major benefits.hoto Credit kzenon/iStock/Getty Images. Cardiorespiratory fitness -- together with muscular strength, muscle endurance, flexibility and body composition -- make up the five essential components of physical fitness.
