Otsego Lake forms most of the eastern boundary, and Canadarago Lake forms part of the western boundary. New York State Route 80 is a north-south highway along the west side of Otsego Lake. At Cooperstown, NY-80 is partly conjoined with New York State Route 28 as an east-west highway. New York State Route 205 intersects NY-80 in the western part of Otsego.
