Recommended daily intake of vitamin B 1. Vitamin B 1 is found in high concentrations in the outer layers and germ of cereals, as well as in yeast, beef, pork, nuts, whole grains, and pulses. Thiamine is also present in cauliflower, liver, oranges, eggs, potatoes, asparagus and kale. Most nations set the recommended daily intake of vitamin B 1 at 1.1 milligrams for females and 1.5 milligrams for males.
