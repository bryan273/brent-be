Whatever plane the big guy is on is Air Force One, although typically people think of the big 747 when they hear this. Whatever plane the #2 guy is on (Big Joe in this case) is Air Force Two. Whatever plane the First Lady is on is Executive Foxtrot One. They have a bunch of different jets they fly, including the big 747, a few 757's, some BBJ's (737's), and a bunch of Gulfstreams (3's, 5's, and 550's mainly).
