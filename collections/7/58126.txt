Love can’t exist without boundaries, even with your children. It’s easy to understand external boundaries as your bottom line. Think of rules and principles you live by when you say what you will or won’t do or allow. If you become highly emotional, argumentative, or defensive, you may have weak emotional boundaries. 2  Emotional boundaries distinguish separating your emotions and responsibility for them from someone else’s. 3  It’s like an imaginary line or force field that separates you and others.
