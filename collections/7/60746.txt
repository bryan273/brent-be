The Eumycota consist of eukaryotic, nonchlorophyllous heterotrophs that absorb nutrients from dead or living organic matter, have cell walls composed of chitin, and store excess energy as glycogen. The kingdom contains four phyla: Chytridiomycota, Zygomycota, Ascomycota, and Basidiomycota.
