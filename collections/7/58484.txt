Now, when it comes to acai bowls, there are a few key things to know. The acai puree packets that are used to make the bowls aren’t really an issue—a 100 gram packet of Sambazon puree contains just 70 calories, 5 grams of fat, and no sugar. But the things that are added to make a bowl (often almond milk, berries, and bananas) add up calorie-wise, mostly from sugar, Cording says.
