The ORIGINAL Sunshine Skyway Bridge was built in 1954. Destroyed in 1980 by the MV Summit Venture (accident) USCGC Blackthorn. The original bridge had 2 lanes Northbound and 2 lanes Southbound. The Southbound section of the bridge was destroyed and the Northbound lanes were used until the new Skyway Bridge was completed. Rebuilt 1987 – opened Feb 7, 1987 Named the Bob Graham Sunshine Skyway Bridge in 1994 Bob Graham was the Governor of Florida in 1994 The Sunshine Skyway Bridge took 5 years to complete (1982-1987) This is Toll Bridge (2009 – $1 for cars)(2011 the toll went up to $1.25)
