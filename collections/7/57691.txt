Aerobic exercise is exercise with oxygen. In other words exercises that get you breathing hard. Cardio exercise is short for cardiovascular. Cardio exercise is any exercise that stresses your cardiovascular system, which are the exercises that get you breathing more rapidly.All cardio exercise is aerobic exercise. However, not all aerobic exercises will give cardiovascular benefits.For example, walking is aerobic. However, walking at a pace of 2 km/h is not going give you cardiovascular benefit.
