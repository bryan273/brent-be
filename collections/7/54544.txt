It is West Indies Mahogany-Swietenia Mahogani. Cuba is the central part of the West Indies and so it is called Cuban Mahogany, but it does not mean it is imported from Cuba. In fact, it is illegal to import this species from Cuba, Dominica, or Haiti because it is a threatened species.The northernmost end of its range is here in Key Largo.uba is the central part of the West Indies and so it is called Cuban Mahogany, but it does not mean it is imported from Cuba.
