Jimmy Ruffin, whose poignant What Becomes of the Brokenhearted is regarded by many as one of Motown's greatest works, died Monday afternoon in a Las Vegas hospital. He was 78. Ruffin had been hospitalized in September, where he developed pneumonia and further complications.
