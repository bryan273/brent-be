Some scientists assert that the aurora ring is most active during the equinoxes (September 20 and March 20, approximately). Because this phenomenon can be very hard to predict, use NOAA's Aurora 30-minute Forecast tool to deduce the highest likelihood of seeing the Northern Lights and help you plan your trip.
