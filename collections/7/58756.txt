A promissory note is simply a contractual agreement by which one party (the borrower, or payor) makes an unconditional promise in writing to pay a sum of money to the other (the lender, or payee), either at a fixed or determinable future time or on demand of the payee, under specific terms. promissory note is simply a contractual agreement by which one party (the borrower, or payor) makes an unconditional promise in writing to pay a sum of money to the other (the lender, or payee), either at a fixed or determinable future time or on demand of the payee, under specific terms.
