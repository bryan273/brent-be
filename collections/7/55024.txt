In high school, Olivia dated Sonny Corinthos, but eventually lost him to his true love, Connie. After High School, Connie changed her identity and moved to Manhattan to make a new start. Olivia and the rest of the Falconeri family were hurt and angry. Years later, Sonny reached out to Olivia and invited her to his wedding to Connie, who was now going by the name Kate Howard. Olivia came to Port Charles and helped her cousin get ready for her wedding.
