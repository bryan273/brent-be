And just be careful: If you make your stew too thick, it will be too hard to skim. Cook ’til the cows come home. Yes, it is possible to overcook a beef stew. As much as we like the idea of a stew that sits on the stove all day long, too much time will result in dry beef and mushy veggies. It depends on how much stew you’re actually making, but the sweet spot is about 2–3 hours.
