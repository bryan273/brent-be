Adirondack Crabapple. Malus ‘Adirondack’. Exhibits a combination of many desirable traits that make it a near-perfect crabapple. Upright-branched growth habit combines with an annual bloom cycle, abundant, small, persistent fruit, slow to moderate growth rate, and multiple disease tolerance that is rare in crabapple. Zone 4 H(20′) W(15′) 2 – 2.5″ cal.
