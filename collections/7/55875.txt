Japanese Knotweed Uses. Japanese Knotweed can be used in two forms, capsule and tincture. It is a highly medicinal and edible plant that is mostly used for the treatment of Lyme disease. The plant has proven itself to be very effective in treating Lyme and its symptoms. Japanese Knotweed contains a compound called Japanese Knotweed resveratrol (an antioxidant, also found in grapes).
