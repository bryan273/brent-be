Livingstone, the first European to see the mighty cataract, would name it for his queen, Victoria. However, the name used by the African residents of the area, Mosi-oa-Tunya, which means the 'Smoke that Thunders,' seems a much more accurate description of this natural wonder. Formation of the Cataract. Victoria Falls is the result of soft sandstone that fills huge cracks in the hard basalt rock of the plateau. As the Upper Zambezi flowed across the plateau in ancient times, it found the cracks and started wearing away the softer rock, eventually creating a series of gorges.
