Guernica is a painting by famous Spanish artist Pablo Picasso. It was painted as a reaction to the aerial bombing of Guernica, Spain by German and Italian forces during the Spanish Civil War in 1937.The Spanish Republic, government of Spain, appointed Picasso to paint a large mural about the bombing to display at the 1937 World’s Fair in Paris.Guernica shows the cataclysms of war as well as the anguish and destruction it inflicts upon people, especially innocent civilians.he Painting – Summary Of Individual Components. The colors of Guernica are black, white, and grey.
