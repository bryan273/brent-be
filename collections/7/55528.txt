Identifying a Cold Sore. 1  1. Recognize an emerging cold sore. The cold sore will go through three phases as it erupts. 2  2. Take extra care of yourself if it is the first outbreak. The first outbreak is generally the worst. 3  3. Go to a doctor if it doesn’t heal. Cold sores will usually heal without medical attention, but if this doesn’t occur or you experience complications, you should get checked by a doctor. Go to a doctor if: Your immune system is suppressed.
