Neither of them achieved enduring happiness. Ultimately they were both victims of the same fate, enmeshed as young girls in a web of social ambition. Frances Shand Kydd was born Frances Ruth Burke Roche on January 20 1936 at Park House, Sandringham, the second daughter of the 4th Lord Fermoy and his wife Ruth. Later that same January 20, less than a mile away at Sandringham, King George V died.
