Choosing an option that guarantees a spouse pension benefits after your death means extra security but also lower monthly benefits. However, choosing a pension plan option that only pays through your lifetime can provide larger monthly payments. These require a lump sum to protect spouses if they outlive you. Use this pension calculator to help decide which pension option works best for particular retirement needs. Information and interactive calculators are made available to you as self-help tools for your independent use and are not intended to provide investment advice.
