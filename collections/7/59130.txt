The July weather guide for Ireland shown below is for Dublin. Weather in Ireland in July. The average maximum daytime temperature in Ireland in July is a comfortable 19°C (66°F). There are usually 5 hours of bright sunshine each day, which represents 32% of the 16 hours of daylight. The average sea temperature in and around Ireland in July is 14°C (57°F). Expect 51mm (2.0 inches) of rainfall across the whole of July with 14 days with some rain falling. Maximum UV levels will be high (6 UV index) at midday when the skies are clear. The average night-time temperature is usually a cool 12°C (54°F).
