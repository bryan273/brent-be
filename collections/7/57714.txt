Even though service tax was introduced on certain specified services as early as in 1994, the mechanism of service tax credits was made available to service providers for the first time in 2002 under the Service Tax Credit Rules, which were thereafter replaced in the year 2004 by the CENVAT Credit Rules.he reverse charge mechanism was first introduced under the Service Tax Rules in 2002 whereby it was provided that in case of provision of service by a non-resident not having an office in India, service tax would be payable by the service recipient.
