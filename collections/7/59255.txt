TRICARE will only cover a single dose of herpes Zostavax (shingles) vaccine for beneficiaries 60 years of age and older. TRICARE will not reimburse for vaccines purchased or administered at a pharmacy. Currently, Medicare only covers the Zostavax vaccine under Medicare Part D pharmacy program.
