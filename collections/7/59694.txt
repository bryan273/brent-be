Large Envelopes Large envelopes must be rectangular to qualify for flat pricing. Large envelopes that exceed size requirements of a letter will be charged package prices. Packages Packages can be sent using Priority Mail Express®, Priority Mail®, First-Class Mail® Parcels, USPS Retail Ground™, and Package Services. Contents must not weigh more than 70 lbs. Lower weight limits may apply. Girth is the distance around the thickest part of the package.
