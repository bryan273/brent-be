Respiratory Therapist Salary. (United States). Respiratory Therapists in the United States pull down an average of $24.37 per hour. Pay ranges from $18.67 per hour on the low end to $32.87 on the high end. This group's pay is mainly influenced by geographic location, followed by tenure and the particular employer. There are more female Respiratory Therapists who answered the questionnaire than male, making up 62 percent.
