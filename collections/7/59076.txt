Choosing Crawfish Colors for Bass. Dennis Tietje talks about why crawfish color is important on your plastics and why he got Strike King to laminate their Rage Craws and DB Craws to more closely mimic one characteristic of a crawfish. He also discusses how dominant crawfish colors can change as the water warms and cools. ...
