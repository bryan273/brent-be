Intersectionality: The belief that gender, race, class, and sexual orientation can’t be separated from each other. Neutrois: Think of it as je ne sais quoi: literally, “I don’t know what,” or “that certain something” which is as unnameable as you are. The cousin of agender or gender neutral. Nonbinary: Aka xenogender, describes any gender identity that doesn’t fit into the traditional male or female gender specification.
