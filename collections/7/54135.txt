Demographics of Republic of Moldova 2016. As of 1 January 2017, the population of Republic of Moldova was estimated to be 4 064 353 people. This is a decrease of -0.08 % (-3 132 people) compared to population of 4 067 485 the year before. In 2016 the natural increase was negative, as the number of deaths exceeded the number of live births by 1 220.
