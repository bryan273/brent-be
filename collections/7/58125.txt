Types of Boundaries. There are several areas where boundaries apply: 1  Material boundaries determine whether you give or lend things, such as your money, car, clothes, books, food, or toothbrush. 2  Physical boundaries pertain to your personal space, privacy, and body. If you become highly emotional, argumentative, or defensive, you may have weak emotional boundaries. 2  Emotional boundaries distinguish separating your emotions and responsibility for them from someone else’s. 3  It’s like an imaginary line or force field that separates you and others.
