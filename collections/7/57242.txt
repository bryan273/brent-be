‘The Flash’ Season 2: Jay Garrick & Patty Spivot Making Series Debut – Comic Con. CW’s The Flash is adding two notable characters from the DC Universe in its second season. Actor Teddy Sears, who was most recently seen on Masters of Sex and Raising the Bar will play Jay Garrick, the guy who was the first DC Comics character to originally call himself The Flash. Garrick is an enigmatic individual who arrives in Central City to warn Barry Allen and his team at S.T.A.R.
