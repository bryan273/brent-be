Otolaryngology vs Otorhinolaryngology. Otolaryngology is the integrated study of ear, nose and throat (ENT). The word Oto refers to ear and originates from the word ‘laryngo’ refers to larynx which is an organ situated in between the nose and the throat. Larynx is the voice box and is made up of vocal cords. Otorhinolaryngology is the same as otolaryngology.
