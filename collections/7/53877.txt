Delta Airlines' (DL) standard checked baggage / hold luggage policy details follow: 1  2 bags standard, up to 10 bags maximum (up to 4 bags on Delta Connection) 2  Maximum dimensions: 62 inches or 157 centimeters (length + width + height). 3  Maximum weight: 50 pounds or 23 kilograms (for Brazil: 70 pounds or 32 kilograms).
