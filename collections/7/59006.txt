1.) Download and extract the folder for the Minecraft: PE map that you want to play. 2.) Download and install iExplorer (the free demo version does everything you need). 3.) Connect your iOS device and Computer together with a USB cable, and then open iExplorer.nce you're ready to extract the map, you will need to locate your minecraft saves folder. To find it, follow these steps: 1.) Open the Minecraft Launcher. 2.) Click on the Edit Profile button in the bottom left corner. 3.) Click on the Open Game Dir button on the bottom right corner of the Profile Editor.
