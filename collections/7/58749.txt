A promissory note is a contract between two parties. One party is obtaining a loan from the other, promising to repay the money within a designated period of time.For the loan, the lending party may receive interest on the amount owed until repayment of the loan.efine who the two parties are in the promissory note agreement and include contact information. The promisor, also known as an obligor, is the person getting the loan and promising to repay it under the contract terms.
