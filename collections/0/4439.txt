Kovrov (Roushie: ковро́в) is a ceety in Vladimir Oblast, Roushie, locatit on the richt bank o the Klyazma River (a tributar o the Oka). Kovrov's population as of the 2010 Census wis 145,214; doun frae 155,499 recordit in the 2002 Census, an further doun frae 159,942 recordit in the 1989 Census). In terms o population, it is the seicont-lairgest ceety in Vladimir Oblast (efter Vladimir).
