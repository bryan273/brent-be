Fancy Yellow diamonds, on the other hand, will price out similar to G color white diamonds. See these two examples: 1) 1.24 Carats, Fancy Yellow, VS1 Clarity, Radiant Cut, GIA Certified – $6300. 2) 1.13 Carats, G Color, VVS1 Clarity, Radiant Cut, GIA Certified – $5790. Similarly, its possible even to find a Fancy Intense Yellow Diamond that compares in price to an F or G color white diamond.
