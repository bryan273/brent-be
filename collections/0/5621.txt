‘Sharknado 4’ Review: This Joke Has No Teeth Anymore. A joke might be funny the first time, but by the fourth time you hear it, the punchline gets tired. “Tired” is a good description for “Sharknado 4: The 4th Awakens,” which premieres Sunday on Syfy. Although the parody movie is as absurd and silly as the first three installments were, this time around the whole thing feels forced.
