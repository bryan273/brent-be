How smoking causes lung cancer. Doctors believe smoking causes lung cancer by damaging the cells that line the lungs. When you inhale cigarette smoke, which is full of cancer-causing substances (carcinogens), changes in the lung tissue begin almost immediately. At first your body may be able to repair this damage.But with each repeated exposure, normal cells that line your lungs are increasingly damaged.ronchioles and alveoli.
