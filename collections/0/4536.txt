How much does a project manager make? According to the United States Bureau of Labor Statistics, there were 1,899,460 project managers employed in various fields throughout the United States in 2012. The average yearly project manager salary is $114,850, which is equivalent to a mean hourly wage of $55.22. The lowest 10% in the profession earn $46,890 or less per annum, while the highest 10% of project managers earn upwards of $179,480 annually.
