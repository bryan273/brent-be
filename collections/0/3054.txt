TENNO REINFORCEMENTS. Posted On 2014-03-26 18:00:15. Warframe is celebrating its One Year Anniversary! DEX FURIS - In celebration of Warframe's First Year, and to Thank all who have helped build Warframe into what it is today, The Lotus is gifting each Tenno with a First Anniversary Edition of the DEX FURIS. This dual version features a Lotus-themed skin, high fire rate and accuracy and low recoil.
