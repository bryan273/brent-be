University of Georgia is a public institution that was founded in 1785. It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 27,951, its setting is city, and the campus size is 767 acres. It utilizes a semester-based academic calendar. University of Georgia's ranking in the 2018 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, 54. Its in-state tuition and fees are $11,818 (2017-18); out-of-state tuition and fees are $30,392 (2017-18). At its founding, The University of Georgia made history as the first public, state-supported college in America.
