After seven the Good Witch movies for the Hallmark Channel, the magical Catherine Bell-starrer was morphed into a TV series. Now it’s back for a second season and Cassie (Bell), now a widow, and her neighbor Sam (James Denton) are in the throes of deciding if they want to move their friendship on to something more romantic.
