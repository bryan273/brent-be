How beautiful upon the mountains are the feet of him that announceth glad tidings, that publisheth peace; that announceth glad tidings of good, that publisheth salvation, that saith unto Zion, Thy God reigneth! English Revised Version.ow beautiful on the mountains are the feet of the messenger who announces the good news, All is well.. He brings the good news, announces salvation, and tells Zion that its God rules as king. JPS Tanakh 1917.
