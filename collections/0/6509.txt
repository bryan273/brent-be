For example, punishments for misdemeanors will generally look like the following: 1  Class 1 or A: Fines of up to $5,000, and/or a jail sentence of up to 12 months. 2  Class 2 or B: Fines up to $1,000, and/or a jail sentence of 6-9 months. 3  Class 3 or C: Fines up to $1,000 and/or a jail sentence of up to 3 months.
