There are two easy ways to take a screenshot using the Surface Pro 3. The first method uses the touch Windows button on the right side of the Surface Pro 3 while in landscape mode. Hold it down and then press the volume down button on the left edge of the Surface Pro 3.This saves the screenshot in the Pictures folder under a folder called Screenshots.
