Fastest Woman Around The World. “Somedays I’m so tired, having had four hours of sleep total that week, and I might feel sick or emotional, but I know that if I don’t muster up the physical and mental strength to keep going, I’ll fall behind. And that’s not an option,” says Cassie De Pecol, triathlete and Guinness World Recorder chaser. The 26-year-old American has set herself the target of being the fastest woman in the world to visit all 196 countries in less than three years.
