Schofield Barracks is located at 21°29′52″N 158°3′48″W / 21.49778°N 158.06333°W / 21.49778; -158.06333 (21.497650, -158.063248). The Main Gate used to be off Wilikina Drive; however, now only the Foote and Lyman gates located along Kunia Road are used for controlled access.
