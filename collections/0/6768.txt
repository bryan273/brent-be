The department is certified by the Commission on Accreditation for Law Enforcement Agencies and is located in Port Orange, Fla. It serves more than 56,800 residents and maintains one of the lowest crime rates in the state of Florida. Port Orange Police Department operates a driver safety program especially for motorists of 50 years and above. Its athletic league serves children in the age group of 8 to18 and offers information on various sports programs, such as boxing, baseball, basketball, bowling and soccer.
