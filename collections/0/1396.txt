DOWNEY -- Golden Corral will open a buffet restaurant at the southeast intersection of Lakewood Boulevard and Gallatin Road, formerly the site of a Ford dealership, Mayor Pro Tem Alex Saab confirmed Wednesday.Saab made the announcement during a town hall meeting held at the Columbia Memorial Space Center.olden Corral is expected to open at a development called The Commons at Gallatin Road, an 18,000 sq. ft. development that is also expected to include three other smaller restaurants.
