One of the most questioned aspects of wildland fire training is the certification requirements for Incident Command System (ICS) and National Incident Management System (NIMS) certification. The basis for NIMS/ICS requirements is outlined by the Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) at this website. The National Wildfire Coordinating Group (NWCG) clarified the wildland training requirements of NIMS/ICS last month, and has an easy-to-use chart.
