Turkey on Jail Turkey on Jail Escape is another new point and click room escape game developed by Games2rule.One day turkey was feared about thanksgiving day celebration so turkey made an escape from farm house. But turkey was caught by someone and put it on a jail. Here the turkey takes frosting because of going to be dead on thanksgiving day. And you are the only person to help the turkey out. You will have to use your intelligence to explore the room by finding useful hints, objects and escape the turkey from the jail.
