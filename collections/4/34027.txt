NEW: Marijuana excise tax rates change. Beginning July 1, 2015, the excise tax structure for recreational marijuana is changing. Under the new law (2E2SHB 2136), the marijuana excise tax is: 1  No longer levied on the producers, processors and retailers. 2  Instead, the tax is now imposed on retail customers. 3  Going from 25 percent (at each level) to 37 percent on the retail customer.
