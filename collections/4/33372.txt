Hematopoiesis is a process that helps the body to fight infection. Erythrocytes are red blood cells that carry oxygen to different areas of the body. Hematopoiesis creates new blood cells. Hematopoiesis takes place in the liver, bone marrow and spleen during fetal development.Bone marrow contains a collection of stem cells that can work to create more blood cells when needed.
