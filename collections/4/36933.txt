Clemson University is one of the less selective engineering schools, admitting 2,939 students in 2013 with an acceptance rate of 75.05%. This is an admit rate higher than the average for all engineering schools (63.49%). Yield Rate. Clemson University has a yield rate of 37.90%, which means it is more likely for a student to accept an offer of admission from this school than the average engineering school (the average yield rate is 35.77%).
