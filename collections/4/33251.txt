The first documented gridiron football match was a game played at University College, a college of the University of Toronto, November 9, 1861.One of the participants in the game involving University of Toronto students was (Sir) William Mulock, later Chancellor of the school.irst American college football game Edit. The first ever intercollegiate football game played under rules which modern audiences would recognize as football occurred between Harvard University and Tufts University on June 4, 1875.
