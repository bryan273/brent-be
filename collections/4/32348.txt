Get Started. STEP 1: Get a SoGo Cash Card. Pick up a card at participating Sodexo locations. If you don't see them displayed, ask a cashier. STEP 2: Activate your card. Take your SoGo Cash Card to your computer and log on to www.sogocashcard.com. Add value to your card on our convenient website using your credit card. Add value to the card right at the cashier station at participating Sodexo locations. Ask any cashier for help. You can load your card using cash or a credit card.
