NBA All-Star Kyrie Irving of the Cleveland Cavaliers has purchased the Westlake, Ohio home of recently split Keyshia Cole and former Cav teammate Daniel Boobie Gibson. Irving paid $800,000 for the 5,508 square-foot home that was built in 2007 and has five bedrooms, four full bathrooms and one half, and a three-car garage.
