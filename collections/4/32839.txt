Loss of earnings benefits continue until you are no longer impaired by your work-related injury or illness, or until you no longer have a loss of earnings, or until you reach age 65. If you are still impaired at age 65, LOE benefits stop, but loss of retirement income benefits may apply.f you miss time from work because of a work-related injury or illness, our insurance pays for loss of earnings. This benefit starts from the working day after the injury or illness occurred. How we calculate your loss of earnings benefit depends on the date the injury occurred.
