Betelgeuse has around a thousand times the radius of the sun, if it was placed in the center of our solar system the star would reach beyond the orbit of Jupiter! Betelgeuse is estimated to have a maximum mass of around 20 to 30 times that of the sun. Betelgeuse is estimated to have surface temperatures of around 3200C (5800F), around 60% as hot as the sun. Due to its enormous size Betelgeuse is around 100,000 times more luminous than the sun. Infrared image of Betelgeuse, the two white areas are hotspots on the star's surface.
