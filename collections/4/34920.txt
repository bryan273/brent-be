We're actors; we're the opposite of people - Tom Stoppard. Shakspeare wrote 37 or 38 plays and 154 sonnets all together, plus some other poetry. How many books that comes to depends on how you bundle them together. None of it (except his two long poems Venus and Adonis and The Rape of Lucrece) was intended to be published at all, and was published by other people at various times and in various bundles.
