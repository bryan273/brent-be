Instructions. 1  Cut the zucchini into bite-sized pieces. Think about how you'll most likely be using the zucchini later on when you chop them up. 2  Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Fill a large bowl with ice water, settle a strainer inside, and place this near the stove. 3  Blanch the zucchini for 45 to 60 seconds.
