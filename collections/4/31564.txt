Southbound on U.S. 101 you will exit at Highway 129 toward Watsonville. The road is a country road that runs straight into the city of San Juan Bautista in a residential area on 1 st Street. Follow the signs to 2 nd Street and then down to the historic area and Mission. From U.S. 101 to the Mission is 3.8 miles. Northbound on U.S. 101 take the Highway 156 exit and proceed 2.5 miles to San Juan Bautista.
