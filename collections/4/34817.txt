The weather in Santorini during winter is slightly more unpredictable than in summer, but the skies generally remain blue and the weather remains mild. Humidity is at its highest during winter, averaging at 74%. Across the winter months, the cold average sea temperature for the beaches in Santorini means that you probably won’t be able to enjoy a swim. The sea is at its coldest at 16°C in January and February, whilst it’s at its warmest at 20°C in November.
