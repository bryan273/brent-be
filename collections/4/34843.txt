Two types of bone tissue are compact bone and spongy bone. Compact bone functions as the basic supporting tissue of the body. Spongy bone's function, meanwhile, is to make up … most of the short, flat, and irregular bones in your body.here are 2 types of bone tissue: Spongy or cancellous  bone (head of most large bones, ribs, shoulder blade) and  Compact (in the diaphysis or middle part of large bones). …  Compact bone forms the extremely hard exterior while spongy bone  (also called cancellous bone) fills the hollow interior.
