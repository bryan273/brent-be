The soil biota includes: 1  Megafauna: size range - 20 mm upward, e.g. moles, rabbits, and rodents. 2  macrofauna: size range - 2 to 20 mm, e.g. woodlice, earthworms, beetles, centipedes, slugs, snails, ants, and harvestmen. 3  Mesofauna: size range - 100 micrometres to 2 mm, e.g. tardigrades, mites and springtails.
