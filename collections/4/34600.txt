Foluke M. Alli, MD is a practicing Family Practitioner in Matthews, NC. Dr. Alli graduated from University of Ibadan in 2002 and has been in practice for 11 years. She completed a residency at Underwood Memorial Hospital. Dr. Alli accepts multiple insurance plans including Aetna, Medicare and Humana. Dr. Alli is board certified in Family Medicine. In addition to English, Dr. Alli's practice supports this language: Spanish.
