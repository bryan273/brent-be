Furies are also known as Erinyes (the kindly ones). Their names are Alecto, Megaera, and Tisiphone. The furies are sisters.Their parents are Gaea and Uranus. They were fertilized by the blood from the castration of Uranus. The three each have fiery eyes, dog heads, and serpents for hair.
