The ulnar nerve and its branches innervate the following muscles in the forearm and hand: An Articular branch that passes to the elbow joint while the ulnar nerve is passing between the olecranon and medial epicondyle of the humerus. 1  In the forearm, via the muscular branches of ulnar nerve 2  : Flexor carpi ulnaris.he ulnar collateral ligament of elbow joint is in relation with the ulnar nerve. The nerve is the largest unprotected nerve in the human body (meaning unprotected by muscle or bone), so injury is common.
