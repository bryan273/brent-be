Acer saccharum, sugar maple, is a large tree native to North America; it is the official State Tree of New York, Vermont, West Virginia, and Wisconsin (USNA 2011). It has a dense, spreading crown, 25-40 m tall.ugar maple is an important timber tree (see Uses). Sugar maple in North America is sometimes confused with Norway maple (Acer platanoides), an introduced European species that looks similar, but is more resistant to pollution and has fewer insect pests.
