A motor vehicle dealer bond, sometimes also called a DMV bond, used car dealer bond, or auto dealer bond, guarantees that the motor vehicle dealer complies with all federal laws, state laws, local laws, and tax and judgment guidelines that relate to motor vehicles.ost of the time, a surety bond's purpose is to protect a consumer against damages due to fraud, defaulting on a contract, or other negative and wrongful behaviors on the part of a business providing a service or product.
