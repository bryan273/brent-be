Fascial Stretch Therapy is a unique system of therapy that dramatically improves your flexibility by lengthening your fascia.Tension in the tissue and around the joints is released to restore and improve range of movement, often relieving pain from past injuries.his therapy was created by former professional ballet dancer Ann & her husband, Physiotherapist, Chris Frederick of the Stretch To Win Institute in Arizona. Fascia is the connective tissue that surrounds & all structures of the body.
