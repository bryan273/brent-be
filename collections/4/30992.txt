Take I-95 SOUTH to SC-38 EAST to MARION (and to MYRTLE BEACH) off EXIT 181A in SOUTH CAROLINA. Take SC-38 EAST to U.S. 501 SOUTH to MYRTLE BEACH, outside of Marion. Take U.S. 501 SOUTH to Myrtle Beach. If you are going to the northern part of Myrtle Beach or North Myrtle Beach, take SC-22 EAST (Conway Bypass).
