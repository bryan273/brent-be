The Village of McFarland Volunteer P rogram provides opportunities for McFarland citizens to contribute to their community. Volunteer Today! Welcome to McFarland, Wisconsin! McFarland is located along U.S. Highway 51 and the east shore of Lake Waubesa. Closely neighboring with Madison, the Towns of Dunn, and Blooming Grove, McFarland offers easy access to the interstate and many travel opportunities while supporting a great neighborhood in which to reside and shop. We have numerous parks and recreation areas along with great local dining and unique shopping venues.
