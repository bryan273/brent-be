Scabies signs and symptoms include: 1  Itching, often severe and usually worse at night. 2  Thin, irregular burrow tracks made up of tiny blisters or bumps on your skin.f you've had scabies before, signs and symptoms may develop within a few days of exposure. However, if you've never had scabies, it could take as long as six weeks for signs and symptoms to begin.
