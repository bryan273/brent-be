Area rock shops. The Southern Hills are a rich lode for rock lovers, as evidenced by the number of rock shops -- from rustic to refined -- that grace the area. Here's a list of rock shops of western South Dakota: Rapid City -- Indigo Iris Rocks & Stones (484-2465).
