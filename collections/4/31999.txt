If you want to get top dollar for your property, you better watch your market time. A lower amount of market time typically results in a higher to list price offer. You will receive the most amount of traffic and showings for your house in the first two weeks your home is listed. It’s very likely your highest offer will come within the first 30 days. The first few weeks of market time is crucial because that’s when everyone takes notice of the new listing on the block.
