Federal Tax Return Step. Determining which line of Form 1040 lists your total income tax for the FAFSA may be confusing because of the wording both on the FAFSA and the federal return. The last line of the Other Taxes section of the Federal 1040 is usually labeled total tax, making you think this is the number to report. But this is incorrect.
