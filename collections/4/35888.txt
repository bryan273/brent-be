Flu. The flu is a viral infection caused by the influenza virus that targets your head and chest. You’ll have symptoms such as a cough, sore throat, and runny nose. Your body will hurt, you’ll be tired, and you might run a fever over 100°F (37.8°C). People often feel the achiness and fatigue first, before their respiratory symptoms develop.
