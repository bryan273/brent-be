Homemade mincemeat. English recipes from the 15th, 16th, and 17th centuries describe a mixture of meat and fruit used as a pie filling. English recipes from the 15th, 16th, and 17th centuries describe a mixture of meat and fruit used as a pie filling. These early recipes included vinegars and wines, but by the 18th century, distilled spirits, frequently brandy, were being used instead.
