Captain Theodore Godet Heyliger was born on Saba on July 2nd, 1834 and died here on October 16th, 1907. He married Ann Louise Simmons on January 20th, 1864. They had no children. The Heyliger family was a prominent Statia family which was among the first Dutch settlers on that island in 1630.e was also Master of the five-master 'Rebecca Palmer'and the four-master 'East Star Jones'. 100.
