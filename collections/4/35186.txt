Data collection for the most recent assessment was completed in Fall 2015. PISA 2015 will assess students' mathematics, reading, and science literacy in more than 70 countries and educational jurisdictions. Science is the major subject area in 2015, as it was in 2006. PISA 2015 also includes a collaborative problem solving assessment and an optional financial literacy assessment. U.S. 15-year-old students participated in this optional assessment. Data from the 2015 assessment will be available in December 2016.
