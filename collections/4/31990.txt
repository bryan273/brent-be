Best Answer: Average time on the market, also known as days on market (DOM) varies from area to area. DOM is dependent on the current real estate markets in your area. A local real estate agent would be able to give you the averages for the areas you are interested in. Look at this site. It give you Georgia market data for like 30 cities. Tybee Island 30-60 days Jonesboro 60 - 90 days Midtown Atlanta 60-90 days It also gives average home prices, which types of houses have the greatest activity (single family home, etc.), and if it's a buyer's or seller's market.
