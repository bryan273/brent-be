Weber grills maker buys ex-Schneider HQ in Palatine. The maker of Weber grills bought a Palatine office building that was the longtime U.S. headquarters of Schneider Electric. Weber-Stephen Products paid $11.9 million for the 154,706-square-foot building at 1415 S. Roselle Road, according to Cook County records. France-based Schneider moved its U.S. headquarters last year to Andover, Mass., and has been seeking a buyer for the three-story building.
