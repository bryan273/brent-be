reek n. Old English rec (Anglian), riec (West Saxon), smoke from burning material, probably from a Scandinavian source, cf. Old Norse reykr, Danish rǿg, Swedish rök smoke, steam, from Proto-Germanic *raukiz (cf. Old Frisian rek, Middle Dutch rooc, Old High German rouh, German Rauch smoke, steam), from PIE *reug-to vomit, belch; also smoke, cloud.
