May 1911 Key to Numerical System of the American Bankers' Association, upper left side of page 72, showing usage of the term bank number at the time. A bank number is a bank code used to identify a bank for electronic purposes in the United States. Bank numbers such as Bank Identifier Code (BIC), Bank Identification Number (BIN), and Routing transit number (RTN) categorizes a bank for machine-aided sorting of checks and so forth.
