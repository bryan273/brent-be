NCUA vs. FDIC: Who Insures Credit Unions and Banks?Here's everything you need to know about NCUA regulations, FDIC insurance limits and more. Some people debate whether banks or credit unions are the best banking choice, citing factors like convenience, customer service and interest rates. What you rarely hear about, however, is the way in which accounts held by these financial institutions are insured. Bank accounts are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation whereas a completely different agency, the National Credit Union Administration, insures credit union deposits.
