Thousands to get refunds of power of attorney fees as applications soar to record numbers. Hundreds of thousands of people may be owed a refund after being overcharged when applying for power of attorney. Power of attorney is a vital document that allows someone to make financial decisions on your behalf should you fall ill or have an accident. Applications have soared to a record 648,318 in the past year, with 2.5 million registered in England and Wales in the past decade.
