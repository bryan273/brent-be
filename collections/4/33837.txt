If you have an abundance and have the freezer space, you might want to freeze the surplus. Freeze the squash cubed or sliced for a side vegetable, casseroles, soups, or stews, or grated it and freeze it in 1- to 2-cup portions for zucchini bread, muffins, and cakes. Preparation and Blanching. Wash the squash and cut off the blossom and stem ends. There's no need to peel the squash.
