Genus vs Species. Although both genus and species are referred to state a particular animal or plant or any organism, there are differences between the two that anyone should know. In biological classification, the genus comes first followed by species, and the species is never stated alone.pecies is a group of organisms with similar features and the sexual reproduction between a male and a female of them produces a fertile offspring. All the organisms of one particular species possess the same number of chromosomes, which means, they have similar morphological features.
