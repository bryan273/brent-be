From outermost to innermost, Meninges around the brain has three layers : 1. The dura mater 2. the arachnoid mater and 3. the pia mater. The dura mater has two layers : periosteal and meningeal. There is a space between the dura mater and the arachnoid mater, called subdural space. There is also a space between the arachnoid and the pia mater, which is called subarachnoid space.
