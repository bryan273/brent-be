Golett (Japanese: ゴビット Gobitto) is a Ground/Ghost-type Pokémon introduced in Generation V. Golett is a light, blue, bulky robot-like Pokémon, with yellow lights and a blue diamond with a spiral pattern on his stomach. It has stubby rocks for feet and big, bulky arms. Golett has asymmetrical eyes, which is a characteristic only found in its evolutionary line.
