In topography, a summit is a point on a surface that is higher in elevation than all points immediately adjacent to it. Mathematically, a summit is a local maximum in elevation. The topographic terms acme, apex, peak, and zenith are synonyms. The summit is the highest point.ummit refers to the highest point in a mountain. Oxford: the highest point of a hill or mountain. and the word comes from the Latin root summus, which means the highest. Every mountain has exactly one summit.
