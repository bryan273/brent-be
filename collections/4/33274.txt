Mini Bio (1) Christopher Connelly was born on September 8, 1941 in Wichita, Kansas, USA as Thomas Maume Connelly. He was an actor, known for Peyton Place (1964), Manhattan Baby (1982) and 1990: The Bronx Warriors (1982). He was married to Cindy Carol. He died on December 7, 1988 in Burbank, California, USA.
