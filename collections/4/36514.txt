Best Answer: Checking your score thru one of the credit bureaus is a soft pull and does not affect your score. Applying for multiple credit cards and personal loans in a short timeframe takes a bite out of your score. Stop applying for more credit cards.Use the credit card you have and pay if in full every month.Don't carry balances. A 24 month consistent on time payment history will improve your score. By the way, it really is a waste of money to sign up for a monitoring service or to buy your score every few months.se the credit card you have and pay if in full every month. Don't carry balances.
