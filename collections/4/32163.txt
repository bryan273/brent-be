This is the smallest poodle size. Toy poodles are 10 inches or under at the highest point of the shoulder, and typically weigh less than 12 pounds. Miniature poodles are taller than 10 inches and up to 15 inches at the highest point of the shoulder. Minis generally weigh between 15 and 35 pounds, depending on their height. This medium poodle size is energetic and needs a moderate amount of exercise. Minis are the most common size we see come into rescue.
