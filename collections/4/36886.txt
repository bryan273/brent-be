Climate, on the other hand, is the general weather conditions. For example, in the winter, we expect it to often be rainy in Portland, Oregon, sunny and mild in Phoenix, Arizona, and very cold and snowy in Buffalo, New York.eteorologists often point out that climate is what you expect and weather is what you get.. Or, as one middle school student put it, Climate helps you decide what clothes to buy, weather helps you decide what clothes to wear.. Climate is sometimes referred to as average weather for a given area.
