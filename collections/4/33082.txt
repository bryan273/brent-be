In  Hunt for Ziro , the ninth episode of the third season of Star Wars: The Clone Wars, several Twi'lek dancers wore outfits similar Leia's slave outfit. While some were golden like Leia's original, others were bronze and copper.uring a Halloween party at the end of the episode, Sarah appears wearing her own slave Leia costume, much to the delight of Chuck and the other male guests. Chuck and Sarah take another, genuine photo of each other together in the costumes.
