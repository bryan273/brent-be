Here is a biblical definition of redemption. A Secular Definition of Redemption. If you went up someone on the street and ask them what the word redemption meant, they might give you a different definition than someone who is a believer in Christ. The secular definition of redemption is the act of redeeming or atoning for a fault or mistake. Other definitions include repurchasing something or making a deliverance or rescue. Some include the idea of making something or someone better or more acceptable.
