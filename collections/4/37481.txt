Other Muscles. The pronator teres, flexor carpi radialis and extensor carpi radialis longus, three small muscles in the forearm, are also involved in elbow flexion, but not to the extent of the biceps, brachialis and brachioradialis.esides elbow flexion, the biceps muscle is also involved in forearm supination -- turning your forearm so your palm faces up. It's more active in elbow flexion when your forearm is supinated and less active when your forearm is pronated -- your palm faces down.
