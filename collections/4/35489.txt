6. Transplant blue poppy seedling into pots once they grow their first set of true leaves. Fill 4-inch plastic pots with the same growing mixture used during germination, inserting your finger to make a hole for each seedling. Gently knock the seedlings out of the seed tray and ease them apart.. Sterilize shallow seed trays with warm water and bleach mixture before use. Once dry, fill the seed trays to the top with the growing medium. Scatter blue poppy seeds on the soil surface and use a finger to press them lightly so that the seeds make contact with the growing medium.
