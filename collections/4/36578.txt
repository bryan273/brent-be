Nintendo font here refers to the font used in the logo of Nintendo, which is a Japanese consumer electronics and video games company founded in 1889. The Nintendo logo, which dates back to the 1980s, features the Nintendo logotype in a capsule like shape. The original color for it was red but the grey color was adopted in 2006.
