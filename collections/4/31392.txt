While the Black Codes ceased in 1866, it opened the door to more repressive restrictions on blacks such as the Pig Laws of the 1870s. Eventually, the Jim Crow era (1890s-1965) adopted many of the restrictions seen under the Black Codes and went even further in relegating blacks to second class citizens.
