OPTION TWO Connect to Available Wireless Network in Settings app 1. Open the Settings app, and click/tap on the Network & Internet icon. 2. Click/tap on Wi-Fi in the left pane, and click/tap on an available wireless network (ex: Brink-Router2) that you want to connect to.(see screenshot below) 3.ou can now close the Settings app if you like. OPTION THREE Connect to Hidden Wireless Network in Settings app 1. Open the Settings app, and click/tap on the Network & Internet icon. 2. Click/tap on Wi-Fi in the left pane, and click/tap on Hidden Network at the bottom.
