Use the following steps to restore your computer when Windows does not open normally. This video shows how to restore your PC when Windows cannot start normally. Click here to see this video in full screen on YouTube. Turn off the computer and wait at least 5 seconds with the computer in a full off state.n Windows, search for restore, and then open Create a restore point from the results list. On the System Properties window, select your hard drive, and then click Configure. Figure: System Properties. The System Protection for Local Disk window opens.
