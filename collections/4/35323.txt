Problems with Title in Real Estate. Even though you may have bought a house, and paid off the mortgage, you may not necessarily have clear title to your property. Some of the clouds or exceptions to your title can include: 1  Past ownership claims: Sometimes, when a particular piece of land has been in the family for generations, the original owner can be forgotten.  Liens: Liens are like mini-mortgages placed on the property to force the property owner to pay a debt.
