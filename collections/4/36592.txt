How to forget wireless networks in Windows 10. If you need to remove or forget wireless networks that you don't use anymore this is how you can do that. From Wi-Fi, click the Manage Wi-Fi settings. Then, from the list of Mange known networks, click on the relevant network, and then click the Forget button.nside Settings, users will find the new Network & Internet section. Unlike Windows 8, the new settings in Windows 10 are much improved. For example, in Windows 10, Microsoft is dividing wireless and wired connections in different sections (Wi-Fi and Ethernet).
