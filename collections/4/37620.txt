Photo Copyright Lonnie Burr. Probably the most well known Mouseketeer is Annette Funicello. She managed to capture the essence of the things Americans in the 1950’s and 1960’s wanted to believe about themselves. After the Mickey Mouse Club, Annette went on to make a few records, have her own TV show, and of course star in movies.
