2 mountain lion litters found in California mountains. This June. 22, 2016, photo released by the National Park Service shows kitten siblings P-50, P-51, and P-52 Litter in the eastern Santa Susana Mountains. THOUSAND OAKS, Calif. – The National Park Service has released photos and video of two litters of mountain lion kittens in the Santa Susana Mountains north of Los Angeles. The Park Service said Wednesday the five kittens — three females and two males — were tagged and returned to their dens.
