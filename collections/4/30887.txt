Genes. A gene is a length of DNA that codes for a specific protein. So, for example, one gene will code for the protein insulin, which is important role in helping your body to control the amount of sugar in your blood. Genes are the basic unit of genetics. Human beings have 20,000 to 25,000 genes.These genes account for only about 3 per cent of our DNA.o, for example, one gene will code for the protein insulin, which is important role in helping your body to control the amount of sugar in your blood.
