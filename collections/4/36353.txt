The Samurai X Cave was built by Nya in a desert mountain. It serves as her personal headquarters and storage for her various machines. Following the demise of the Overlord, Wu and Nya began rebuilding the Destiny's Bounty in the cave and it served as their headquarters during the second Serpentine War. Garmadon and Lloyd head to the Samurai Cave to retreve Nya's car in order to get as far away as possible from the Overlord.
