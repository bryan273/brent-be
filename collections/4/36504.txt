Top 50 Love Songs of All Time. We've ranked the biggest 'Love' songs to ever hit the Hot 100. Songs have been written about every topic imaginable, but the best ones -- from swooning '50s ballads to contemporary club bangers -- have been penned about the ups and downs of being in love. We are counting down the top 50 Hot 100 hits with a form of the word love in the title. The romantic tunes cover all seven decades of the Hot 100's history, ranging from 1958's To Know Him Is to Love Him by the Teddy Bears to 2011's We Found Love by Rihanna.
