Tragus piercing: it’s placed on the thich cartilage part that is closest to your head, on the front part of your ear. It’s usually a ring or a small stud. 1 to 6 months healing time. Inner conch piercing: it’s made on the flat part of the cartilage more or less in the middle of your auricle. It’s usually a barbell that comes out from the back of your ear. 6 months for primary healing, 12 to 18 for full healing. Anti-tragus piercing: it’s the part of your ear right under/in front of your tragus, i.e. opposite the ear canal. 6 to 12 months healing time.
