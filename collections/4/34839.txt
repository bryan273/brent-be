Bone tissue, or osseous tissue, is a type of connective tissue used in forming bones. Bones protect the vital organs and help support the body. It is composed mainly of collagen, or ossein, fibers, and bone cells called osteocytes. There are two types of bone tissue, referred to as cortical bone and cancellous bone.Cortical bone is also known as compact or dense bone, and most of a body's bones are made from this kind of bone.
