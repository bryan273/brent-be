Loquats peak season is mid-winter into spring. The Loquat, botanically known as Eriobotrya japonica, is also commonly known as the Japanese plum. Loquats are a member of the Rosaceae family, also known as the rose family, along with apples, strawberries and pears.he Loquat, botanically known as Eriobotrya japonica, is also commonly known as the Japanese plum.
