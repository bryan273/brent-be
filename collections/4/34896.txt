If nothing else, his characters — George Washington in “Hamilton” and a fashion consultant on “Bull” — have allowed the actor to explore the power of image and appearance. Jackson plays Chunk Palmer on “Bull,” which stars Michael Weatherly as an unconventional but in-demand trial consultant, Dr. Jason Bull. He and his team create detailed psychological profiles of jurors, opposing counsel and witnesses, and they also make sure their defendants have the right look for every situation.
