Why Abba’s Dancing Queen is the best pop song ever. Since its 1976 release, Abba’s ‘absolute best song’ (according to Frida Lyngstad) has won over everyone from punks to royalty and almost caused a riot in New York. So how has the song’s low-lit Friday night managed to last for ever? Abba in 1977: Benny Andersson, Frida Lyngstad, Agnetha Fältskog and Björn Ulvaeus.
