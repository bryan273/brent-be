Bandeja Paisa. This recipe is one of the most popular of Colombian meals. It is a complex dish, not because is difficult to make, but because it has many different ingredients. It is better to be in a group of friends or family to share all the cooking and the fun. Bandeja Paisa or Paisa Meal Tray (English version below) Ingredientes para 4 personas. 500 gramos de arroz blanco. 4 Arepas.
