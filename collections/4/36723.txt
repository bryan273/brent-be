Full Definition of DERIVATIVE. 1. : a word formed by derivation. 2. : something derived. 3. : the limit of the ratio of the change in a function to the corresponding change in its independent variable as the latter change approaches zero.4. a contract or security that derives its value from that of an underlying asset (as another security) or from the value of a rate (as of interest or currency exchange) or index of asset value (as a stock index).
