Official Crafting Dead Version Aftermath 1.4.7b. Check out the huge fleet of servers for multiplayer! Survive the Apocalypse with a friend and conquer the world with tons of guns and melee weapons! Crafting Dead is a Multiplayer based mod that puts you into a Zombie Apocalypse. Look for loot, craft base parts and fend off endless masses of zombies. The mod completely changes the way you look at Minecraft.
