Vehicles that are self-parked for more than one hour are subject to a parking fee. The self-parking fee is USD 7 for vehicles parked 1-4 hours and USD 10 for vehicles parked 4-24 hours. The valet parking fee is USD 13 for the first 4 hours (no grace period) and USD 18 for 4-24 hours.
