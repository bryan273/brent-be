The Crafting Dead. The Crafting Dead is a Minecraft Youtube mystery/adventure series starring FuriousDestroyer (PopularMMOs) and SuperGirlyGamer (GamingWithJen). It takes place in a heavily modded world with lots of zombies. In this series, Pat and Jen are trying to find out who the killer is and stop Herobrine. Along the way, they run into many problems and NPCs, like an insane Dr. Trayaurus and Evil Jen. They also loot chests, defeat mutant mobs, and go on quests.
