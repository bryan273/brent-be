HUNK is playable in the Mercenaries minigame in Resident Evil 4, but has no relation to the story. HUNK appears as a side character in Resident Evil: Operation Raccoon City, where he works with other Umbrella Security Service operatives to recover a sample of the G-virus prior to the events of Resident Evil 2. He is also a playable character in Resident Evil: The Mercenaries 3D and Resident Evil Revelations.
