This season pairs hilarious TV and film producer, Judd Apatow (Superbad,Freaks and Geeks) with the voice of a new generation, Lena Dunham (HBO's Girls). Maverick actor, James Franco (127 Hours) meets up with boundary pushing artist, Marina Abramovic. The outspoken creator of Family Guy, Seth McFarlane, shares his musical chops with jazz songstress, Norah Jones.
