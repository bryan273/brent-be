Medicaid and Managed Care Breast Cancer Surgery. Breast Cancer Surgery Facilities for Medicaid Recipients; Enrolling in a Medicaid Managed Care Plan. Medicaid Managed Care offers many New Yorkers a chance to choose a Medicaid health plan. Managed Care plans focus on preventive health care and provide enrollees with a medical home for themselves and their families. In many counties, once you are eligible for Medicaid, you can join a plan if there is one available and you want to join.
