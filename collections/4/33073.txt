The Meet Outfit are the clothes a doll is wearing when she is purchased. This term was coined from both the original titles of the historical books and the dolls themselves; the first Historical book was titled Meet ______ and the Historical Characters were originally sold with their Meet Books.hen a character is retired, so is her meet outfit. With BeForever, the new outfits are currently available separately for $36; later, the original Classic Outfits were made available for Addy, Julie, Rebecca, Josefina, and Kit for $36.
