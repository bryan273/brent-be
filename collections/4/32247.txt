Spring and Fall. Spring and fall temperatures in Savannah can range from comfortable to warm. Daily highs in April average in the 70s, creeping into the 80s in May, with overnight lows in the 50s and 60s. Highs in October and November tend to run in the 70s, with nightly lows falling from the 50s into the 40s.ummer. Savannah summers are long, hot and steamy, rendering air-conditioning a necessity for most visitors. Daytime high temperatures in the hottest months of the year, July and August, average in the low 90s, and overnight lows typically sink only into the low 70s.
