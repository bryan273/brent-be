A. Uric acid is a chemical breakdown product of metabolism. The liver produces uric acid by breaking down other chemicals, called purines. Uric acid is normally found in the blood in low concentrations. Some people are prone to high uric acid levels, which can sometimes lead to gout, kidney stones, or kidney damage..
