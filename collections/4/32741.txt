France has been selected to host the 2019 FIFA Women's World Cup finals, and will also stage the 2018 FIFA U-20 Women's World Cup. The French Football Federation (FFF) bid was picked ahead of that of South Korea at today's FIFA Executive Committee meeting in Zurich.
