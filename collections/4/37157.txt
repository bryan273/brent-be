Survive the Apocalypse with a friend and conquer the world with tons of guns and melee weapons! Description: Crafting Dead is a Multiplayer based mod that puts you into a Zombie Apocalypse. Look for loot, craft base parts and fend off endless masses of zombies. The mod completely changes the way you look at Minecraft. Customize your character with backpacks, clothing, tactical vests and guns! The guns are fully customizable with attachments and paints! Crafting Dead has been around for over two years now and is still constantly evolving. Welcome to Aftermath.
