1 If you use big sidewalk chalk, you’ll need two chalk sticks per court. 2  If you use vinyl court lines, you should use two sets per court for easier visibility.  If you paint a full court, you’ll generally need 20 gallons of PicklePave acylic surface coating and 1 gallon of California Sports Surfaces textured line paint. PicklePave’s spread-rate is about 90 sq. ft per gallon using a 3/4 “ nap roller on a textured asphalt surface.
