Common symptoms of a duodenal ulcer. You may experience duodenal ulcer symptoms daily or just once in a while. At times any of these abdominal symptoms can be severe: 1  Abdominal bloating. 2  Belching. 3  Burning stomach or upper abdominal pain that may be severe. 4  Feeling of fullness. 5  Loss of appetite. 6  Nausea with or without vomiting. 7  Unexplained weight loss.
