Symptoms for Children. Infants and children with celiac disease tend to have digestive problems. Common symptoms for infants and children include: 1  Growth problems. 2  Decreased appetite and failure to gain weight. 3  Chronic diarrhea, which can be bloody. 4  Chronic constipation. 5  Vomiting. 6  Abdominal bloating and pain. 7  Fatigue.
