Conduit & Raceway Fittings. Conduit is a tube that is used to protect and route electrical wiring in a building or non-building structure. It may be made of metal, plastic, fiber or fired clay. Most conduit is rigid, but flexible conduit is used for some purposes. Raceway Fittings have a low voltage, one-piece hinged design with a single channel surface raceway that includes adhesive backing and shall be made of impact resistant material with a smooth finish.
