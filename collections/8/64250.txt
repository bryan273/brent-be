Jet Set Radio (ジェットセットラジオ, Jetto Setto Rajio), titled Jet Grind Radio on the first North American release, is an action game developed by the Sega studio Smilebit. It was published by Sega for the Dreamcast on June 29, 2000 in Japan, October 30, 2000 in North America, and November 24, 2000 in Europe.
