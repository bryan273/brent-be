Best Answer: It seems that the both words are quite similar, but in fact both are quite different from each other.if the words illegal and unlawful are considered in regard the sense which both conveys, it will be clear that unlawful covers the more area as that of illegal.he adjectives unlawful and illegal can both mean ‘contrary to or forbidden by law’, but unlawful has a broader meaning ‘not permitted by rules’: thus handball in soccer is unlawful, but not illegal.
