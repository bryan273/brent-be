More About The Side Faces. The side faces of a prism are parallelograms (4-sided shape with opposites sides parallel). A prism can lean to one side, making it an oblique prism, but the two ends are still parallel, and the side faces are still parallelograms! But if the two ends are not parallel it is not a prism.
