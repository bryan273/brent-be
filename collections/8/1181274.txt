1 Myotonic (also called MMD or Steinert's disease). 2  The most common form of muscular dystrophy in adults, myotonic muscular dystrophy affects both men and women, and it usually appears any time from early childhood to adulthood. 3  In rare cases, it appears in newborns (congenital MMD). Duchenne. 2  The most common form of muscular dystrophy in children, Duchenne muscular dystrophy affects only males. 3  It appears between the ages of 2 and 6. 4  The muscles decrease in size and grow weaker over time yet may appear larger.
