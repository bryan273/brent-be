The urethra is the tube between the bladder and the end of the penis. Urethritis means inflammation of the urethra. 1  Gonococcal urethritis is caused by a germ (bacterium) called Neisseria gonorrhoeae.2  Non-gonococcal urethritis (NGU) is due to causes other than gonorrhoea. 3  Some men have both gonococcal and non-gonococcal urethritis at the same time.
