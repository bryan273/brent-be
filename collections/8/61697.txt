Etymology & Historical Origin - Conner. The name Conner is most likely a variant spelling of the Irish name Connor. There are two possible origins for the name – one is Irish Gaelic and one is Anglo-Saxon. The Irish name Connor is derived from the old Gaelic name “Conchobhar” meaning ‘lover of hounds’ or from “conáire” meaning ‘hound-nobleman’. Conchobhar Mac Nessa was a legendary king of Ulster in Irish mythology.
