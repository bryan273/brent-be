Headache is one of the symptoms of hangover and it might be associated with other symptoms like nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, excessive thirst, depressed mood, anxiety, irritability, fatigue, weakness, photophobia, etc. Hangover symptoms will again return once the blood alcohol levels falls again. 2  Taking Acetaminophen Before Going to Bed: Acetaminophen (Tylenol) should never be taken after drinking heavily. 3  Acetaminophen is has potential to cause liver toxicity and this effect is potentiated in presence of alcohol.
