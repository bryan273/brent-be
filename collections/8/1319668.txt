9 Potential Causes For Severe Stomach Pains. 2. Lactose Intolerance. Lactose intolerance causes, simply, a lack of the enzyme, lactase, which makes it impossible to digest lactose (or the sugars in dairy). Typically symptoms such as gas, abdominal pain, cramps, nausea, bloating, diarrhea, and even vomiting will occur within 30-minutes of consuming a dairy product.
