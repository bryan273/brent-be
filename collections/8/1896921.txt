Symptoms. The main symptom of burning mouth syndrome (BMS) is pain in the mouth that is burning, scalding, or tingling. Or, the pain may be a feeling of numbness. Other symptoms include dry mouth or altered taste in the mouth.BMS is a painful condition.ey Points. 1  Burning mouth syndrome is burning pain in the mouth that may occur every day for months or longer. 2  Doctors and dentists do not have a specific test for burning mouth syndrome, which makes it hard to diagnose. 3  No specific treatment works for all people.
