The baseline, non-high-cost conforming loan limits for 2016 are : 1-unit home : $417,000; 2-unit home : $533,850; 3-unit home : $645,300; 4-unit home : $801,950; High-cost conforming loan limits range up to $625,000 for a one-unit home; $800,775 for a two-unit home; $967,950 for a three-unit; and $1,202,925 for a four-unit.
