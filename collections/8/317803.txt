A boil is also referred to as a skin abscess. Boils can occur anywhere on the body, including the trunk, extremities, buttocks, or other areas. There are several different types of boils: 1  Furuncle or carbuncle: This is an abscess in the skin usually caused by the bacterium Staphylococcus aureus. 2  A furuncle can have one or more openings onto the skin and may be associated with a fever or chills.
