With the standard wall thickness of EMT and the strength of steel, Allied Tube & Conduit®’s EMT Conduit and elbows create an Electrical Raceway System recognized as an equipment grounding conductor. EMT conduit is a raceway solution which allows for future wiring changes and provides excellent mechanical protection to conductors and cables. EMT provides EMI shielding and is 100% recyclable.
