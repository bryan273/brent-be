The City of Wilmington City of Wilmington - Tax Assessor & Finance Officer Office is located at 800 North French Street, at the Louis L. Redding City/County Building building in Wilmington, Delaware. City of Wilmington is a locality in New Castle County, Delaware. Unlike many other areas where property taxes are collected on a county level, City of Wilmington has its own tax assessor's office.
