The Field Artillery branch was founded on 17 November 1775 by the Continental Congress, which unanimously elected Henry Knox Colonel of the Regiment of Artillery.The regiment formally entered service on 1 January 1776.rtillery of all types was part of the Artillery Corps until 1901, when the Corps was split into battery-sized units of Field Artillery and Coast Artillery. In 1907 the Artillery Corps was reorganized into the Field Artillery and the Coast Artillery Corps.
