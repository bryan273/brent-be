If you are living with pain, becoming aware of some of the known medications most often prescribed by doctors can give you an idea of what may be prescribed for your own pain. Some of these narcotic pain meds(most belonging to the narcotic analgesics drug group) include: Buprenorphine: Used to treat moderate to severe pain. Buprenorphine can be absorbed through the skin with the use of a transdermal patch, it can be taken by mouth in the form of tablets, or injected.
