Your doctor may recommend that you have upper GI endoscopy with a colonoscopy. The decision to have these procedures is yours. Upper GI endoscopy is also known as EGD for EsophagoGastroDuodenoscopy. EGD and colonoscopy can be life saving.ormally, you can eat after the procedure. Call your doctor immediately if any of the following occur: 1  You have trouble swallowing. 2  You have throat, belly, or chest pain that worsens. 3  You vomit. 4  You have blood in your bowel movement (Feces look black or very dark when they contain blood). 5  You have a fever.
