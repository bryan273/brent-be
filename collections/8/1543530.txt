A wart is typically a small growth that appears on a person's hands or feet and looks like a solid blister or a small cauliflower. They may also appear on other parts of the body. Warts have a rough texture and are caused by viruses, particularly one of several kinds of HPV (human papillomavirus).The virus causes keratin, which is a hard protein on the top layer of the skin, to grow too fast.Warts are not the same as moles.hey may also appear on other parts of the body.
