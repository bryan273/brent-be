Definition. A gastrointestinal fistula is an abnormal opening in the stomach or intestines that allows the contents to leak. 1  Leaks that go through to a part of the intestines are called entero-enteral fistulas.2  Leaks that go through to the skin are called enterocutaneous fistulas.auses. Most gastrointestinal fistulas occur after surgery. Other causes include: 1  Blockage in the gastrointestinal tract. 2  Infection. 3  Inflammatory bowel disease (most often Crohn's disease).
