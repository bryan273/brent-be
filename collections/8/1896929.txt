Burning Mouth / Burning Tongue Syndrome is a relatively common condition that is characterized by a moderate to severe burning sensation on tongue or other mouth tissues. Patients initially experience a burning, itchy, dry or sticky mouth.ossible causes of burning mouth syndrome, include 1  : Dry mouth syndrome, caused by medications, alcohol and tobacco use, aging. 2  Oral thrush, an infection that occurs when the chemical balance of the body is disrupted and the fungus Candida albicans grows out of control.
