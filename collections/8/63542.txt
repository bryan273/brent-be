Steeped in natural and historical features, the 696-acre Pine Grove Furnace State Park is at the northern tip of the Blue Ridge Mountains, in an area known as South Mountain. Visitors enjoy many recreational opportunities, including: 1  Two mountain lakes (Laurel Lake and Fuller Lake) 2  Hiking the Appalachian Trail. 3  Biking the rail trail. 4  Visiting the Appalachian Trail Museum. 5  Imagining when the park was a charcoal-fired iron furnace community.
