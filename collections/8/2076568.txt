If you suffer from gout, knowing the foods that contain high levels of purines can help you avoid these foods and prevent a gout attack. 1  Meat-Out of all foods that are high in purines, meat is ranked the highest. 2  Organ meats, such as liver, heart, kidney and brain, contain the most amount of purines. Shellfish such as crab, lobster, and shrimp, sardines, tuna, mackerel, herring, halibut and salmon are some seafood that contains high purine levels. 2  Vegetables-Although certain vegetables are high in purines, they are not as high as in organ meats and seafood.
