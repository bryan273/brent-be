The census is a count of all the people in the United Kingdom and is normally taken every 10 years. The 1901 census was taken on 31 March 1901, and recorded the details of over 32 million people who were resident in England and Wales at that time.Since all the details are collected at the same time, the census provides a snapshot of what life was like on census night.Taking the 1901 census.he 1901 census was taken on 31 March 1901, and recorded the details of over 32 million people who were resident in England and Wales at that time.
