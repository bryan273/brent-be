Gout and the Role of Purines. Uric acid is a waste product found normally in your body as a result of purine digestion. Purine is a compound found mostly in animal protein. When you eat foods high in purines, you have higher uric acid levels.Gout occurs when uric acid accumulates in your body.ou must avoid organ meats since they're very high in purines. Other foods that contain a rich level of purines are canned sardines, anchovies, trout, canned herring, and shrimp. These foods contain anywhere from 234 to 554 milligrams of purines per serving.
