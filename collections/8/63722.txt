Symptoms of urinary schistosomiasis infection, due to the damage eggs cause to the bladder and kidneys, include abdominal pain, painful urination, and blood in the urine. Symptoms of intestinal schistosomiasis, due to the damage caused by eggs to the intestine and liver, include abdominal pain, fever and blood in the stool. Depending on the parasite, schistosomiasis infection can lead to chronic and life-threatening diseases or cancers of the bladder, kidney, liver or intestines.
