Buttons and Bows. Sung by Bing Crosby in The Adventures of Ozzie and Harriet episode A Visit From Bing Crosby, broadcast December 5, 1948. Buttons and Bows was a popular song with music written by Jay Livingston and lyrics by Ray Evans. The song was published in 1947. The song was written for and appeared in the Bob Hope and Jane Russell film, The Paleface, and won the Academy Award for Best Original Song.
