Couples wishing to marry within the Roman Catholic Church may hold their wedding at San Carlos Borromeo de Carmelo Mission. See the Carmel Mission website for more information. Map and Directions. Carmel Mission is located at 3080 Rio Road, Carmel, California. From Route 101: Take Route 68 to Route 1 South. Turn right on Rio Road. Follow for 6/10 of a mile. Carmel Mission will be on your left. From Monterey and points north: Take Route 1 South to Rio Road. Turn right.
