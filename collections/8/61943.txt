The WWE made wrestling cool in the late 90's with their Attitude Era. Mick Foley, known as Mankind in those days, won his first WWF championship on an episode of Raw when the WWE was known as the World Wrestling Federation. Stone Cold Steve Austin would often clash with his devious boss Vince McMahon.ach Monday night, the superstars of WWE bring the action right into your living room. Airing live from various locations around the country, Raw is among one of the highest rated shows on cable television.
