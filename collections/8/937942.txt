Eyelash and Eyelid Demodex Symptoms. Rosacea and seborrheic dermatitis are by far the most common disorders affecting eyelids and ocular surface. Often they are benign and commonplace, however if you see symptoms of dry eye or (more rarely) ocular corneo-conjunctival inflammation then Demodex mite involvement is very common. Lash Mites (Demodex on Eyelashes, EyeBrows and Eyelids) is common condition among people suffering from Demodicosis.
