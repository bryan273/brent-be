June 29, 1948 - September 15, 2017 Jackie died on September 15th, 2017. She was the oldest child of Jack L. London, DVM and Merle C. London. Dr. London in early 1948 had accepted a position with the Mexican-American Commission to combat and eradicate hoof and mouth disease in Mexico before it could be spread across the border into the USA. After Jackie was born, she and her mother moved to Mexico to the city of Oaxaca.
