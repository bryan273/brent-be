The Field Artillery was also a dominant force in many of the Civil War battlefields. Leading artillerymen who became combined arms leaders included Joseph Hooker, Braxton Bragg, William T. Sherman, A.P. Hill, and Stonewall Jackson.uring WWI the Field Artillery became one of the most dominant forces in the trench warfare of France. It emerged from the war to end all wars as the greatest killer on the battlefield, responsible for 75% of all combat casualties.
