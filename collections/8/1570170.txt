Ovarian cancer symptoms. 1  Persistent abdominal bloating, indigestion or nausea. 2  Changes in appetite, often a loss of appetite or feeling full sooner. 3  Feelings of pressure in the pelvis or lower back. 4  Needing to urinate more frequently. 5  Changes in bowel movements. 6  Increased abdominal girth. 7  Feeling tired or low energy.
