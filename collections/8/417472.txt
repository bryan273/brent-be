Sudden onset pain on the outer side of the foot can be from a fracture of the 5th Metatarsal and injuries to this bone are a common sports injury. Causes of pain on the outside of the foot are listed here: 1  Midtarsal Joint Sprain A mid tarsal joint sprain is an injury to the ligaments holding the mid tarsal joint together causing pain in the outside middle of the foot. 2  Peroneus Brevis Tendon Injury The Peroneus Brevis tendon inserts into the 5th matatarsal bone on the outside of the foot.
