Causative organism and its life cycle. Pubic lice called Pthiris pubis are different from head louse. Adults are yellowish grey or tan and appear like crabs. Pubic lice have six legs front legs are very large and look like the pincher claws of a crab.Their second and third pairs of legs have claw like ends that helps them hold on to the coarse hairs tightly.
