Knee pain kneeling. Pain in or around the knee when kneeling can be caused by fat pad, bursa or tendon injuries. Conditions such, as housemaids knee where there is fluid or inflammation in and around the pre patellar bursa is common when kneeling. Kneeling pain can also be due to the position of the knee joint if there are issues on the knee surface, with greater flexion causing different areas to meet of the tibia and femur.
