Symptoms of Cellulitis. Cellulitis can appear on almost any part of the body. It usually shows up on damaged skin such as inflamed wounds, dirty cuts, and areas with poor circulation. It needs to be treated by a doctor. Common symptoms include: 1  Redness. 2  Red streaking. 3  Swelling. 4  Warmth. 5  Pain or tenderness. 6  Leaking of yellow, clear fluid or pus.
