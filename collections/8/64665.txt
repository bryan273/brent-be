Crabs or pubic lice, contrary to common belief, are not caused by only poor personal hygiene. They may be transmitted to a healthy and clean person through close contact with an infected person. The lice crawl from the hair of one person to the hair of another person.ubic lice have six legs front legs are very large and look like the pincher claws of a crab. Their second and third pairs of legs have claw like ends that helps them hold on to the coarse hairs tightly.
