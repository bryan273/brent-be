Bursae can become swollen and painful after injuries. Bursitis of the shoulder may cause pain to radiate to the chest. Sometimes the smaller bursae in the chest wall can become inflamed and cause pain. Heart attacks: Although the problem is the heart, heart attacks can cause referred pain to the shoulder or neck.
