Ankylosing spondylitis; Synonyms: Bekhterev's disease, Bechterew's disease, morbus Bechterew, Bekhterev-Strümpell-Marie disease, Marie's disease, Marie–Strümpell arthritis, Pierre–Marie's disease: A 6th-century skeleton showing fused vertebrae, a sign of severe ankylosing spondylitis. Specialty: Rheumatology: Symptoms: Back pain, joint stiffness
