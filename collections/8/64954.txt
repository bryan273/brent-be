Maduro also announced Saturday a series of measures, including visa requirements for U.S. citizens and the downsizing of the U.S. Embassy in Caracas, to counteract what he called U.S. interference in his country. Venezuela has in recent weeks accused the United States of being behind an alleged coup plot.
