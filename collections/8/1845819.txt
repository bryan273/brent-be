Dog flu outbreak: How to protect your pet. 1  Pet owners are worried about a dangerous strain of dog flu spreading through western states. 2  Symptoms The most common symptoms of dog flu are high fever, cough, runny nose, watery eyes, sore throat and loss of appetite.
