Chest X-rays are a common type of exam. A chest X-ray is often among the first procedures you'll undergo if your doctor suspects you have heart or lung disease. It can also be used to check how you are responding to treatment.A chest X-ray can reveal many things inside your body, including: 1  The condition of your lungs. Heart-related lung problems. 2  Chest X-rays can show changes or problems in your lungs that stem from heart problems. 3  For instance, fluid in your lungs (pulmonary edema) can be a result of congestive heart failure.
