St. George Island State Park. The St. George Island State Park occupies the far eastern end of St. George Island. There you will find nine miles of undeveloped shoreline, majestic dunes, a bay forest and salt marshes. St. George Island State Park has a series of hiking trails, boardwalks and observatioplatforms. In 2011, the St. George Island State Park ranked among the Top 10 Beaches of America. Birding is a popular activity on St. George Island.
