Kim Matula. Kimberly Marie Matula (professionally known as Kim Matula) (born August 23, 1988) is an American actress best known for her role as Hope Logan, the daughter of Ridge Forrester (Ronn Moss) and Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) but is biologically the daughter of Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) on the soap opera, The Bold and the Beautiful.
