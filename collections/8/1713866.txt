Here are ten more amazing foods that help reduce muscle pain and speed recovery so that you can hit it hard again and get more out of your body whether your goal is performance, muscle, or fat loss. #1: Eat blueberries, tart cherries, and other dark-colored fruit to reduce DOMS. Both blueberries and tart cherries have measured up in studies that found they reduce delayed onset muscle soreness (DOMS) and may help recuperate strength faster.
