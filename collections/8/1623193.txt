Pregnancy Symptoms 1 week before a missed period. Most women require at least a couple of weeks, to realize that they are pregnant. Many women do not even think about pregnancy, till they miss their period.ome of the early pregnancy symptoms to watch out for after a missed period are: 1  Cramps in the abdominal region. 2  Difficulty in sleeping. 3  Experiencing a metallic taste in the mouth. 4  Feeling dizzy. 5  Food cravings and changes in appetite. 6  Frequent urination. 7  Headaches. 8  Heartburn.
