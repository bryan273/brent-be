Common causes of mouth ulcers. The most common causes of mouth ulcers are related to disturbances of the mouth and include: 1  Bacterial infections. 2  Canker sores. 3  Dietary deficiencies, such as lack of vitamin B-12, zinc, folate (folic acid), or iron. 4  Emotional stress. 5  Food allergies. 6  Nutritional deficiency. 7  Spicy foods.
