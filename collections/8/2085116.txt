The major types of leukemia are: 1  Acute lymphocytic leukemia (ALL). This is the most common type of leukemia in young children. 2  Acute myelogenous leukemia (AML). AML is a common type of leukemia. 3  Chronic lymphocytic leukemia (CLL). With CLL, the most common chronic adult leukemia, you may feel well for years without needing treatment.
