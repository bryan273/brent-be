Acute pancreatitis signs and symptoms include: Upper abdominal pain; Abdominal pain that radiates to your back; Abdominal pain that feels worse after eating; Fever; Rapid pulse; Nausea; Vomiting; Tenderness when touching the abdomen; Chronic pancreatitis signs and symptoms include: Upper abdominal pain; Losing weight without trying; Oily, smelly stools (steatorrhea)
