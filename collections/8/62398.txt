EcoFoil reflective insulation rolls are easy to install. Just roll over whatever surface you want to insulate (including over or between joists) and staple or tape into place. It can even be rolled over fiberglass or cellulose insulation. Our insulation rolls are the toughest in the industry. Each 1,000 square foot insulation roll weights 28 pounds and is virtually impossible to tear.
