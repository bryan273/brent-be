According to the University of Maryland’s website, skin elasticity is better defined as skin turgor. And, skin turgor is, “the skin’s ability to change shape and return to normal (elasticity).”. Following are NINE practical and inexpensive ways to promote better skin turgor: 1  Avoid the sun and/or use a good sunscreen.2  Sun damage is well-known to wreak havoc upon the best of skin. Partaking in regular exercise can improve skin elasticity. 2  It is recommended to have 30 minutes of aerobic exercise to improve circulation for the skin. 3  This gives the benefit of feeding the skin and removing any waste products more effectively. 4  Hence, you will have a more youthful skin.
