Tomi Lahren is an American conservative political commentator, and former television host, currently working for Great America Alliance, an advocacy organization that supports Donald Trump. She is best known for hosting Tomi on TheBlaze. She previously hosted On Point with Tomi Lahren on One America News Network. In August 2017 Lahren joined Fox News as a contributor.
