Dogs are susceptible to a number of health problems, of which liver diseases are most frequent. Examining for elevated alkaline phosphatase in dogs is considered as a reliable approach for confirming liver and bone abnormalities.y elevated alkaline phosphatase, we mean that the amount of ALP is higher than the recommended levels. Similar to humans, elevated alkaline phosphatase in dogs can be related to certain health problems. The exact reasons for elevated alkaline phosphatase in pet dogs are not known as yet.
