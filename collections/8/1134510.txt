In June of 2013, researchers from Italy and the USA continued investigating cephalalgiaphobia in migraine patients. Cephalalgiaphobia is the fear of having a headache attack during a pain-free period that may induce patients to use analgesic in the absence of pain to prevent headache and to improve their performances.
