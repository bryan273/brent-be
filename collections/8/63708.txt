Wilson County, North Carolina (NC) County population in 2014: 81,401 (61% urban, 39% rural); it was 73,814 in 2000. County owner-occupied with a mortgage or a loan houses and condos in 2010: 12,998. County owner-occupied free and clear houses and condos in 2010: 6,199. County owner-occupied houses and condos in 2000: 17,512.
