Influenza (flu) and a cold. are both respiratory (breathing) system infections caused by viruses. Initial symptoms of flu and. colds are similar, and it can sometimes be difficult to tell if you have the flu or a very bad cold. The flu can cause more serious illness than a common cold. Influenza vaccine only protects. against certain influenza viruses and will not protect you from colds or other respiratory infections.
