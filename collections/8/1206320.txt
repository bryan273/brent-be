Lupus Nephritis. Lupus Nephritis is a kidney damage caused by Systemic Lupus Erythematosus (SLE). SLE means an immune system disorder which leads damages to skin, joints, brain and kidneys. The root cause of lupus nephritis is the underlying immune system disease. In normal condition, the immune system helps protect your body from virus or bacteria. However, in lupus patients, the immune system cannot tell the harmful substances from healthy cells.
