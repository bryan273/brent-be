Study Names Tukwila, Washington, As ‘Most Dangerous City In America’. Andrew Galbreath. Research of FBI crime statistics now say that Tukwila, Washington, is no longer the second-most dangerous city in America, but has now claimed the number one spot, according to a study released on Friday. Tukwila now tops the list of most dangerous cities in the United States, surpassing former champion Myrtle Beach, South Carolina.
