The Bunya Mountains are a distinctive set of peaks forming an isolated section of the Great Dividing Range in southern Queensland.The mountain range forms the northern edge of the Darling Downs near Bell and Dalby.The mountains are south of Kingaroy and just to the south west of Nanango.
