Following is a list of causes or underlying conditions (see also Misdiagnosis of underlying causes of Heart block) that could possibly cause Heart block includes: 1  Heart attack. 2  Coronary artery disease. 3  Sinus node disease. 4  Congenital heart defect. 5  Myocarditis. 6  Coronary thrombosis (see Heart symptoms) 7  Cardiomyopathy. 8  Heart valve disease.
