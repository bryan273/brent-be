Mission Carmel. Carmel Mission (San Carlos de Borromeo) ••• Mission San Carlos Borromeo de Carmelo. The Carmel Mission was the second one built in California, founded June 30, 1770 by Father Junipero Serra. The full name of Mission San Carlos de Borromeo de Carmelo is for Saint Charles Borromeo, the Bishop of Milan who died in 1538. If you're here because you want to visit the Carmel Mission, you may want to read up on its history first.
