Obama Sign Language Surprise : What Other Languages Does He Know? [VIDEO] At a 2008 campaign stop in Dayton, Ohio, Barack Obama said in front of a town hall meeting, I don't speak a foreign language. That statement may not have been entirely true; back in 1997, Obama told a reporter for the Chicago Daily Law Bulletin that he had picked up some Bahasa Indonesia while he lived on the Southeast Asian archipelago as a child.
