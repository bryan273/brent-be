Causes of Hypersomnia. Hypersomnia has many and varied causes, including sleep apnea, narcolepsy, excess weight, drug or alcohol abuse, brain tumor and certain medications. Following is a list of causes or underlying conditions (see also Misdiagnosis of underlying causes of Hypersomnia) that could possibly cause Hypersomnia includes: 1  Sleep deprivation. 2  Clinical depression.  Schizophrenia.
