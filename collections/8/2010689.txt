Here is a look at the most common causes of sore feet, based on region: 1  Heel: Plantar fasciitis, heel spurs, calluses, and dry, cracked heels. 2  Arch: Flat feet, pes planus or fallen arches, plantar warts, Metatarsalgia, pes cavus, and overpronation. 3  Toes: Broken toe, bunions, hallux rigidus, turf toe, and hallux limitus.
