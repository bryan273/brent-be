To: Barclays Bank PLC Firm Reference Number: 122702 Date: 20 May 2015 1. PROPOSED ACTION 1.1. For the reasons given in this Notice, the Authority hereby imposes to impose on Barclays Bank PLC (“Barclays”) a financial penalty of £284,432,000. 1.2. Barclays agreed to settle at an early stage of the Authority’s investigation. Barclays therefore qualified for a 20% (Stage 2) discount under the Authority’s executive settlement procedures.
