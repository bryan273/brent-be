Causes of Slow heart rate: The following medical conditions are some of the possible causes of Slow heart rate. There are likely to be other possible causes, so ask your doctor about your symptoms. Sinus bradycardia; Obstructive jaundice; Myxoedema; Anorexia nervosa; Hypothermia; Aortic stenosis; Sick sinus syndrome; Junctional rhythm; Atrioventricular block
