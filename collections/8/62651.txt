Zenith Zoysia is sold here by SEEDLAND. Zenith Zoysia grass is one of the few seeded zoysia varieties that is easily planted and grown on a well-prepared seedbed. Both Compadre and Zenith Zoysia makes a beautiful, dense, warm season turf. Once fully established this variety can perform well in high traffic areas. Varieties that can be grown from seed are Improved turf types; Zenith and Compadre varieties, and the common Zoysias; Including Panda and Traveler Brand. 2  Seeded varieties should ONLY be planted into prepared bare soil areas.
