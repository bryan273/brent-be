Foods to Avoid for High Cholesterol. 1  Limit saturated fats and oils, such as butter, bacon drippings, lard, palm oil, and coconut oil. 2  Instead, use soft tub margarine or vegetable oils, such as olive or canola oil.3  Avoid trans fats or partially hydrogenated vegetable oils. Avoid fast foods like hamburgers, fries, fried chicken, and tacos. 2  They are high in both total fat and saturated fat. 3  When you eat out, choose broiled sandwiches or chicken without skin, salads with low-fat dressing, and foods that aren't fried.
