Horizon NJ Health invites dental providers to join our dental network and help provide high-quality care to our members. If you are interested in becoming a Horizon NJ Health participating provider, call Scion Dental at 1-855-878-5368 or email providerservices@sciondental.com. Scion Dental is an independent company that provides Medicaid dental benefits administration services for Horizon NJ Health members.
