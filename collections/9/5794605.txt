Medical Definition of HYPERCHOLESTEROLEMIA. : the presence of excess cholesterol in the blood—see familial hypercholesterolemia. — hy·per·cho·les·ter·ol·emic \-tə-rə-ˈlē-mik\ or hy·per·cho·les-ter·emic \-tə-ˈrē-mik\ or chiefly British hy·per·cho·les·ter·ol·ae·mic or hy·per·cho·les·ter·ae·mic adjective.
