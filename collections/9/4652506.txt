“The Walking Dead” Season 7 premiere date was officially announced during the show’s San Diego Comic-Con panel on Friday. Fans in Hall H went wild when it was announced that the hit zombie apocalypse series will return on Oct. 23 at 9 p.m. on AMC. As in previous seasons, the show’s seventh season of 16 episodes will air in two parts, with the first eight episodes kicking off Oct. 23 and the final eight returning Feb. 2017.
