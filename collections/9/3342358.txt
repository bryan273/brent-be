However, some patients with status epilepticus may also require days or weeks of treatment. Pentobarbital coma is associated with a host of serious adverse effects. The latter is an important issue since the beneficial effects of the therapy may be negated by the hypotensive effect. The most common side effects of barbiturate coma or pentobarbital coma include respiratory depression and hypotension. In fact, many treated patients will need extra cardiovascular support including vasopressors and pulmonary artery catheter insertion to monitor cardiac function and volume status.
