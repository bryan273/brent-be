Influenza can be a serious illness. Symptoms include fever, chills, headache, body aches, sore throat, runny nose, dry cough and a general feeling of exhaustion and sickness. While the fever usually lasts two to five days, other symptoms can linger for 10 days or more.If you have the flu, stay home, rest and do not exercise.
