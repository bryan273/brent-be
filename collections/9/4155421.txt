Vitamins are important for the whole body, including the liver. Vitamins B, C and E all help maintain a healthy liver. Vitamin C and E both act as antioxidants. They lower the damage from free radicals, cleaning and healing the liver. Vitamin B acts as a decongestant and keeps the liver functioning properly. Foods that are high in Vitamin B, C and E are broccoli, carrots, peas, oatmeal, lemons, tomato juice, peppers, kiwi, oranges, turkey and tuna.
