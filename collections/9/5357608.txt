Tingling lips may develop as a sort of symptom before the allergies, imbalances in the blood chemistry, as well as various problems that may occur neurologically. It is a feeling of pins and needles in your lips. It becomes extremely vital to visit a physician, when symptoms such as numbness, heat, and tingling as well as other sensations occur which cause irritation in the lips and thus examination by a doctor becomes extremely important.
