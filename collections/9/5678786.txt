Interrupted blood supply to the parts of brainstem affects the functions of those parts seriously. Here is an overview of symptoms and effects of this brain stroke. Rupturing of a blood vessel, often due to hypertension, leads to hemorrhage in pons or other parts of the brainstem, resulting in a brain attack (stroke). This does not mean that all those who have hypertension will be victims of pontine stroke. Hypertension is one of the main risk factors for this stroke.
