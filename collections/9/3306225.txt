It's also possible that the problem is actually referred pain, when you have an injury elsewhere, and it's referred to the knee. Pain in the lower back or spine, or another part of the knee, can travel through nerves and be felt behind the knee. Less Common Causes of Behind-Knee Pain. -Popliteus tendonitis is an injury to or inflammation of a small muscle at the back of the knee called the popliteus. -Gastrocnemius tendonitis is an inflammation of a calf muscle tendon that originates at the back of the knee.
