3. Skip high-fat foods to help avoid discomfort. Eating the wrong things after gallbladder surgery can induce pain, bloating and diarrhea. To side-step this gastrointestinal discomfort, avoid eating high-fat or spicy foods, including: French fries and potato chips. High-fat meats, such as bologna, sausage and ground beef. High-fat dairy, such as cheese, ice cream and whole milk.
