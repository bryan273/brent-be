Azithromycin is most commonly used to treat the following infections: 1  Respiratory infections, such as bronchitis. 2  Ear infections (otitis media) 3  Sinus infections (sinusitis) 4  Pneumonia. Throat infections (tonsillitis/pharyngitis)  Skin infections, such as cellulitis, folliculitis, or 1  impetigo. Pelvic inflammatory disease  (PID).
