Your Child's Immunizations: Meningococcal Vaccines. The meningococcal vaccines protect against meningococcal disease, a serious bacterial infection that can lead to bacterial meningitis. Two kinds of meningococcal vaccines are currently given to kids in the United States. The meningococcal conjugate vaccine (MCV4) protects against four types of meningococcal bacteria (called types A, C, W, and Y). It is recommended for all kids.
