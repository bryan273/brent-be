The majority of vegetable oils on the market are comprised of healthy unsaturated fats, but a few still contain high amounts of saturated fats. Discovery Health reports that oils such as olive, canola, peanut, safflower and sunflower all contain unsaturated fats.ncluding these healthy fats as a part of a well-balanced diet can result in a lowering of cholesterol levels and help prevent heart disease, reports the MayoClinic.com. The body needs a moderate amount of fat to function properly, and the main source of fat should come from unsaturated fats.
