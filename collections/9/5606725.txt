Sinus Tachycardia and Sinus Bradycardia General idea with definition and causes and Treatment. Causes of Sinus Tachycardia. Fever, thyrotoxicosis, exercise, anaemia, lack of activity, hypertension etc. Drugs like Sorbitrate, .Nifedipine, nasal decongestants drops, atropine eye drops etc can cause tachycardia.
