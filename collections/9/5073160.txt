For many women who enter menopause naturally, their menstrual cycle first becomes irregular. On average, it stops completely within about 4 years. Other symptoms that can happen during this time include: 1  Mood swings. 2  Lower sex drive. 3  Hot flashes. 4  Sweating. 5  Racing heart. 6  Headaches. 7  Vaginal dryness and soreness. 8  Painful sex. 9  Trouble sleeping.
