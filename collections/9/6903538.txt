There are, however, some major differences between these two painful and debilitating conditions. It is helpful to have a good understanding of both conditions before the differences between the conditions can be fully understood. Fibromyalgia is a condition in which the main symptom is pain. Pain can be felt anywhere on the body and is typically experienced in more than one area, although there may be one area that is most painful. Most people with fibromyalgia also complain of fatigue.
