J-Lag Hooks and Eye-Lag Screws. J- Lags and Eye Lags are used primarily for tree care where a short run of cable is needed, but other applications include construction and trellising. You can use the combination of right and left hand thread j-lags to tension a cable, eliminating the need for turnbuckles. 1/2 x 3-1/4 Eye Lag Screw.
