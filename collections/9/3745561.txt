There are no particular foods that can cause tonsillitis directly; however some foods are likely to irritate the throat or aggravate symptoms of tonsillitis. These are spicy, oily, astringent food items.arm foods that do not irritate the throat may also be had. Broth, soups and warm water with honey provide relief from pain. A saltwater or a mint water gargle is a natural remedy for tonsillitis. Pure salt or mint leaves can be added to warm water and this mixture can be gargled and spat out.
