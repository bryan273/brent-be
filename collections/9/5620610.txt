Gastritis is an inflammation, irritation, or erosion of the lining of the stomach. It can occur suddenly (acute) or gradually (chronic).owever, the most common symptoms include: 1  Nausea or recurrent upset stomach. 2  Abdominal bloating. 3  Abdominal pain. 4  Vomiting. 5  Indigestion. 6  Burning or gnawing feeling in the stomach between meals or at night.
