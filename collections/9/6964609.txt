Avoiding these liver-clogging foods can help keep your liver healthy and functional. Sodium. Foods that are very high in sodium, like canned soup, tax your liver. A normal amount of sodium helps to regulate fluids in your body and blood pressure. But if you're prone to fluid retention, try to avoid high sodium foods as too much salt can create excess fluid in your liver, too. Other high sodium foods to watch out for include: 1  Deli Meat. 2  Cheese. 3  Pizza. 4  Soy Sauce.
