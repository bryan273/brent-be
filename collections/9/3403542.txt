Chest x rays show the structures in and around the chest. The test is used to look for and track conditions of the heart, lungs, bones, and chest cavity. For example, chest x-ray pictures may show signs of pneumonia, heart failure, lung cancer, lung tissue scarring, or sarcoidosis.Chest x rays do have limits.They only show conditions that change the size of tissues in the chest or how the tissues absorb radiation.
