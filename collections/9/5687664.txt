Burning Mouth Syndrome (BMS) is a hot feeling or sensation which can affect your tongue, lips, palate, or areas all over your mouth. BMS is sometimes called ‘glossodynia'.You get this feeling when there are changes in the way the nerves in your mouth send messages to your brain-for example, about taste and temperature.
