There is always bleeding after C-Section Surgery. It's referred to as Lochia Bleeding. In fact the amount of Lochia bleeding is about the same as it is after a vaginal birth. Blood flow is usually heavier immediately after your c-section and continues for about 4-5 days.Your bleeding will taper off and get lighter after about 10-12 days.
