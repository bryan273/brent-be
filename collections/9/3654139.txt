Signs Of Blocked Fallopian Tubes September 24, 2014 symptoms of blocked fallopian tubes Beula Dsouza A woman is termed to be having blocked fallopian tubes when there is an obstruction that is preventing the egg to travel down the fallopian pipes. Just like any other health condition, there are various signs of blocked fallopian tubes. These are: Unusual discharge. Abnormal discharge during the menstrual cycle maybe as a result of blocked fallopian tubes. The abnormality of the discharge can be detected by unusual color or smell.
