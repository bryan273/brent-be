In 1888, a man named Harry Seeley proposed a way of classifying dinosaurs into two groups by looking at their hip structure, these groups are called Saurischia (lizard hipped) and Ornithischia (bird hipped). The two groups can be further broken down into sub-groups such as families, sub-families and so on.et’s take a look at some of the interesting sub-groups and examples of the dinosaurs that are part of them. Saurischia (lizard hipped dinosaurs). Theropods-The name Theropod means ‘beast foot’ and this group includes all of the carnivorous (meat eating) dinosaurs.
