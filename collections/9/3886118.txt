Legendary singer, songwriter and producer Nick Ashford, husband and songwriting partner of Valerie Simpson, died at age 70 in New York City on Monday, August 22, 2011. According to The New York Times, Ashford had been undergoing treatment for throat cancer -- the exact cause of death is unknown at this time.
