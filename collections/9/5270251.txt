A tutorial on how to make a grass and realistic sky environment with Mental Ray in 3ds Max. Cool sun and halo effects. 3ds Max Grass Tutorial. First, you will create a Plane object, and then add a Noise modifier.On top of that apply a Hair and Fur modifier. This will be used to simulate realistic grass. We create the sun and the sky with the Daylight System. Add a Lens and Ring Effect for the sun and a cool realistic effect.ow to make a Grass Field and realistic Sky environment. Plane. Use Height: 100 and Width: 100. Then add a Noise modifier to the plane. Select the plane and go to Modify panel and select Noise. Use Seed: 2; Scale: 70 and on the strength, Z: 15. Now apply Hair and Fur modifier on it.
