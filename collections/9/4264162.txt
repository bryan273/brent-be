The virus of herpes simplex can cause potential danger to people with lowered immunity level. Causes : Lip blisters are caused by herpes simplex virus HSV-1. It may occur due to emotional stress or trauma on the lips or due to prolonged exposure to sunlight. Sometimes it may infect your eyes, fingers and even genitals. Symptoms : Fever, nausea, vomiting, headache are some of the common symptoms of lip blisters.
