Peritonitis is an inflammation of the peritoneum, the tissue that lines the inner wall of the abdomen and covers and supports most of your abdominal organs. Peritonitis is usually caused by infection from bacteria or fungi.t's linked to a higher risk of peritonitis due to accidental contamination of the peritoneum by way of the catheter.
