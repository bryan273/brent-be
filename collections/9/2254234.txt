Hives are the red, itchy bumps that show up due to a food or drug allergy. They normally go away after a few days. However, some cases of chronic hives can last for months or years. Until hives go away, you can relieve symptoms using antihistamines. Meanwhile, allergic contact dermatitis is the rash that results from you touching something you're allergic to.
