Children ages 12 months to 10 years have a normal pulse rate of 70 to 120 beats per minute. The typical pulse rate in children over 10, adults and senior citizens will be 60 to 100 beats per minute (lower for professional athletes, according to the NIH).ccording to the National Institutes of Health, the normal pulse rate of a newborn infant will be between 100 and 160 beats per minute.
