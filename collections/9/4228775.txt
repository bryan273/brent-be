According to the U.S. Department of Agriculture, certain nuts and seeds contain potassium, magnesium and calcium. Eating dried pumpkin seeds, almonds, cashews and pistachios boosts your intake of these minerals.airy products are a source of magnesium and potassium in addition to calcium.
