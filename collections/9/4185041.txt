Headaches are a common symptom of hypoglycemia, or low blood sugar. When glucose levels drop, your body sends a series of hormonal signals that trigger hunger; these hormones may also cause anxiety, mood swings, blurred vision and headache.he only way to treat low blood sugar is to eat, particularly simple carbohydrates that your body can quickly convert to glucose. The key to managing hypoglycemia is prevention -- making changes to your diet to stabilize blood sugar levels.
