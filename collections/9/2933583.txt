The fiber in sunflower seeds is an especially key nutrient for weight loss. According to a research review published in the journal “Nutrition” in 2005, people who eat the most fiber tend to have the lowest percentages of body fat and the lowest body weights. Fiber can also make you feel full and control hunger.unflower seeds are a triple threat. In addition to being tasty and good for you, they contain nutrients that can help you lose weight in a healthy way. Before you start shoveling down the seeds, though, it’s important to realize that no single food can successfully lead to weight loss.
