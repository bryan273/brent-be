Sinus Headaches. Barometric pressure changes may lead to sinus headaches. Damp and cold weather may intensity pain in your sinuses and barometric pressure changes may trigger sinus headaches if your sinus cavities are slow to equalize air pressure inside your sinuses.Symptoms of sinus headaches include pain in front part of your head and around your eyes.oping. MayoClinic.com recommends that you keep a headache journal to cope with headaches that may be caused by changes in barometric pressure.
