Elephants have the longest gestation period of all mammals. These gentle giants' pregnancies last for more than a year and a half. The average gestation period of an elephant is about 640 to 660 days, or roughly 95 weeks.By comparison, a human pregnancy lasts an average of 280 days, or 40 weeks.Female elephants live for 60 to 70 years, but only have about four offspring throughout their lifetime.
