Other causes of hair loss. Different types of hair loss may have different causes. Common causes of hair loss include: 1  Ringworm of the scalp, which is common in children. 2  Mental stress or physical stress, such as recent surgery, illness, or high fever. 3  You may have a lot of hair loss 1 to 3 months after severe physical or emotional stress. 4  Your hair usually will grow back within a few months.
