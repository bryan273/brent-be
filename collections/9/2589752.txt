Sometimes the pain is mild. But the pain may feel as though it bores through the abdomen to the back. Sitting up or leaning forward sometimes reduces the pain. Other symptoms of an attack of pancreatitis are: 1  Nausea and vomiting. 2  Fever. 3  Fast heart rate. 4  Sweating. 5  Yellowing of the skin or the whites of the eyes (jaundice). 6  Shock.
