Tropical Fruits. Fruits that are caressed by tropical breezes tend to have high sugar content such as pineapple, pomegranates, mangoes, bananas, and fresh figs. Papaya and guava are the least sugary of the tropical fare. Non-tropical fruits high in sugar are tangerines, cherries, and grapes.hen surveying the array of fruit at a market or the bowls of fruit on a buffet table, the following categories can help you remember whether a fruit is in the low, medium or high ballpark of sugar content (per 1/2 cup serving, according to the USDA). The categories are in order from lowest sugar content to highest.
