Chest heaviness or tightness, wheezing, coughing and difficulty breathing are symptoms of allergy-induced asthma. In many cases, daily medication can stave off this type of asthma attack, as can avoiding the substances that trigger the reaction.f you have seasonal allergies, you may take a decongestant to alleviate your symptoms of nasal congestion, headache and ear pain. Unfortunately, decongestants, particularly those containing pseudoephedrine, can cause chest heaviness, tightness and heart arrhythmias.
