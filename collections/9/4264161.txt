Blood blisters are formed due to injury, skin infections or damage to the surface of the skin. Chewing of hard food items can lead to blood blisters in the mouth. The following are the few causes of this condition. 1  Oral Ulcers: Usually, an ulcer is referred to as an open wound which is formed inside the mouth and this occurs due to the invasion of micro organisms. 2  Sometimes, blood blisters are caused due to the skin infections caused as a side effect of medications.
