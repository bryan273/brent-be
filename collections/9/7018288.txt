Bile reflux occurs when bile — a digestive liquid produced in your liver — backs up (refluxes) into your stomach and the tube that connects your mouth and stomach (esophagus).Bile reflux may accompany acid reflux, the medical term for the backwash of stomach acids into your esophagus.However, bile acid reflux and acid reflux are separate conditions.
