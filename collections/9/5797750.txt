Flushing Imaging Center is an ACR accredited diagnostic imaging facility located in downtown Flushing, New York. Established in 2003, Flushing Imaging Center offers a wide range of medical exams using MRI, CT, Ultrasound, Mammography, Bone Densitometry, and X-rays. We are an FDA accredited facility. Our staff speaks English, Chinese, Korean, and Spanish. As of 2015, we are now an ACR designated lung cancer screening center.
