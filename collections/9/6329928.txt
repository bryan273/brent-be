Pneumonia: Types list. The list of types of Pneumonia mentioned in various sources includes: 1  Viral pneumonia. 2  Influenza. 3  Influenza A. 4  Bacterial pneumonia. 5  Fungal pneumonia. 6  Chemical pneumonia-caused by inhaled fumes or chemicals. 7  Pneumococcal pneumonia-caused by Streptococcal pneumoniae (pneumococcus) bacteria.
