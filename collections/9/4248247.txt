If you have a mild case of the disease, you may not experience any symptoms. However as more damage occurs, you may begin to experience: 1  changes in appetite. 2  dry mouth. 3  weight loss. 4  nausea and vomiting. 5  pain or swelling in the abdomen. 6  yellowing of the skin or eyes (jaundice). 7  fever. 8  changes in your mental state, including confusion.
