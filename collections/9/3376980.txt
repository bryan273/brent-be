Common AIDS symptoms in men include: 1  swollen lymph glands. 2  unusual lesions on the tongue; skin rashes and bumps. 3  nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. 4  cough and shortness of breath. 5  chronic fatigue. 6  fever, chills, and night sweats. 7  rapid weight loss. 8  blurred or distorted vision. 9  other symptoms of infection or disease.
