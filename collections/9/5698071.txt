Dehydration is one of the most common causes of cloudy urine symptoms. When your body does not have adequate amounts of water, your urine may become dark and cloudy. 2. Urinary Tract Infections. UTIs are common causes of urine cloudiness, and these infections may require medical attention to cure. Male urinary tract infections are not as common but do occur. 3. Water Soluble Vitamins. Some vitamins are water soluble, and can cause cloudy urine.
