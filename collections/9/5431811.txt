Fibroids are benign, non-cancerous tumors that involve the uterus. Fibroids can be classified based on their location in the uterus. There are 3 types of fibroids based on this classification.1. Submucosal: along the lining of the uterus, 2. Intramural: in the muscular portion of the uterus, and 3.
