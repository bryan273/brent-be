Most eye floaters don't require treatment. Eye floaters can be frustrating, and adjusting to them can take time. However, you may eventually be able to ignore them or notice them less often. Treatments for floaters that impair your vision. If your eye floaters impair your vision, which happens rarely, you and your eye doctor may consider treatment. Options may include: Using a laser to disrupt the floaters. An ophthalmologist aims a special laser at the floaters in the vitreous, which may break them up and make them less noticeable.
