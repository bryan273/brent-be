Neomycin is a topical antibiotic ointment used to treat bacterial infections of the skin. Swelling of the throat is one of the first signs of an allergy to oral neomycin. Facial swelling may occur as a result of an allergic reaction. Neomycin may come in the form of an antibiotic eye drop. Side effects of neomycin may include difficulty urinating. Hives may occur as a result of a neomycin allergy.
