Medicine choices. 1  BronchodilatorsBronchodilators are used to open or relax your airways and help your shortness of breath. Short-acting bronchodilators ease your symptoms. 2  Phosphodiesterase-4 (PDE4) inhibitors are taken every day to help prevent COPD exacerbations. The only PDE4 inhibitor available is roflumilast (Daliresp).
