SYMPTOMS: Sleepwalking is most often initiated during deep sleep but may occur in the lighter sleep stages or NREM, usually within a few hours of falling asleep, and the sleepwalker may be partially aroused during the episode. In addition to walking during deep sleep, other symptoms of sleepwalking include: 1  Sleeptalking. 2  Little or no memory of the event. 3  Difficulty arousing the sleepwalker during an episode.
