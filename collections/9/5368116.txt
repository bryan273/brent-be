Along with the factors determined, different types of anesthesia medications are used depending on the medical procedure being done. While the patient is under level two of sedation by anesthesia, depending on the type and dose administered, they are in a state that is known as a “twilight” state, thus attaining the name twilight anesthesia. Drugs and technology used. The same drugs used in general anesthesia are also used for twilight anesthesia, except in smaller doses and in a bolus interval (A concentrated mass of a substance administered intravenously for diagnostic or therapeutic purposes).
