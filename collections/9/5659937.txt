Hexagon Calculator. Hexagon is a six sided polygon containing six interior angles each equal to 120 o and their sum is 720 o. All sides of the regular hexagon are equal. The perimeter of hexagon is the lengths of all its sides. Area is the total area of all 6 triangles contained int it. In circle radius is the radius of the largest circle that will fit in the polygon. The diagonal of hexagon is the length of line joining any two vertices.
