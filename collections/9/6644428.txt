Osteoarthritis Osteoarthritis happens when the cartilage in your joints breaks down causing pain, stiffness, and swelling. Cervical (neck) spondylosis Cervical spondylosis is arthritis of the neck pain and stiffness the neck, shoulder, arm, or chest. Torticollis Torticollis are spasms of the neck muscles causing neck pain and stiffness, tilted head, and more.
