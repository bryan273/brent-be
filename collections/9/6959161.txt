Think before you snack. Instead of reaching for the Doritos, pop some popcorn. Instead of having packaged cereal, make a batch of steel-cut oatmeal. “Oatmeal is a great breakfast for a breastfeeding mom who’s trying to lose weight. It keeps you full for a long period of time and keeps insulin levels stable,” Ritchie says. Starchy veggies, such as baked potatoes and sweet potatoes, can also satisfy your craving for carbs.
