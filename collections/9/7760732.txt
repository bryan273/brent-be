While the full-sized version of weeping cherry trees can grow from 25 to 40 feet tall, the dwarf version is much smaller.This makes it an ideal tree for many gardeners with a small amount of space.(noririn/iStock/Getty Images).warf Weeping Cherry. The true dwarf weeping cherry tree is a larger cultivar than the Snow Fountains or Carmine Jewel. At its maturity, it can grow to be around 15 feet tall. This type of tree can live for up to 70 years.
