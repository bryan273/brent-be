Tinnitus 1-2-3. Learn what causes that ringing in your ears, and how you can control it. When an excessive amount of ear wax builds up in the canals, it can cause a number of problems, including hearing loss and tinnitus, which are undeniably associated with one another. Earwax compaction is only of the most common and simple explanations as to why people hear a ringing or buzzing sound which has no external cause.
