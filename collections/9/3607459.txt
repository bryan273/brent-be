Some of the most common causes of swelling fingers include arthritis, fluid retention, and pregnancy. In addition, finger edema can be caused by cardiac problems, abnormal kidney function, and allergies.Before treatment can be implemented to reduce swelling, a thorough medical examination must take place.regnant women often experience swollen fingers and feet.
