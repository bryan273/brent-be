URL of this page: //medlineplus.gov/ency/article/003089.htm. Malaise is a general feeling of discomfort, illness, or lack of well-being. Malaise is a symptom that can occur with almost any health condition. It may start slowly or quickly, depending on the type of disease. Fatigue (feeling tired) occurs with malaise in many diseases. You can have a feeling of not having enough energy to do your usual activities.
