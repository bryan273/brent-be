Celine Dion performs at the Sinatra 100 - An All-Star Grammy Concert in Las Vegas, to be broadcast on Dec. 6, 2015 on CBS. Celine Dion has canceled her shows at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas in the wake of husband Rene Angelil's death. The singer has a residency show at Caesars, and she canceled her Saturday and Sunday performances after Angelil passed.
