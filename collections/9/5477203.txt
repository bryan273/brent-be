If you find that your eyes feel dry, uncomfortable and irritated or you feel like there is something in your eye all the time, then you should tell your GP, optometrist or ophthalmologist (eye specialist).bout dry eye. Dry eye often affects both eyes but sometimes one eye is affected more than the other. It can make you feel you have something in your eye, like an eyelash or a piece of grit, even when there is nothing there. Dry eye can make your vision slightly blurry, though usually only for short periods of time.
