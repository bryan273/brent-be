Venous Thromboembolism (VTE) Prophylaxis. 1  Surgery and trauma – major abdominal/pelvic, hip/knee replacements, TBI. 2  Lower limb problems – fractures, varicose veins. 3  Obstetrics – late pregnancy, C-section, puerperium. 4  Malignancy – abdominal/pelvic, advanced/metastatic. 5  Mobility – hospitalization, institutional care. 6  Miscellaneous – previous VTE.
