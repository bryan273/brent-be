Seropositive Rheumatoid Arthritis. Seropositive rheumatoid arthritis is nothing but the same rheumatoid arthritis, the blood test done for which shows presence of rheumatoid factor, or RF, in it.here is no definite cure for this disease and the treatment is mainly based on symptom relief. In seropositive rheumatoid arthritis, it can only be said that the patient definitely has the disease, which is not certain with the seronegative counterpart.
