Unlike many other traditional roof shingles and rolls, which are mainly composed of slate, wood, ceramic, asbestos, or bitumen-soaked paper, rubber roofing shingles are usually be made from recycled (vehicle) tires (a tremendous source of waste material), sawdust and slate dust.
