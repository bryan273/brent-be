Common symptoms of an allergic reaction include: 1  sneezing and an itchy, runny or blocked nose (allergic rhinitis) 2  itchy, red, watering eyes (conjunctivitis) 3  wheezing, chest tightness, shortness of breath and a cough.  a raised, itchy, red rash 1  (hives) swollen lips, tongue, eyes or face.  tummy pain, feeling sick, vomiting or diarrhoea.
