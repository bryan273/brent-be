Dust mites are tiny insects that are scientifically known as Dermatophagoides. These insects cause allergic reactions in susceptible humans, resulting in hay fever, asthma and other respiratory symptoms. Dust mite allergy is the most common cause of year-round allergy and asthma in the United States. Millions of Americans have dust mite allergy. Dust mites are found in most areas of the world. Symptoms of dust mite allergy in humans develop through breathing and touching waste products of the mites.
