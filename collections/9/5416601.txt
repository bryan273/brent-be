The Hollywood Reporter. 'Walking Dead' Casts Major Comics Villain Negan. Jeffrey Dean Morgan ('The Good Wife,' 'Watchmen') will take on the iconic role from Robert Kirkman's comic series. Following an extensive casting search, AMC's The Walking Dead has found its next major villain. Jeffrey Dean Morgan has been tapped to take on the iconic role of Negan, the F-bomb-dropping, dastardly character from Robert Kirkman's comic series, The Hollywood Reporter has learned.
