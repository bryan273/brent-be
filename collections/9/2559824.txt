Numbness And Tingling In Nose. Tingling, numbness, burning sensation is generally the symptom when your sensory nerve gets damaged due to a disease or an injury. Sensory nerve is the nerve that supplies the skin. In nasal tingling, there is sensation of pins and needles all over the nasal skin or on certain portion of the nose; it is also termed as paresthesia. In the beginning the symptoms may begin as paresthesia and later on there may be numbness all over the nose.
