Foods high in fiber include whole grains such as brown rice, barley, and quinoa, certain vegetables and fruits (especially dried fruits), flaxseeds, and legumes such as beans and lentils. Here's a look at the amount of fiber found in specific foods that may help with constipation: 1  navy beans (9.5 grams of fiber per ½ cup) 2  kidney beans (8.2 grams of fiber per ½ cup) 3  pinto beans (7.7 grams of fiber per ½ cup)  artichokes (6.5 grams per 1  artichoke) sweet potatoes (4.8 grams in one medium sweet potato)  pears (4.4 grams in one small pear)
