All-Star Game has added significance for three players from first-place Yankees. The American League's Brett Gardner, Mark Teixeira and Dellin Betances, of the New York Yankees, watch with children during the MLB All-Star Home Run Derby, Monday, July 13, 2015, in Cincinnati.
