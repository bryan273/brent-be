The most important vitamins for hair loss are the B group (B6, biotin, and folate for slowing loss, and pantothenic acid and niacin for promoting hair growth). You can get B vitamins through diet as well, by eating such foods as beans, peas, carrots, cauliflower, soybeans, bran, nuts, and eggs.he most important vitamins for hair loss are the B group (B6, biotin, and folate for slowing loss, and pantothenic acid and niacin for promoting hair growth).
