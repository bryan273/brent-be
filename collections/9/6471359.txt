Certified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNA's) administer anesthesia to patients undergoing surgery in both clinics and hospitals. Nurse Anesthetists require up to three years of advanced training, and students must be a registered nurse with a baccalaureate degree to enter most CRNA programs.ertified Registered Nurse Anesthetists (CRNA's) administer anesthesia to patients undergoing surgery in both clinics and hospitals.
