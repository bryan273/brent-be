What are the types of allergic rhinitis? The two categories of allergic rhinitis include: Seasonal - occurs particularly during pollen seasons. Seasonal allergic rhinitis does not usually develop until after 6 years of age. Perennial - occurs throughout the year. This type of allergic rhinitis is commonly seen in younger children.
