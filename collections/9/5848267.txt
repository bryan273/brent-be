Laryngitis is the condition in which your voice box, or larynx, suffers from inflammation. In laryngitis, the voice box is irritated, and your voice will become hoarse, or even disappear.Because of the inflammation, pain is sometimes associated with the condition. The acute type last for two or three weeks, at most.oice rest is the main step in its treatment. 1  Viral infection is the most common cause. 2  Bacterial infections such as diphtheria may, albeit rarely, cause acute laryngitis. 3  Sudden overuse of the voice like shouting, singing or taking a long lecture may cause fatigue and swelling of the vocal cords.
