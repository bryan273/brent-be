Ease Into Eating. 1  Feed an infant or child easily digested foods; the BRAT diet (bananas, rice, applesauce, and toast) is a good choice as soon as they can tolerate food. 2  For an adult, add semisolid and low-fiber foods gradually as diarrhea stops.
