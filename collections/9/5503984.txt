There are two different forms of vertigo: peripheral vertigo and central vertigo. According to the American Institute of Balance, peripheral vertigo is usually more severe than central vertigo (AIB). Peripheral vertigo is caused by a problem with the inner ear, which controls balance.Central vertigo refers to problems within the brain or brainstem.
