These rafts are USCG Approved for use on commercial vessels. They are constructed of polyurethane-coated nylon fabric for superior abrasion resistance and feature RF welded seams for increased strength and reliability. The canister comes complete with cradle and hydrostatic release. These Crewsaver United States Coast Guard / SOLAS Approved Life Rafts are specially hand packed units. These rafts are complete units that include either a SOLAS A (offshore passages) Pack or SOLAS B (coastal passages) Pack.
