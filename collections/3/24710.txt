Milk can stay in a separate freezer door (also part of a fridge) for 3-4 months. 6. Milk can stay in a deep freeze for 6 months. 7. Milk kept at room temp for less then 24 hours can be re-refrigerated but not re-frozen. 8. Once milk has been thawed from being frozen, you cannot refreeze (but you can refrigerate).
