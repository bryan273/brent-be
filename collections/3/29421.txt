Advertisements: A capitalist economy is a form of economic organisation in which means of production viz., land and capital are owned by private people. Capitalistic economy is also known as free market economy and Laissez faire economy. Features of Capitalist Economy: The main features of a capitalist economy are as follows: (a) Factors of production are owned by the individuals. (b) Every individual has freedom to start business of his own choice. (c) All economic activities are guided by the motive of profit.
