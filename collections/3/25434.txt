Teaching assistants were paid a mean $25,270 per year as of May 2011, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. The lowest paid 10 percent earned $17,090 per year or less, while the highest paid 10 percent received $36,560 or more.lmost 80 percent of all 1,214,090 teaching assistants worked in elementary and secondary schools as of May 2011, where they earned a mean $25,490 per year.
