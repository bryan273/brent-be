BIRMINGHAM, Ala. - UAB (University of Alabama at Birmingham) is looking for volunteers to help hospitalized elderly patients at meal time. The Spoons Program, directed by the UAB Center for Aging, will provide trained volunteers to assist older adult patients in UAB Hospital who need assistance or just companionship while eating. April 1, 2008.
