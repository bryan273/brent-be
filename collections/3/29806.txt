Kazyon and BiM stores in Cairo (source: Facebook, Trendtype) BiM is the market leader in its domestic grocery market, Turkey, with a share of over 7%. In Turkey the company has grown from its first store in 1995 to nearly 6,000 stores today. It has opened nearly one thousand stores in Turkey in the last eighteen months alone. From the moment it entered Egypt, BiM has been clear about its ambitions: it aims to open more than 100 new stores in a year. It currently operates 256 stores in Egypt. Kazyon, which is owned by Tawfeer for Food Products, has also been clear about its expansion plans. It opened almost 160 stores in its first two trading years.
