Early life and career [edit]. Colleen Hoover was born December 11, 1979 in Sulphur Springs, TX, to Vannoy Fite and Eddie Fennell. She grew up in Saltillo, TX, and graduated from Saltillo High School in 1998. In 2000, she married Heath Hoover, with whom she now has three sons and a pig named Sailor.oover published her first novel, Slammed, in January 2012. Since then, all of her full-length novels have become New York Times Best Sellers.
