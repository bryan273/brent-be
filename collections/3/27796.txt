The differences between the Fascist economies of Italy and Germany. There are many similarities, as well as important differences, between the fascism that emerged in Italy and Germany during the 1920s and 1930s. To be able to compare and contrast the two one needs to know more about the rise of Fascism in Italy. After the First World War, Italy suffered several crises. There were food shortages, mass-unemployment and homelessness. As the cost of living rose, violent strikes were held.
