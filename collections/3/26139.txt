These skirts can be different lengths and can be casual or worn at formal occasions. Cargo skirts, like cargo pants, have lots of pockets and loops. Mini skirts are often thigh length, while maxi-skirts fall to the ankles. Skirts can have all types of pleats, including box pleats, kick pleats, sunburst pleats and knife-edged pleats. Sarong skirts are based on garments worn by both men and women in Indonesia and Malaysia.
