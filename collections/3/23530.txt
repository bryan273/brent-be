Charles' blog. Sana Dati is a romantic drama film that was directed by Jerrold Tarog who is also the one who directed the famous movie Heneral Luna. Starring Lovie Poe as Andrea Gonzaga, Paulo Avelino as Dennis Cesario, TJ Trinidad as Robert Naval and Benjamin Alves as Andrew Cesario. The film is about a woman who stalls her big wedding when a guy with a camera shows up and reminds her of a love she once had.
