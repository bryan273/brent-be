Most spider bites cause only minor injury. A few spiders can be dangerous. In the United States, these include the black widow spider and the brown recluse spider. To take care of a spider bite: 1  Clean the wound. 2  Use mild soap and water and apply an antibiotic ointment. 3  Apply a cool compress.4  Use a cloth dampened with cold water or filled with ice. 5  This helps reduce pain and swelling. 6  If the bite is on an arm or leg, elevate it. 7  Use over-the-counter medications.ost spider bites cause only minor injury.
