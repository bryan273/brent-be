The Crow Wing County sheriff's office is asking for help locating an elderly woman who went missing near Brainerd, Minn. Tuesday afternoon. Evelyn Nelson, 82, of Brainerd was reportedly running some errands in the Brainerd or Baxter area around 12 p.m. and never returned home. She was last seen driving a white 2010 Toyota Venza with Texas license plates. Anyone with information is asked to call the Crow Wing County sheriff's office at 218-829-4749.
