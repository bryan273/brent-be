Nitika in Nautical Flags. Naming a child in the Native American culture is a sacred event only to be performed by the wisest in the group, the elders. Because of the level of ritual that it is given, Native American names are considered especially significant and powerful.itika is generally used as a girl's name. It consists of 6 letters and 3 syllables and is pronounced Ni-ti-ka. In the U.S. in 2014, it ranked 15883 in baby name popularity for girls with 6 occurrences. Less than 5 boys were given the name.
