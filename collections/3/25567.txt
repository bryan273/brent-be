If anemia is severe, symptoms may include: 1  Weakness, fatigue, or lack of stamina. 2  Shortness of breath during exercise. 3  Headache. Trouble concentrating. 4  Irritability. Dizziness.  Pale 1  skin. Craving substances that are not food (pica). In particular, a craving for ice can be a sign of iron deficiency  anemia.
