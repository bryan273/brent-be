Thrill-seekers should keep an eye on this ride, as it is set to be one of the tallest and fastest in the world. The Valravn is set to outdo every other coaster in the world for tallest dive, longest dive and number of inversions. The ride will be situated in Ceder Point, Ohio. The theme park is already advertising the coaster and it is possible to buy a pass for the ride, expected to be completed in 2016. 2.
