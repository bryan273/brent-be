URANIUM MINERALS. Web site for sale see main page. Shown below is a partial list of radioactive minerals which can be found in nature. Uraninite (combined UO2and UO3; 50-85 percent U308). Uraninite is a naturally occurring uranium oxide with cubic or octahedral crystal form. It has a specific gravity of 8-10.5 (iron = 7.85), a grayish-black color sometimes with a greenish cast and a hardness of 5-6, about the same as steel. Its streak3 is black.
