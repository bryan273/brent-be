Pancreas (hormonal-Endocrine) low blood sugar symptoms: 1  Dizziness when standing suddenly. 2  Sugar cravings. 3  Headaches that are relieved when sugar or alcohol are consumed. 4  Shakiness, jitteriness. 5  You wake up craving sweets in the night. 6  Tired, weak or shaky feeling if you miss a meal. 7  Need to drink coffee to get going in the morning.
