Simmons College's 110th Commencement Ceremonies was held on May 15, 2015 at the Blue Hills Bank Pavilion (formerly Bank of America Pavilion) . The Undergraduate Commencement was held at 9:30 a.m. The Graduate Commencement was held at 2:30 p.m. A separate Doctoral Hooding Ceremony was at 5:30 p.m. on Wednesday, May 13 on the Simmons College Academic Quad at 300 The Fenway.
