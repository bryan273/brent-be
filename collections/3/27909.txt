Average Salaries. The average annual salary for a union carpenter was $76,000 as of 2013, according to Careerbliss. This equates to $36.53 per hour, based on 40-hour workweeks.During apprenticeship training, a carpenter is typically paid 30 to 50 percent of the wages he'll earn once he completes his training, according to the BLS.nion carpenters usually earn more when their unions negotiate higher wages with companies, government agencies or the employers for which they're working. For example, in 2012, the New York City District Council of Carpenters Union negotiated a new hourly rate of $99.16 for their carpenters.
