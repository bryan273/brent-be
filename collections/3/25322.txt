So when, after Missy’s funeral, Karen asked to move in with Irene, a divorcée then living with two of her three sons, she welcomed her. Karen vowed to find the “animal” who had killed her friend, and she helped Irene try to track down clues. She lived with Irene for three months and remained close afterward, sitting up nights sharing memories of Missy. Irene felt she might not have survived Missy’s death if not for Karen.
