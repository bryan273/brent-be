In the 2015 - 2016 school year, full time students at Creighton were charged $36,422, prior to adjustments for financial need. Of this amount, the cost of tuition totaled $34,810. $1,612 was fees. Regrettably, this school does not offer any discounts to students who live in state. Creighton University fees and tuition is the number charged to students for one academic year, not including room and board. Bear in mind a lot of students obtain financial aid and scholarships which minimize the cost of college
