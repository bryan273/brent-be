Brain stem death is a clinical syndrome defined by the absence of reflexes with pathways through the brain stem-the “stalk” of the brain, which connects the spinal cord to the mid-brain, cerebellum and cerebral hemispheres-in a deeply comatose, ventilator-dependent patient.
