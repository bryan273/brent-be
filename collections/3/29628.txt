Katharine Hepburn was the previous record holder with 12 nominations, and she still holds the record for the most Oscar wins by an actress, with 4 Best Actress trophies. Jack Nicholson is Oscar's most honored male actor, currently with 12 nominations, and 3 Oscar wins, most recently in 1997 for AS GOOD AS IT GETS. Talk about a perennial Oscar favorite: Woody Allen, having only one acting nomination, has been nominated 19 times for either writing or directing.
