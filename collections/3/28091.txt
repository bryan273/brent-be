Some other more non-specific signs and symptoms of strep throat which can also be seen in strep throat due to other causes are: 1  sudden onset of sore throat, 2  headache, 3  odynophagia (painful swallowing), 4  nausea, vomiting, abdominal pain, 5  red, swollen soft palate (uvula), and/or. 6  rash (diffuse red patchy rash).
