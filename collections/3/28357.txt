A report from Kotaku reveals that a leaked image of Pokemon Sun and Moon is a possible confirmation that the game will come out this year. The Pokemon company finally confirmed that Pokemon Sun and Moon will come out for systems in the Nintendo 3DS family in holiday 2016. According to Neurogadget, the April issue of CoroCoro monthly manga magazine, which will be released on April 15, is rumored to reveal the first glimpse or details of the Pokemon game.
