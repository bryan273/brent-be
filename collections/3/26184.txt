N Terraria 6. There are presently 10 classes to choose from. You begin as a Terrarian. Once your class level is at Level 10, you are able to claim your Tier 1 Class. When you class level for your Tier 1 class becomes level 20(the max level of all tier 1 classes are 30), you are able to claim your Tier 2 class. Once you choose a class for that tier, you can not change it.
