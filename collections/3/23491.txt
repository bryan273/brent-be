Brown sausage; drain well. Preheat oven to 350°. In a bowl, beat eggs, then add onion, green peppers, salt, pepper and garlic powder. Stir in sausage and cheese. Spoon 1/3 cupfuls into greased muffin cups. Bake at 350° for 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean. Submit a Correction.
