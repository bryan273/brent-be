Hayden Panettiere and her longtime boyfriend Wladimir Klitschko have announced that they are engaged to be married. The “Nashville” star coyly broke the news on a Wednesday appearance on “Live with Kelly & Michael,” confirming that she and Klitschko plan to tie the knot, but have not yet set a date.
