Chambord's famous figures and intermittent residents. Francois I (1494-1547) King of France, ordered the chateau to be built. Caston d'Orleans (1608-1660) Louis XIII's brother, stayed at Chambord and Blois from 1634 to 1643 and 1652 to 1660. Louis XIV (1638-1715) King of France, stayed at Chambord nine times between 1660 and 1685.
