In 2012, Jaffri appeared in a lead role in Hum TV's program Aseerzadi. In 2013 she made her film debut in Humayun Saeed's production Main Hoon Shahid Afridi. Ainy Jaffri has also voiced the main character Jiya in the animated series, Burka Avenger. She is currently shooting for Balu Mahi with Osman Khalid Butt, a film by Haissam Hussain.
