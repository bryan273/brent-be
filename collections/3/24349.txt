Competitor Analysis: Kit Kat is owned by Nestlé SA which also owns a range of other product brands, e.g. baby food, bottled water, cereals, coffee, etc. In 2012, Nestlé’s company share in Singapore’s confectionary market was 9.4%. This has remained fairly constant since 2009, following a drop from 10.1% in 2007 to 9.6% in 2008.
