Icelandic courses click here. Mímir-símenntun is a centre for lifelong learning in Reykjavík, Iceland, the largest of its kind in the country. The centre was established in 2003 and is a private corporation, owned by the Icelandic Confederation of Labour (ASí). The Mímir-símenntun main office employs 22 people as well as 200 teachers and instructors who work on various projects on behalf of the centre.
