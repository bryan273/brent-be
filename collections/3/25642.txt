Urea is a raw material used in the manufacture of many important chemicals, such as: 1  Various plastics, especially the Urea-formaldehyde resins. 2  Various adhesives, such as Urea-formaldehyde or the urea-melamine-formaldehyde used in marine plywood. 3  Potassium cyanate, another industrial feedstock. 4  Urea nitrate, an explosive.
