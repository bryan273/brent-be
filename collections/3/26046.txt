Domain Expiration. On March 10th, 2012, the domain name Meatspin.com expired and the image was replaced with a parked domain advertisement. On March 12th, news of the site’s removal was picked up by The Daily What, the Daily Dot, Guyism, pop culture blog I Heart Chaos and the General Mayhem forums. By 12 a.m.pread. On March 10th, 2005, the single serving site Meatspin.com was launched, which hosted a larger version of the video clip paired with Dead or Alive’s 1984 synth-pop hit “You Spin Me Round.” On December 1st, the term “meatspin” was defined on Urban Dictionary.
