grid ratio a ratio comparing the height of lead lines in an x-ray grid to the distance between the lead strips. inspiratory-expiratory ratio the ratio of the inspiratory time to the expiratory time during the breathing cycle.ignal-to-noise ratio the ratio between the amplitude of a signal being measured and that of the noise. urea excretion ratio the ratio of the amount of urea in the urine excreted in one hour to the amount in 100 ml of blood. The normal ratio is 50.
