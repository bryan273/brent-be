Say your 2015 gross income was low enough that you’re not required to file Form 1040 for last year. Great, but it may be a good idea to file anyway. Here’s why. * You may be due a federal income tax refund for 2016, for example, because of the refundable earned-income tax credit or the refundable child tax credit. No 2016 return means no refund. * Until your 2016 return is filed, the three-year statute of limitations period for the commencement of an IRS audit won’t start.
