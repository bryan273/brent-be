For the CollegeHumor editor, writer, and actor named Jeff Rubin, see CollegeHumor. Jeff Rubin (born August 24, 1954) is a Canadian economist and author. He is a former chief economist at CIBC World Markets. Rubin had worked at CIBC World Markets and its predecessors since 1988, and served as chief economist from 1992 to 2009, when he resigned. 1 Education and early career.
