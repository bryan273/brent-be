(February 2015) Silver Spoons is an American sitcom that aired on NBC from September 25, 1982, to May 11, 1986, and in first-run syndication from September 15, 1986, to March 4, 1987. The series was produced by Embassy Television for the first four seasons, until Embassy Communications moved the series to syndication.
