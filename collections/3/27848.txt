Live Gold Spot Price. This live chart shows the current spot price for an ounce of gold. Gold spot prices are derived from 24-hour trading around the world. Gold trading in the spot market is active Sun 6 PM thru Fri 5:00 PM EST, with a 1-hour break at 5:00 PM daily. Gold spot prices are used by bullion dealers to set prices for physical gold. For opportunities to buy gold on price dips, buyers continuously watch gold spot prices.
