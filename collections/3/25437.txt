Employers. Most assistant teachers work in elementary and secondary schools and earn an average salary of $24,500 a year, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Others work in child day care services, for an average of $20,480, or in colleges, universities and professional schools, for an average of $28,840.he Bureau of Labor Statistics identifies Alaska as the state with the highest average salary for assistant teachers, at $35,290 a year. California is next with an average salary of $29,840, followed by Rhode Island at $29,280.
