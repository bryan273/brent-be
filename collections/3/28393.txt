Liver Enzymes. AST – (aspartate aminotransferase), which was previously called SGOT. This enzyme can also be elevated in heart and muscular diseases and is not liver specific. Normal range of AST is 5-45 U/L. ALT – (alanine aminotransferase), which was previously called SGPT and is more specific for liver damage. Normal range of ALT is 5-45 U/L.
