Features. Smart numbers are highly configurable and can contain organizational, temporal, or factual data that relates to the procurement document. You can define smart numbering schemes by process or by transaction type.If you define a smart numbering scheme for the process of creating procurement documents, a new serial number is generated with each newly created document.his function enables the generation of smart numbers (formerly called “long procurement numbers”) as identifiers for SAP Supplier Relationship Management (SAP SRM), Procurement for Public Sector (PPS) procurement documents.
