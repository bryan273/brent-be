As Burger King rose in popularity, restaurants began to open across the United States. In 1963, Burger King opened its first international restaurant in Puerto Rico. In 1975, drive-thru service began, and by 1977, Burger King had opened 2,000 restaurants on three continents. Today, the Burger King system operates more than 11,300 restaurants in 50 states and 69 countries. Approximately 90 percent of Burger King Restaurants are owned and operated by independent franchisees.
