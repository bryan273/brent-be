If the reason that you are lifting your home is a damaged foundation, it may be due to poor soil or ground movement. If you're not transporting your home, then you'll need to secure your new foundation with helical or concrete piers. This cost is also dictated by the size and weight of the structure. Helical piers are cheaper than concrete, and this option is a much more affordable choice than transporting the home to a new location. Raising your home's foundation comes with what are known as hidden costs..
