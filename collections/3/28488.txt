Although the term armlet may be technically similar, it is taken to mean an item that sits on the upper shoulder: an arm ring. The origin of the term 'bracelet' is from the Greek 'brachile' meaning 'of the arm', via the Old French 'bracel'.A bracelet is also a small brace or bracer (an arm-guard used by archers).n Greece a similar tradition, weaving a bracelet from red and white string on the first day of March and wearing it till the end of summer, is called Martis and is considered to help protect the wearer's skin from the strong Greek sun.
