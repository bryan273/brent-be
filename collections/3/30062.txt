You don't have to have the bank withhold the estimated taxes on the withdrawals; you can pay estimated taxes yourself each quarter, Slott said. To the question about the best way to withdraw the money, Slott said the tax bite can be minimized, and it hangs on the exact age of you or your wife, whoever is the holder of the IRA. In the year the IRA holder turns 70 1/2 , the holder has until April of the following year to begin making required minimum distributions from the account.
