New Spanish speakers often confuse the words muy and mucho. The two words are not synonyms. One means “very” and the other means “a lot”. This Spanish lesson gives a quick and easy explanation of when to use muy and when to use mucho. When to Use Muy and When to Use Mucho. Muy means “very” and comes before an adverb or adjective. Esta revista es muy interesante. This magazine is very interesting. Tu casa es muy grande. Your house is very big.
