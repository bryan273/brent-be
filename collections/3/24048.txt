prostacyclin (ˌprɒstəˈsaɪklɪn) n (Biochemistry) biochem any of several prostaglandins produced in the blood vessels, causing vasodilation and inhibiting platelet aggregation. pros•ta•cy•clin (ˌprɒs təˈsaɪ klɪn) n. a prostaglandin, C 20 H 32 O 5, that specifically inhibits the formation of blood clots.
