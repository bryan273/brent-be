Wages, Salaries, Tips. In general, employers must provide every employee a W-2 to be issued by January 31. The W-2 reports the amount of wages, contributions, social security, U.S. or Massachusetts retirement and Medicare tax, as well as amounts withheld for state and federal income taxes.edicare tax paid is in Box 6 and should be added to amounts paid for social security in box 4 and/or U.S. or Massachusetts retirement as a deduction. State income tax withheld in Box 17 is the amount that employers have withheld from wages.
