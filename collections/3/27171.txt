According to the Robert Half 2013 Salary Guide, the average salary for a cost accountant with up to one year of experience with a large company ranges from $43,000 to $53,750. The salary of a cost accountant with the title of manager for a large company ranges from $79,250 to $108,500. Cost accountant salaries vary depending on an individual's experience and education, and the size and industry of an employer. Job Outlook: Projected to grow 16% between 2010 and 2020, according to the BLS.
