Ecuador population density Ecuador population density is 59.2 people per square kilometer (153.3/mi 2) as of January 2018. Density of population is calculated as permanently settled population of Ecuador divided by total area of the country. Total area is the sum of land and water areas within international boundaries and coastlines of Ecuador. The total area of Ecuador is 283,560 km 2 (109,483 mi 2) according to the United Nations Statistics Division .
