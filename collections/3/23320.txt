Symptoms of a blocked bile duct include: 1  Yellowing of the skin (jaundice) or eyes (icterus), from the buildup of a waste product called bilirubin. 2  Itching (not limited to one area; may be worse at night or in warm weather) 3  Light brown urine. 4  Fatigue. 5  Weight loss. 6  Fever or night sweats.
