By Fireman Yasmine Muhammad, Naval Base San Diego Public Affairs SAN DIEGO, Calif. (NNS) -- Naval Base San Diego (NBSD) Personnel Support Det. (PSD) received a grade of 'excellent' during an unannounced inspection by the Commander, U.S. Pacific Fleet Fleet and Industrial Supply Center, June 13-16. Inspectors examined PSD's personnel and fiscal expertise in core pay and personnel operations.
