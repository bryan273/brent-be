Most of the medicines in this book that need to be injected should go into the muscle. IM injections can be given in a large muscle in the buttock, upper arm, or thigh. It is best to use the buttock or thigh instead of the arm if: 1  the amount to inject is more than 2 ml (2 cc). ( 2 But you should never inject more than 3 ml (3 cc) in a single dose. 3  Use 2 injections instead.). 4  the medicine is likely to cause pain when injected.
