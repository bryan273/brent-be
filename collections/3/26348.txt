How Much Will Tuition & Fees Be for Creighton? In the 2016 - 2017 school year, full time students at Creighton were charged $37,606, prior to adjustments for financial need. Of this amount, the cost of tuition totaled $35,942. $1,664 was fees. Regrettably, this school does not offer any discounts to students who live in state. Creighton University fees and tuition is the number charged to students for one academic year, not including room and board.
