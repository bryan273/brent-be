1 How to Identify Common Bites and Stings Many insects and spiders bite or sting, and while the bites of common bugs like mosquitoes, mites, and fleas are itchy and uncomfortable, they're usually otherwise harmless.But some bites and stings, like those from fire ants, wasps, hornets, and bees, may cause intense pain or even an allergic reaction. You may notice itching, hives, and swelling around an injury or sore, or a rash of small, red bumps that may or may not bleed. 2  Flea bites are more common on your ankles, in your armpits, around your waist, and in the bends of your knees and elbows.
