As you might already be aware, Facebook (which is the most used social networking website in the world today) now supports emoticons and smileys following the introduction of its live chat feature. Without further ado, here is a complete list of facebook emoticons and their keyboard shortcuts. Here is how to use them: type in the code and press enter to get it to show up. Each emote may have more than one shortcut, click on the read more link next to each icon to find all the codes and learn more about the meaning of the smiley. Note that some of the shortcuts are case-sensitive.
