Place bay leaves & cloves of garlic inside lamb. Roll up the leg & secure it with string (cotton type). Place leg in a basting tin, brush with the oil & place in an oven preheated to 230C or 450F & after 15 min reduce temp to 190C or 375F. Cook for a further 1&1/4 hours or until meat is tender.lace leg in a basting tin, brush with the oil & place in an oven preheated to 230C or 450F & after 15 min reduce temp to 190C or 375F.
