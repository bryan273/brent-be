ClaryCalm ® PRODUCT DESCRIPTION ClaryCalm is a proprietary blend of essential oils that provide soothing and calming effects during a woman’s menstrual cycle. A topical blend of Clary Sage, Lavender, Bergamot, Roman Chamomile, Cedarwood, Ylang Ylang, Geranium, Fennel, Carrot Seed, Palmarosa, and Vitex, ClaryCalm provides a cooling effect to the skin and helps balance emotions. USES • During your menstrual cycle, apply to abdomen to experience a soothing and calming massage. • During moments of heat, apply to the skin for a cooling, soothing effect. • Diffuse for a calming aroma that helps soothe heightened emotions.
