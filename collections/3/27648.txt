Needlepoint is not synonymous with all types of embroidery. Needlepoint is often referred to as tapestry in the United Kingdom and sometimes as canvaswork.. Needlepoint differs from true tapestry, which is woven on a vertical loom. Needlepoint is stitched on canvas mesh. When worked on fine weave canvas in Tent Stitch it is also known as Petitpoint. Needlepoint lace is also an older term for needle lace, a historic lacemaking technique.
