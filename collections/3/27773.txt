Seven of the ten shipping. companies interviewed indicated that break bulk. cargo plays a significant role in their business (see. to Table 2). For some carriers imports of break. bulk cargoes accounts for approximately 95% to. 100% of the total cargo volume discharged at. Australian ports. Exports range from 40% of the. total cargo volume loaded by some carriers, to. 100% for others. For two major carriers alone, their total inward and outward break bulk. carryings reached more than 1 million revenue. tonnes in 2008/2009.
