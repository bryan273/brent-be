4501:2-1-06 Motor vehicle equipment standards for tires. All tires shall have a minimum of one sixteenth of one inch depth of tread on the road surface. All tires shall be free of major bumps or bulges, breaks, thrown tread or any condition which would constitute an unsafe condition. Five Year Review (FYR) Dates: 03/30/2016 and 03/30/2021. Promulgated Under: 119.03.
