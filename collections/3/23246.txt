Fast track tickets are available from the thorpe park website or you can buy them at the gates when you get to thorpe park. *NOTE* The fast track tickets are subject to availability that's why it's better to reserve them online. The ticket price online is considerably cheaper than the tickets on the gates.est Answer: The following fast track tickets can be purchased inside the large entrance dome at the park; £9 - Adrenaline Rush fast track; includes Colossus, Nemesis Inferno, X:/ No Way Out and a choice between Tidal Wave or Loggers Leap. £9 - Extreme fast track; includes Stealth, Rush, Samurai and Zodiac.
