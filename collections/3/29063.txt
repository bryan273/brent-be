Emphysema is a condition that forms part of chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and involves the enlargement of the air sacs in the lung. 2-4. The alveoli at the end of the bronchioles of the lung become enlarged because of the breakdown of their walls.ther symptoms may be experienced, especially in more advanced lung disease: 4,6,8,10. 1  Frequent lung infections. 2  Producing a lot of mucus (phlegm or sputum). 3  Wheezing. 4  Reduced appetite. 5  Weight loss. 6  Fatigue. 7  Blueness of the lips or fingernail beds (from cyanosis caused by poor respiration).
