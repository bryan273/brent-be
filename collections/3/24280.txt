Wash and dry fillets. Spread thin coat of mayonnaise over salmon. Spray Pam or similar product over Pyrex or tin foil pan or rectangular pan (depending on amount and size of portions). Sprinkle seasonings over fish ending with paprika. Bake in 375F. oven for 10 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily. Note: This recipe is wonderful used for boned and skinned chicken.
