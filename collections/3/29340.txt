Threekingham (sometimes Threckingham) is a village in the North Kesteven district of Lincolnshire, England. It is situated on the A52 Grantham to Boston road, 6 miles (10 km) south from Sleaford, and close to the A15 Threekingham Bar roundabout.Mareham Lane, the Roman Road aligned with King Street, crosses the A15 at Threekingham.he Medieval Stow Fair was held nearby, less than 1 mile (1.6 km) to the south. It is possible that it was the site of the early medieval nunnery founded in the late 7th century by Saint Werburh, dedicated to Saint Æthelthryth, æthelthryth and probably destroyed by The danes. c. 870
