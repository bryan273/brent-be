Otoplasty in India. The affordable plastic surgery prices in India without compromising on the quality of care or services is a major driving factor for Westerners to choose to get their plastic surgery in India. Otoplasty is a very popular cosmetic surgery procedure as the cost of ear plastic surgery in India is quite affordable. Otoplasty or Pinnaplasty is a common cosmetic procedure among ear reconstruction plastic surgeries where the pinna or external ear is brought closer to the head with the removal of cartilage.
