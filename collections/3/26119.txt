1. Prepare a film of blood or bone marrow on a microscope slide and allow to air dry. 2. Prepare three containers (e.g. coplin jars, or staining dishes). Fill one container with One Step Wrights Stain and the second and third containers with distilled or deionized water.lace slide on staining rack. 3. Apply sufficient One Step Wrights Stain may be applied to the slide using dropper bottles or pipettes. 4. Wait 15-30 seconds and add an equal volume of distilled or deionized water.
