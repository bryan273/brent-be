Appraisal Information for Comanche County. Welcome to the Comanche County section of OkAssessor. We are a privately owned company that has written the Appraisal Assistant program to provide easy appraisal data access for Appraisers, Real Estate Agents, Banks and anyone else needing this data. We host our own servers on a dedicated T3 line that is supported by users of this site. We purchase the county data, and pay the salaries for all of our employees by charging a subscription to access this online program. If you wish to subscribe to the Appraisal Assistant please check out the Subscriber page.
