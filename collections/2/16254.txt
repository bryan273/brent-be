To use the Path tool do the following: Click the Path bubble from the Prezi menu. Three smaller bubbles — 1-2-3 Add, Capture View, and Delete All — open. To set up your path, click the 1-2-3-Add bubble. To start the path, click on the text or graphic you want to show first. It might be the title you created.lick the Path bubble from the Prezi menu.
