The largest part of the brain is the cerebrum. The cerebrum is divided into two halves called the left and right cerebral hemispheres. The left and right cerebral hemispheres have very different functions.he Temporal Lobes-The temporal lobes have many important functions. The temporal lobes can be divided into two parts. One part is on the bottom (ventral) of the hemispheres, and the other part is on the side (lateral) of each hemisphere.
