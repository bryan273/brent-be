To delete just the frame, click the frame to bring up the Transformation Tool. Click on the frame icon and select Remove frame. To delete a frame and its contents, select it and click Delete from the options that appear on the Transformation Tool. You can also right-click the frame and select Delete from the menu.If you accidentally delete a frame, you can undo it using the “Undo” button on the left side of the top toolbar.
