Southwest Airlines Check-in. Passengers can check in online, at check-in desks, at the departure gate or at self-service kiosks. Check-in times vary by airport, but Southwest suggests arriving between 60 and 90 minutes before your flight is due to depart. Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes one hour beforehand. Carry-on: One item plus one personal item which includes a purse, briefcase, laptop, or diaper bag.
