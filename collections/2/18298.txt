Tipping [edit]. Tipping in restaurants is optional. Waiters earn wages in Portugal and a 'tip' is considered a note of appreciation, not a means to make up for a tiny salary-if you are not too happy with the service, don't tip.ortugal is a member of the Schengen Agreement.
