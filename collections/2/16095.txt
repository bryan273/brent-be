An enterprise WAN is a corporate network that connects geographically dispersed users areas that could be anywhere in the world.WAN services are typically delivered to smart phones and other handheld devices sold by cellular service providers and their retail partners but other mobile devices can use them as well. Some netbooks are available with WWAN cards installed; you can also purchase wireless WAN cards to install yourself.
