Fees, Fees and More Fees. If you don't have an estate plan or have failed to completely fund your Revocable Living Trust, then your loved ones will be faced with probating some or all of your assets. The overall cost of probate will vary depending on the type and value of the property that's being probated. In general, the greater the value of the probate property, the more probate will cost.
