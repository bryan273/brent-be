pro·drome (prō′drōm′) n. An early symptom indicating the onset of an attack or a disease. [French, from Latin prodromus, precursor, from Greek prodromos, precursor : pro-, forward; see pro-2 + dromos, running.] pro·dro′mal (-drō′məl), pro·drom′ic (-drŏm′ĭk) adj.
