Half runner beans are a type of green bean that is thinner than most other green beans but are still packed with flavor. These beans can be eaten fresh, dried, frozen or canned. The bean plants are a cross between a pole bean and bush bean plant.ush beans plants are shorter and wider. Half runners grow in a fashion that is between these two types. The bean runners of this plant can grow to around 3 feet long, but they tend to grow wider, more like a bush.
