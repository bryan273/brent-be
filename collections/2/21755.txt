puff. 1  a short, quick blast, as of wind or breath. 2  an abrupt emission of air, smoke, vapor, etc. 3  an act of inhaling and exhaling, as on a cigarette or pipe; whiff. 4  the sound of an abrupt emission of air, vapor, etc. 5  a small quantity of vapor, smoke, etc., emitted at one blast. 6  an inflated or distended part of a thing; swelling; protuberance.
