Chinstrap Penguin. This is one of the three species of penguins classified as brush tail. This species of penguin has a black band that is very narrow under the head. Emperor Penguin. The largest of all penguins in the world is the Emperor Penguin. They tend to get plenty of attention too due to their size. Erect-Crested Penguin. The name of the Erect-Crest Penguin stems from the fact that they have yellow plumes above the eyes that are erect.
