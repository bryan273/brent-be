How to cage a pet in WoW. 1: Right click the pet in your inventory to add it to your pet journal. 2: Open up your pet journal and click “Put in Cage” and it will appear in your inventory in a cage. 3: Sell your newly caged pet.n my server these pets sell for 2000 – 20000g + and this is on top of all the other items you would get from farming Molten Core or any other zone in the first place.
