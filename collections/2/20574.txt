Part A: First let’s explain how to configure Ranger extender via WPS button: 1. Plug your Range Extender into a power outlet near your main Router/AP. 2. Push the WPS button on your main Router/AP first, then immediately push the WPS button on your Range Extender.Note1: After you have pushed WPS button of your Router/AP, you need to push WPS button of your Range Extender within 2 minutes.. At last, you can put your Range Extender in a proper place where you want to extend the wireless network’s coverage, and enjoy the extended wireless network.
