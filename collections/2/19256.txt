1 kelvin is equal to 1 degree Celsius, or 1.8 degree Fahrenheit. Note that rounding errors may occur, so always check the results. Use this page to learn how to convert between degrees Celsius and degrees Fahrenheit.he degree Celsius (C) is a unit of temperature named after the Swedish astronomer Anders Celsius (1701 1744), who first proposed a similar system in 1742. The Celsius temperature scale was designed so that the freezing point of water is 0 degrees, and the boiling point is 100 degrees at standard atmospheric pressure.
