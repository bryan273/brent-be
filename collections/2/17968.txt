Important Safety Information. 1  QVAR does not replace quick-relief inhalers for sudden symptoms. 2  Do not use QVAR if you are allergic to beclomethasone dipropionate or any of the ingredients in QVAR. 3  Do not use QVAR more often than it is prescribed. 4  QVAR may cause serious side effects, including: 1  Fungal infections (thrush). Tell your healthcare provider if you have any redness or white-colored patches in your mouth or throat. Rinse your mouth after using QVAR to help prevent an infection in your mouth or throat. 2  Worsening asthma or sudden asthma attacks.
