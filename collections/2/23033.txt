You might be surprised to find out their meaning. According to this post on Snopes, leaving coins on top of military headstones is a tradition in which soldiers outside of the deceased’s family visits the soldier’s grave to pay their respects. penny is just a simple sign that the grave was visited and respects were paid. A nickel means that the visitor spent time with the deceased at boot camp. If you see a dime, it means that the visitor served with the soldier at some point in time.
