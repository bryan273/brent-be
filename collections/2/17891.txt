Welcome to the world of percents! In order to complete practice exams dealing with percentages, you need to select a starting level that you want to begin with. For example, you may choose to select “Level 1 as your primary starting point within the world of percentages. You press the button below to select the level and then you click “start test.”. You then have to complete the test with at least an 80% to go onto the next level. If you stop at a specific level and then decide to take a break, you will begin at this level the next time you decide to practice percentages on this site.
