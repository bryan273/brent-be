NBA to Raise Rim Height to 12 Feet Next Season April 01, 2013 00:31. Today the NBA announced that, as of next season, they will be making a huge alteration to the game of basketball, and changing basketball history. They have announced that they will be raising the height of the an NBA basketball rim from 10 feet, up to 12 feet. The news is already spreading via journalists and bloggers, the majority of which seem to be opposed to the idea. Left: 10 foot rim. Right: 12 foot rim.
