The red comes from the tinge of the new growth. This Holly produces lovely large dark green leaves and abundant berries. It is cold hardy and does best when planted in full sun to partial shade. Red Robin Holly can reach a mature size of 14 feet high by 8 feet wide. Ilex X attenuata 'Fosterii' Foster Holly. This larger variety is excellent as a specimen tree reaching a height of 20 feet and a width of 10 feet.
