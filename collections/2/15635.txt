The 7-08, in most cases, with reduced recoil loads, kicks less than a .243 does.. Even with the reduced recoil loads, Sarotte said it is an effective cartridge on deer out to 200 yards. Since they came out with that reduced recoil ammo, the 7-08 and .308 have both out sold the .243, Sarotte said. Ballistics on a 7-08 full load are almost as good as a .270..
