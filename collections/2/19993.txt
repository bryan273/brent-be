How is a Hamburger Digested? If you have ever wondered how the food you eat digests then you have come to the right place :) How a Hamburger is Digested By: Kiana Mcknight To begin, the process starts in your mouth. Your teeth chew the food which then stimulates the exocrine glands. The exocrine glands release digestive enzymes such as salivary amylase (which helps break down carbohydrates). Chewing causes the release of saliva!!
