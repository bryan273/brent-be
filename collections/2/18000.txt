Phagocytosis of spermatozoa by epithelial cells of the female reproductive tract has been described in various vertebrate taxa, including mammals (Eisenbach, 2003 and Suarez and Pacey, 2006), birds (Koyanagi and Nishiyama, 1981), amphibians (Sever and Brunette, 1993) and reptiles (Sever and Hopkins, 2004).
