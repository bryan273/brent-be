As well as discovering what Guernsey has to offer, the island is the gateway to its neighbouring Bailiwick islands of Alderney, Sark and Herm, as well as Jersey and the Normandy and Brittany coastlines of France. On a clear day, flying to Guernsey can offer spectacular views across the Bailiwick.here are also direct flights from Dinard in Brittany and the option to fly via Jersey from other European destinations. There is one ferry company operating to Guernsey from the UK and the French port of St Malo. During the summer months there is a passenger-only service from Normandy.
