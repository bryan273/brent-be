View gallery. Three generations of Elvis Presley' s family turned out to join a host of dazzling stars at the Mad Max: Fury Road premiere in Hollywood last night. Lisa Marie Presley, 47, and Priscilla Presley, 69, were supporting Riley Keough, 25, who stars in the thriller, and all three stunned as they arrived together. Priscilla was married to Elvis from 1967 to 1973, before he died in 1977, while Lisa is their daughter and Riley their granddaughter.
