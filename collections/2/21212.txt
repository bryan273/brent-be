CLE Requirements. Rule X including professionalism requirement. Rule X of the Supreme Court Rules for the Government of the Bar of Ohio was amended, effective Jan. 1, 2014. Active attorneys and attorneys registered for corporate status must complete and report 24 hours of accredited CLE activities every two years.
