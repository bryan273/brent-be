In an ideal project monitoring setup, your project would record the planned progress and the actual progress in every control period. Planned progress is presented by the Schedule % Complete field. Actual progress is presented by Performance % Complete, which is the subject of this article. Performance % Complete is depended on many settings and intermediate values, including Activity % Complete, which itself depends on either Duration % Complete, Units % Complete, or Physical % Complete.
