Tampa Family Health Centers (TFHC) is a not-for-profit company dedicated to providing high quality, accessible medical care to under served communities of Hillsborough County. With fourteen conveniently located health centers to serve patients, TFHC provides a full range of comprehensive health services, including family practice, internal medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics/gynecology, family planning, Dental, pharmacy and behavioral health. Our fees are assessed on a sliding scale based on a patient’s income. No patients will be turned away because of inability to pay for services. TFHC provides preventive health maintenance, disease management and education to our patients: • Family Practice • Internal Medicine • Pediatrics • Obstetrics/Gynecology • Family Planning •Dental • Pharmacy
