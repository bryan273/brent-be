Parkinson’s disease is much less common than essential tremor.It does cause shaking of the hands but, unlike essential tremor, the shakingis worse when you are resting and not using the hand. The shaking in Parkinson’s disease is called ‘pill rolling’ because it is like rolling a small pill between your thumb and the side of your index finger.
