A wide area network (WAN) is a telecommunications network or computer network that extends over a large geographical distance.Wide area networks often are established with leased telecommunication circuits.s of 2014, households are connected to the Internet with ADSL, Cable, Wimax, 4G or fiber at speeds ranging from 1 Mbit/s to 1 Gbit/s and the connections in the core of a WAN can range from 1 Gbit/s to 100 Gbit/s.
