A: Glucophage (metformin) is used to treat type 2 diabetes. Glucophage (metformin) helps control blood sugar in a number of ways. These include helping the body respond better to the insulin it makes naturally, decreasing the amount of sugar the liver makes, and decreasing the amount of sugar the intestines absorb.: Metformin is an oral diabetes medicine that helps control blood glucose (sugar) levels. It is used to treat type 2 (non-insulin-dependent) diabetes, either alone or in combination with insulin or other diabetes medications.
