It spans most of the states of New South Wales, Victoria, and the Australian Capital Territory, and parts of the states of Queensland (lower third) and South Australia (southeastern corner). The basin is 3,375 kilometres (2,097 mi) in length, with the Murray River being 2,530 km (1,570 mi) long.otal water flow in the Murray–Darling basin 1885 to the present has averaged around 24,000 gigalitres (24 km3; 19,000,000 acre·ft) per year. This is the lowest rate of the world's major river systems.
