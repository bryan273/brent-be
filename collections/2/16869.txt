1 pound of fresh blueberries is usually between about 2 and 3 cups of berries. It takes about 4 cups (about of blueberries to make a blueberry pie (see this fantastic and easy blueberry pie recipe) A normal batch of blueberry preserves, jam or jelly requires 5 pints of berries. Blueberries do come in a variety of sizes from small (190-250 berries per cup) to extra large (<90 berries per cup).
