Three Poorest U.S. Counties In South Dakota Pine Ridge, SD The latest U.S. Census shows the three most poverty-stricken counties in the nation are in South Dakota, west of the Missouri River. Ziebach, Todd and Shannon Counties are all home to Indian Reservations. Ziebach County is the poorest in the nation with just more than 50 percent of the people living in poverty. Todd and Shannon Counties are not far behind, with slightly more than 49 and 47 percent poverty, respectively.
