It's All Over Now, Baby Blue  is a song written and performed by Bob Dylan and featured on his Bringing It All Back Home album, released on March 22, 1965 by Columbia Records (see 1965 in music).he Byrds ' recording of It's All Over Now, Baby Blue first saw release on October 29, 1969 as part of the band's Ballad of Easy Rider album. The song also appeared on the B-side of the band's December 1969 single,  Jesus Is Just Alright , which reached #97 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
