Installing the extender with WPS (Wi-Fi Protected Setup). If your existing wireless network supports WPS, follow the steps below if you want to setup your extender using the WPS button. 1  Press the WPS button of the Extender.2  Within 2 minutes, press the WPS button on your wireless router, gateway, or access point.ote: If the 2.4 GHz Link Rate LED does not light up, repeat steps 1 and 2, or make sure your router supports WPS. 1  If your router supports the 5 GHz band, just repeat steps 1 and 2 to connect the Extender to the 5 GHz band. 2  If successful, the 5 GHz Link Rate LED on your Extender should light up.
