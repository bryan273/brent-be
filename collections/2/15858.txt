On October 3, 2008, Congress passed the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act, which requires brokers to report adjusted cost basis for taxable accounts to the IRS and taxpayers via Form 1099-B starting with tax year 2011.. Not all cost basis methods listed will be available for every fund. The following cost basis methods may be available to you: 1  First In First Out (FIFO) A standing order to sell the oldest shares in the account first. 2  Last In First Out (LIFO) A standing order to sell the newest shares in an account first.
