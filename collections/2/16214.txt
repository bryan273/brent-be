Jane Inglis (nee Andrews) was the daughter of Harmon Andrews and Mrs. Andrews, the sister of Billy, Gracie and Ralph, the wife of Mr. Inglis and one of Anne Shirley's friends and classmates. Jane was born in 1865 in Avonlea, Prince Edward Island, to Harmon Andrews and his wife. She was raised along with her older brother Billy and her best friend Ruby Gillis. Years later, her younger siblings, Gracie and Ralph, were born.
