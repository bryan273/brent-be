The Byrds' August 1965 version of It's All Over Now, Baby Blue has never been released. Guitarist and band leader, Roger McGuinn, returned to the composition during a July 22, 1969 recording session for the band's Ballad of Easy Rider album.he Byrds ' recording of It's All Over Now, Baby Blue first saw release on October 29, 1969 as part of the band's Ballad of Easy Rider album. The song also appeared on the B-side of the band's December 1969 single,  Jesus Is Just Alright , which reached #97 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.
