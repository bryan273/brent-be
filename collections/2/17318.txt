The sinuses are cavities, or air-filled pockets, near the nasal passage. Like the nasal passage, the sinuses are lined with mucous membranes. There are four different types of sinuses: 1  Ethmoid sinus. 2  Located inside the face, around the area of the bridge of the nose. 3  This sinus is present at birth, and continues to grow. 4  Maxillary sinus.
