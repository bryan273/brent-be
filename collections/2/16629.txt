111th Congress, Vote 165; House of Representatives #3590. Affordable Care Act (Obamacare) Official Title: An act entitled The Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act. HR 3590: Patient Protection and Affordable Care Act. Passed House March 21, 2010, 219-212. Synopsis: This was originally a health reform bill created by the Senate. The negotiations that led to it were essentially between the White House and Democrats in the House and Senate, since Republicans were unanimous in opposing the bill.
