Zoning establishes the types of land uses permitted on a parcel of land within the full or limited. purpose jurisdiction of the City of Austin. Zoning also sets the development standards for a site, such as building height, setbacks, floor-to-area ratio, neighborhood compatibility, screening, landscaping, and impervious cover limitations. The purpose of land use regulations such as zoning is to create compatible land uses, ensure. proper design and construction standards, and promote the overall public good.
