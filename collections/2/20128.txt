Health care costs are a big concern for people going into retirement, but the costs of long-term care can still be a shock. Here are a few facts: • 70% of people over 65 will need some form of long-term care at some point. • For married couples, the chance that one spouse will need long-term care rises to 91%, says Byron Udell, CEO and founder of AccuQuote.com. • People living alone are more likely to need some sort of home health care.
