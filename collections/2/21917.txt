Akimichi Chouza. 1  A Konoha jounin, a member of the Akimichi clan, and the father of Chouji, Chouza is a brave, wise man that instills great confidence in his allies. In a fight, Chouza uses his size to become a nuisance to his enemies while granting safety to his team. Requirement to unlock: Mission: A Proud Father.
