You'd expect that certain sea animals have a longer lifespan than the ones on earth, including the humans, but the fact that a certain sea creature has discovered the secret of immortality is bound to take anyone by surprise. So sit back and check out our top 8 list of longest living sea creatures. 8. Galapagos Tortoise - 177 years. A symbol of longevity in certain cultures, the tortoise is a creature well known to all as a slow, yet persistent animal. The longest living Galapagos tortoise reached an impressive figure of 177 years. This specie in general can grow up to 5.9 feet (1.8 m) in length with a weight of around 880 pounds (400 kg).
