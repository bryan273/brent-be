But beware: When a Yo-kai enters your life, things will never be quite the same.. 1  The Animated Adaptation of the popular Yo-kai Watch video game series.The series is about an eleven year old boy named Nate who finds a capsule machine while looking for bugs in the woods. When he opens a capsule from the machine he releases a Yo-kai named Whisper, who decides to become his butler out of gratitude. He later gives Nate the titular Yo-Kai Watch. The Yo-kai Watch enables Nate to see other Yo-kai by shining a light on them.
