For those individuals with multiple high levels of the inflammatory markers listed here, consultation with a rheumatologist is strongly recommended. Nonsystemic vasculitic neuropathy has a better prognosis than systemic vasculitic neuropathy. The former may have normal laboratory results, while systemic vasculitis often features elevated antinuclear antibody (ANA) titers, erythrocyte sedimentation rate (ESR), and other more specific markers of disease.
