How to Move an Upright Piano. An upright piano can weigh anywhere between 300 and 800 pounds, sometimes more. Moving anything of this size requires several strong people, but it also requires that you take your time and be careful so as not to damage the piano.ove the piano to the side of the truck, with the back side of it facing the inside wall of the truck. If any damage occurs due to scraping against the wall, it will likely be confined to the back of the piano where it will not be seen.
