Otis Blue/Otis Redding Sings Soul, or simply Otis Blue, released September 15, 1965 on Stax Records, is the third studio album by soul singer Otis Redding.hree of the eleven songs were written by Redding:  Ole Man Trouble ,  Respect , and  I've Been Loving You Too Long . Three songs were written by Sam Cooke, a soul musician who had died a few months earlier.
