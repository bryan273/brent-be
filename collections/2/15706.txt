How to Grow Onions-Sowing Maincrop Onion Seeds. Sow maincrop onion seeds as soon as your soil allows you to-late February to early April for an August or September crop. Sow the onion seeds very thinly into drills 13mm(1/2) deep, in rows 23cm(9) apart. Carefully cover the onion seed with soil and gently water in.
