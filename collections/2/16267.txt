Design and development. Two new types were introduced in Pokémon Gold and Silver – namely the Dark and Steel types – which were intended to better balance the gameplay of Pokémon battles. Dark and Steel types fare well against Psychic attacks, which was a dominant type in Red and Blue. The concept of breeding was introduced in the second generation of Pokémon games as well, which allows players to manipulate the skills of their Pokémon to a greater degree.
