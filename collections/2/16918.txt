June 8, 1866 - The Senate passed the 14th Amendment by a vote of 33 to 11. June 13, 1866 - The House of Representatives passed the 14th Amendment by a vote of 120 to 32. June 16, 1866 - The text of the 14th Amendment can be found in the United States Statutes at Large, volume 14, page 358 (14 Stat. 358).
