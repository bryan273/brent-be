This map shows the major streams and rivers of Louisiana and some of the larger lakes. Louisiana is within the Gulf of Mexico Watershed. Most drainage leaves the state through the Mississippi River or flows into Lake Pontchartrain or directly into the Gulf of Mexico through smaller streams.
