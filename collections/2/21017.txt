A Few Key Characteristics of Implicit Biases. 1  Implicit biases are pervasive. 2  Everyone possesses them, even people with avowed commitments to impartiality such as judges. 3  Implicit and explicit biases are related but distinct mental constructs. 4  They are not mutually exclusive and may even reinforce each other.mplicit biases, explicit biases, and structural forces are often mutually reinforcing. The Kirwan Institute is committed to raising awareness of the distressing impacts of implicit racial bias and exposing the ways in which this phenomenon creates and reinforces racialized barriers to opportunity.
