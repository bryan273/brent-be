How To No comments. ^.*YOUR STRING.*$. You can easily remove the whole row or line within a txt file that contains a repetitive string or text. In order to do this you will need an advanced editor like notepad++ , a free open source application.You click on search and replace then use the regular expression search, not normal search, and use this query:^.*YOUR STRING.*$.
