Samsung Wireless Monochrome Printer (SL-M2020W/XAA... Pros: Users can print from their smart device with the Samsung SL-M2020W/XAA, printing is fast and of good quality. Toner is not much more expensive than inkjet cartridges and lasts longer. Cons: Users have reported difficulties with set up and unhelpful tech support from Samsung.
