Coloring with Recolor is easy and truly a relaxing experience. Just tap to color an area, tap again to undo. To pick a color select one from one of the ready made palettes or long hold over already colored area. Your work is never lost and you can safely leave the app and continue your work again later.oloring with Recolor is easy and truly a relaxing experience. Just tap to color an area, tap again to undo.
