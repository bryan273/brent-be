Zmodo DVR's are small recorders that store video for long periods of time depending on the size of the DVR's hard drive. Most Zmodo DVR's will support up to 2TB hard drive and the standard size on mostly all of them is 500GB. On a 4 camera Zmodo System, recording time will be 14 days with a 500GB drive. This is based on setting the Zmodo DVR to 24/7 recording time.
