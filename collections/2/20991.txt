Your Bird Flu FAQs. WebMD has been in touch with the CDC, the World Health Organization, and infectious disease experts to answer your bird flu questions. What Is Bird Flu? Bird flu, or avian influenza, is a viral infection spread from bird to bird. Currently, a particularly deadly strain of bird flu -- H5N1 -- continues to spread among poultry in Egypt and in certain parts of Asia. Technically, H5N1 is a highly pathogenic avian influenza (HPAI) virus. It's deadly to most birds.
