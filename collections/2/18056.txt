Dog Vitamins and Supplements: Get the Facts. WebMD teams up with veterinarians to talk about vitamin safety, dangers, and what to look for. Many people take dietary supplements or vitamins. And increasingly, they’re likely to give them to their pets, too. As many as a third of U.S. dogs and cats may receive vitamins or supplements. The most common are multivitamins, supplements to support arthritic joints, and fatty acids to reduce shedding and improve a coat’s shine, according to a 2006 study published in the Journal of the American Veterinary Medical Association.
