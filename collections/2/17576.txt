Good Weather – Most of the Time. The climate in Las Vegas is a subtropical arid climate. There are about 300 sunny days a year and 4.2 inches of rain, which on average occurs on 29 days per year.The summer months are very hot and mostly dry with average daytime highs of 94 to 104 °F (34 to 40 °C).ccording to a November 22, 2010 Las Vegas Sun (newspaper) report about a study conduced by the CQ Press, Las Vegas is nation’s 76th most dangerous city out of 400 U.S. cities with a populations of at least 75,000. The good news is that this is down nine spots from last year.
