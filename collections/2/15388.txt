Hawaiian Gardens is a city in Los Angeles County, California, United States. It is the smallest city in the county and was incorporated on April 9, 1964. The population was 14,254 at the 2010 census, down from 14,779 at the 2000 census. The town's unusual name originated in a 1920s refreshment stand that was decorated with palm fronds and bamboo.
