You can get Logitech Options software from the MX Master Download page. Customize the mouse buttons. To change the specific function or task assigned to a mouse button: Launch Logitech Options: Start > Programs > Logitech > Logitech Options. If you have more than one device that uses Logitech Options, click on the Wireless Mouse MX Master image in Logitech Options to start configuring it. In the upper left of the window, click on the Mouse tab and then click on the button you would like to customize: Gesture button.
