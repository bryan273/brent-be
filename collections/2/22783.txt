Courtesy/IMDB. According to Soap Opera Digest, actor Sean Carrigan has been cast in the recurring role of Stitch on The Young and the Restless! Stitch will be an old Army buddy of Dylan McAvoy played by Steve Burton. Carrigan will first air on the Friday, June 28th episode of the CBS soap. Some of Carrigan’s television credits include guests spots on Grey’s Anatomy, Hart of Dixie, and NCIS: Los Angeles, and a role in the series, The Flip Side!
