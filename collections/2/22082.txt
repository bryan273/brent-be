Governor Jonathan Belcher (1682-1757), who was very proud of the family name and had researched the matter, believed that Belcher was the most correct and honorable spelling of the name. Therefore, the surname (family name) of Belcher has its roots in medieval England, and, before that, in France.he Meaning of the Belcher Name. Bel * cher (Good * Cheer). The family name of Belcher is a historic one in England and America. There are many thousands of individuals named Belcher throughout the world.
