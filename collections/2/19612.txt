Origins. The red lion of Reyne confronts the golden lion of Lannister. The Rains of Castamere immortalized the destruction of House Reyne by Tywin Lannister. House Reyne was obliterated after they rebelled against their liege lord, Tytos Lannister, who was perceived as weak by his own vassals.To restore Lannister dominance, Tytos's son, Tywin, marched against the upstart Lord Reyne.eason 3. Thoros of Myr sings the Rains of Castamere as he crosses the Riverlands alongside Anguy and other members of the Brotherhood Without Banners.
