The cost of labor operates within a wide range for this type of repair. The general range is between $100 and $350, but there are some vehicles which cost significantly more for this repair. For parts, you will pay anywhere between $40 and $150 for oil pan gasket replacement. The part itself is not the expensive part of this repair, as you can see, but the labor can often be intensive. Some cars simply take a lot longer to get to the faulty part and remove it because of what other components are in the way and how troublesome it can be to remove them.
