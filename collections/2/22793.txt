There are different types of plant species, which are found on planet earth. They are sorted and classified into a separate kingdom known as Kingdom plantae. This classification is based on their similarities and differences. The Kingdom plantae is also called as kingdom Metaphyta. The Kingdom plantae includes all types of eukaryotic, multicellular, photosynthetic plants found in this biosphere. Most of the organism in this kingdom is autotrophs, which synthesis their own food with the help of solar energy.
