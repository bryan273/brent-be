The first television play. in the world. 'The Man With A Flower In His Mouth', by Pirandello. Produced by Lance Sieveking in July 1930. The purpose of the BBC Tuning Signal was to give the television set time to warm up, and for the viewer to adjust all of the controls in order to get a reasonable picture. The first televised Test match from The Oval in 1938. England v. Australia.
