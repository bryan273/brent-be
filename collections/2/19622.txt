Big, Bigger, and Biggest Waves What s the biggest wave ever recorded? Surfersvillage Global Surf News, 27 December, 2009 : - - The biggest wave on record occurred in Lituya Bay on the southern coast of Alaska in 1958. An earthquake measuring 8.3 on the Richter scale hit the area and shook loose an estimated 40 million cubic yards of dirt and glacier from a mountainside at the head of the bay.
