A coin left on a headstone or at the grave site is meant as a message to the deceased soldier's family that someone else has visited the grave to pay respect. Leaving a penny at the grave means simply that you visited.he tradition of leaving coins on the headstones of military men and women can be traced to as far back as the Roman Empire.
