Europe covers about 10,180,000 square kilometers, which accounts for about 2% of the surface of the earth, and a 6.8% of the total land area on the earth. This makes it the second smallest continent as per the surface area. Russia is the largest country in Europe in terms of the population as well as the area. It takes up around 40% of the total area of the continent, even though it has got territory in the continent of Asia also. The Vatican City is the smallest country in Europe. After Asia and Africa, Europe is the third most populated continent. It accounts for about 11% of the total population of the world. Euro is the common currency of the countries in Europe.
