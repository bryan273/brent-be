Flood Insurance Zone Map Symbols. Mortgage Lenders Will Likely Require Flood Insurance In These Zones. Zone VE - Coastal Flood Zone with Velocity Hazard (wave action), FEMA determines Base Flood Elevation. Zone AE - FEMA determines Base Flood Elevation. Zone A - County determines Base Flood Elevation. Flood Insurance Not Mandatory In These Zones. Zone X - Areas of 0.2% Annual Chance Flood Hazard. Zone X - Outside the Special Flood Hazard Area (SFHA). Floodway. Channel of a stream plus any adjacent floodplain areas that must be kept free of encroachment.
