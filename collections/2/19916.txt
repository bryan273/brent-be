Class Summary Edit. Clerics are the primary healing class in Aion and they utilize powerful healing spells and dispelling magic to keep their allies standing through even in the worst of conditions.lay Style Edit. The Cleric is the main healing class of Aion and is practically a requirement for many areas of the game. The Cleric is the only class that can actively dispel both physical and mental debuffs and is the main class for single target and AoE healing capabilities.
