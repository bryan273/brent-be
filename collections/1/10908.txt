Jenny /jen-ny/ [2 sylls.] as a girls' name is pronounced JEN-ee. It is of English origin. Originally a pet form of Jean in the Middle Ages; now used as a short form of Jennifer. Also used for blends and compound names. Dancer Jennita Russo.enny reached its top rank of #133 in the U.S. in the 1970s, and is currently at #822. (Top 1000 Birth Names Records, 2014).
