Required Information. The person requesting a certified copy of a birth record must provide: 1. A completed and signed request form. 2. A certified check or money order made payable to Vital Records Services. 3.A photocopy of your valid photo ID, such as. 1  Driver's License.2  State Issued ID Card. 3  Employee ID Card. Request a birth certificate in person at the state Vital Records Office or the County Vital Records Office where the birth occurred. 2  Obtain a birth certificate online at ROVER (Request Official Vital Event Records).
