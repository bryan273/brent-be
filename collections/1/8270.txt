Delt Heat grills feature an 18 gauge 304 stainless steel burner with a ten (10) year limited warranty, but offers a fifteen (15) year limited warranty on the stainless steel grates and fabricated stainless steel housing. Delta Heat Built-In Grills and Freestanding Grills come in 26″, 32″ and 38″ sizes. Delta Heat also offers a single and double side burner, drop-in coolers, sinks, and an outdoor refrigerator.
