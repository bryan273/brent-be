• Normally, serum lipase values range from 0 - 160 U/L. • Gastric lipase is an acidic, water-soluble enzyme, which takes active part in breaking complex food molecules into simpler ones (lipids) to ease the process of digestion. • It is secreted by the chief cells in the fundic mucosa (a dome-like subdiaphragmatic portion of the stomach) and has a pH optimum of 3 - 6.
