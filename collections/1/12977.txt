Lordsburg is an Amtrak train station located at Center Street and East Motel Drive, on the south side of the tracks, in Lordsburg, New Mexico, United States. The station building has been demolished, and there is no shelter or platform for passengers. The station is a flag stop, served only when passengers have tickets to and from the station. ^ Amtrak Fact Sheet, FY2015, State of New Mexico. (PDF).
