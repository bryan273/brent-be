Step to recover corrupted Ms Excel file. 1 – Download and install Ms Excel file repair tool. The home screen of software will guide you. Click on “OK” to begin the process. 2 – To recover your corrupt excel file click on “Select file” icon or click on “Search file” icon to search Excel file. 3 – With the help of checkbox you can select particular excel file, if you want to choose all file then click on “select all” button and then click on “Start button” to begin the scanning process.
