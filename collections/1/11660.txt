Apply for other official documents for your baby. You may also want to obtain a Social Security number for your baby. Some states urge you to apply for your baby's SSN almost immediately. 1  You can get a Social Security Number using the parent's vital information and a certified copy of the hospital's birth record. Get a birth certificate for your baby at the hospital after having your baby there. 2  At most hospitals, it is mandatory that you be given the birth certificate before you leave. 3  If you weren't given one forthright, ask the hospital staff for a birth certificate form before leaving with the baby.
