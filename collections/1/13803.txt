Atlas Mountains. For the volcanic cone, see Mount Atlas. The Atlas Mountains (Arabic: جبال الأطلس‎‎, Jebal al-Atlas; Berber: ⵉⴷⵓⵔⴰⵔ ⵏ ⵓⴰⵟⵍⴰⵙ, Idurar n Waṭlas) are a mountain range in the Maghreb. It stretches around 2,500 km (1,600 mi) through Algeria, Morocco and Tunisia. The range's highest peak is Jebel Toubkal, with an elevation of 4,167 metres (13,671 ft) in southwestern Morocco.
