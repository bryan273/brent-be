Tyler Perry's House of Payne (season 6) The sixth season of the Tyler Perry's House of Payne began airing on November 4, 2009, and concluded on January 19, 2011. It stars LaVan Davis as Curtis Payne, Cassi Davis as Ella Payne, Allen Payne as CJ Payne, Lance Gross as Calvin Payne, Demetria McKinney as Janine Payne and Keshia Knight Pulliam as Miranda Payne.
