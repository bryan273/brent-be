The company produces an array of protein-based and prepared food products, which are marketed under the Powered by Tyson strategy. Tyson is recognized as one of the leaders in the retail and food service markets it serves. The company provides products and services to customers throughout the United States and in more than 80 countries throughout the world. In addition, Tyson operates more than 300 facilities and offices in the United States and abroad. Tyson Prepared Foods has a location in Dallas.
