By Barbara Bolen, PhD - Reviewed by a board-certified physician. 1 Foods To Avoid When You Have Diarrhea. It is important to know what to eat for diarrhea. But it is just as important to know what not to eat when you have diarrhea. The last thing you would want to do is to make matters worse by eating foods that can potentially aggravate your GI system or increase the speed of intestinal contractions, leading to more misery.
