View Westerville Courses. The Westerville Center has more than 2,100 students enrolled per semester in its two buildings. Building 1 (WV) opened in 1982 and Building 2 (W2), located across the parking lot, opened in 2003. Westerville offers many student services such as academic advising, testing services, writing center, math, chemistry, and biology tutoring, and is a pick-up location for web ordered books.
