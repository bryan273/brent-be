Zooey Deschanel is an American actress, musician and singer-songwriter. She played the role of Mary Spuckler in the episode Apocalypse Cow. Additionally, she played herself on the FOX 25th Anniversary Special. She plays supporting and leading roles in many films, such as Elf, The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy and Yes Man. She reprises her role as Mary Spuckler in the Season 24 premiere Moonshine River and the Valentine's Day episode Love is a Many Splintered Thing.
