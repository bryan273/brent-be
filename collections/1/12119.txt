Throughout the week following his death, Adult Swim put up a bumper reading Iwao Takamoto [1925-2007]. He is buried at Mount Sinai Memorial Park Cemetery in Los Angeles in Gardens of Blessing, Section 3, Lot 1390, Space 3. There was a memorial added to the end of a Scooby-Doo film: ' Chill Out, Scooby-Doo! '.
