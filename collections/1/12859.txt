Concrete Walkway Average Costs. The cost of a concrete walkway is largely determined by the type of concrete poured. 1  A plain concrete walkway costs $4 to $6 per square foot. 2  Adding a single color or simple finish to the concrete raises the price to $6 to $10 per square foot. Expect to pay $8 to $12 per square foot for multiple colors or more elaborate patterning such as stamping or engraving. 2  Fully-custom walkways with a high level of hand-applied details (such as chemical stains or stenciling) cost $12 to $15 and up.
