Indeed says salaries for equine surgeons in the Northeast and Mid-Atlantic states range from a high of $157,000 in the District of Columbia to a low of $112,000 in Delaware. In the Southeast, equine surgeons in Georgia set the pace with an average salary of $145,000, while they make a low of $108,000 in Kentucky. Illinois leads the pack of Midwest and Plains states at $143,000 vs. the lowest pay in Nebraska, $93,000.
