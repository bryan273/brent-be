If assigning online, For those vehicles that have an existing registration number, you can apply to have the new number assigned using the DVLA online service, www.gov.uk/put-registration-number-vehicle. You will need the vehicle's Registration Certificate (V5C) and Certificate of Entitlement (V750).ou will need to have the registered keeper of the vehicle named as a Nominee on the Certificate of Entitlement (V750). You can name a Nominee at the time of purchase, or you can add or change the Nominee later by applying online via 'My Account' or by post.
