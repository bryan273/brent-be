Please use the following location map for The Aspens On Blackcomb to plan your arrival in Whistler resort. Whistler is located about 4-5 hour drive from Seattle, depending on weather conditions. From Seattle the border crossing is about 176 km or 100 miles. Travelling north take the Interstate 5 (the I-5) to the very last exit before the Canadian border crossing (do not cross the Peace Arch border crossing).
