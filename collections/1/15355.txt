Management Aptitude Test (MAT) is a standardised test being administered since 1988 to facilitate Business Schools (B-Schools) to screen candidates for admission to MBA and allied programmes. Govt. of India, Ministry of HRD approved MAT as a national level test in 2003. The Paper-Based Test will take place only on one day i.e., 06 December 2015 at one time slot i.e., 10.00am-12.30pm, whereas the Computer Based Test will take place from 12 December 2015 in different time slots.
