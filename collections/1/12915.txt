Rio de Janeiro: Annual Weather Averages. February is the hottest month in Rio de Janeiro with an average temperature of 27 °C (81 °F) and the coldest is July at 21 °C (70 °F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 7 in August. The wettest month is December with an average of 169mm of rain. The best month to swim in the sea is in March when the average sea temperature is 26 °C (79 °F).
