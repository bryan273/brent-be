Minecraft Economy Servers. Minecraft servers having some kind of economy system like iConomy, BOSEconomy, EssentialsEcon, MultiCurrency, MineConomy, eWallet, EconXP, CurrencyCore, CraftConomy, AEco... This means you will be able to earn ingame coins of some sort. You will then be able to sell, buy or even use special stuff ingame with the use of coins.
