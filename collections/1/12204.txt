You must apply to DVLA to assign the registration number to a vehicle. You must not display the registration number on a vehicle until you have received confirmation from DVLA in the form of a Registration Certificate (V5C) in the new registration number.ou will need to have the registered keeper of the vehicle named as a Nominee on the Certificate of Entitlement (V750). You can name a Nominee at the time of purchase, or you can add or change the Nominee later by applying online via 'My Account' or by post.
