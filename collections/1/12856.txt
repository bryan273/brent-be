It's Very easy. First use a grinder with a cut off wheel. Make a square using a strait edge with a black marker. Grind down about 1/2 to 3/4 inch. Remove marcite with a hammer and chisel down to the concrete. Clean area with a wire brush and wash with water and allow to dry. Marcite patch comes in 80lb pre-mixed bags. Call pool companies around your area. They can order it for you.
