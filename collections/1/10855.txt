The upfront costs of cement board siding are greater than wood or vinyl. Hardie plank can cost twice as much to buy and install compared to some siding options. With no future painting and replacement costs, it probably ends up being a better deal if you can afford it up front.ardie plank can be purchased with a wood-grain look, a plain smooth look or a stucco type finish. Hardie plank also comes in simulated cedar shingle planks.
