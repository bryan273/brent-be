Carrots can be harvested when the roots are at least 1/2 inch in diameter. Gently dig out a carrot to remove the roots without damage. The days to duration depends on your carrot variety. It can range from 50 to 90 days.he carrots start out fine, but when it is time for harvesting, the carrots do not get very bulky. The top half of the carrot is about an inch wide, but the rest of the carrot is one thin root that extends about 3 or 4 inches with no bulk at all.
