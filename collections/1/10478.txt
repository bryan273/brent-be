For other uses, see 3D (disambiguation). Kirby 3D is a 3D animated short based on Kirby: Right Back at Ya!. It was initially titled 星のカービィ～特別編～ 倒せ!!甲殻魔獣エビゾウ (Take it Down! The Crustacean Monster Ebizou) in Japan and only released on the Wii no Ma, which is the name for the Wii Channel in Japan. It was then subsequently divided into two volumes for the North American release.
