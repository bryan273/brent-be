Singular and plural nounsNouns can be either singular or plural. Singular means just one of the person, animal or thing which the noun refers to. Plural means more than one. Forming the plural of nounsThe rules for making the plural of nouns depend on the spelling and pronunciation. Most nouns form their plural by adding -s: …. Nouns: singular and plural …. Nouns used only in the singularSome nouns are used only in the singular, even though they end in -s.
