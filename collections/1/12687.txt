Title I-Helping Disadvantaged Children Meet High Standards. Program purpose authorization : Title I, formerly known as Chapter 1, is part of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, and is the foundation of the federal commitment to closing the achievement gap between low-income and other students.Nearly 14,000 of the 15,000 school districts in the nation conduct Title I programs.The original purpose of Title I was additional resources to states and localities for remedial education for children in poverty.early 14,000 of the 15,000 school districts in the nation conduct Title I programs.
