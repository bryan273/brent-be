Apples grow best in full sun away from constant wind. An apple tree that fall in partial sunlight is not likely to fully yield. Apples grow in Zones 3 to 9. They can tolerate winter temperatures as low as -40°F. Apples generally do not grow well close to the ocean where temperatures remain moderate most of the year.ext consider the space you have to grow an apple tree. Then consider how you plan to use the fruit and when you want the fruit to come to harvest, early, mid-season or late. Storing and preserving. Apples will keep for 6 to 8 weeks in a cool place; a refrigerator just above 32°F is best.
