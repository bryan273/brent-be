Typical costs: 1  A certificate in carpentry costs $750-$6,500 and can usually be completed in one year. 2  An associate's degree in carpentry costs $4,000-$20,000 and typically takes two years to complete. 3  A formal apprenticeship in carpentry may cost $0-$15,000 and last 3-4 years. For instance, the Ohio Carpenters' Joint Apprenticeship & Training Program is offered at no cost to students, and workers may earn 40%-95% of a standard carpenter's wage while they work.
