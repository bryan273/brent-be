I'm in Love Again. This article is about the Patti LaBelle album. For the title song, see Love, Need and Want You. For the Fats Domino song, see I'm in Love Again (song). I'm In Love Again is the sixth solo album released by American recording artist Patti LaBelle in 1983. LaBelle's commercial breakthrough, it featured her first top ten R&B hits, Love, Need and Want You and If Only You Knew, the latter topping the R&B chart in early 1984.
