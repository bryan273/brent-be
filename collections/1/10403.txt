Physics Notes. Car Braking Distances. The following table comes from data originally published in Popular Science and AutoWeek magazines1. Stopping distances are for new cars (1991-1995). The values of stopping distances (from 60 mi/h = 97 km/h = 27 m/s) for 55 vehicles ranged from 114 ft (35 m) to 179 ft (55 m) with an average of 140 ft (43 m).
