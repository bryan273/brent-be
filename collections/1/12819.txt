The most common rural dish is cooked mutton, often without any other ingredients. In the city, every other local displays a sign saying  buuz . Those are steamed dumplings filled with meat. Other types of dumplings are boiled in water (bansh, manti), or deep fried in mutton fat (khuushuur).uuz. The nomads of Mongolia sustain their lives directly from the products of domesticated animals such as cattle, horses, camels, yaks, sheep, and goats, as well as game.
