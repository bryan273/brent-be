Based on these guidelines, goals for all adult women, including pregnant and nursing women, is to consume 45 to 65 percent of their total calorie intake from carbohydrates. For a 2,000-calorie diet, this means consuming 225 to 325 grams of carbs each day. The Institute of Medicine has also established a minimum carbohydrate requirement of 130 grams for women, 175 grams for pregnant women and 210 grams of carbs per day for women who are breastfeeding.
