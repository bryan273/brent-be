Norfolk, Va. (WTKR) - Sailors and Marines serving in the USS Kearsarge Amphibious Ready Group left Hampton Roads Tuesday for a scheduled seven-month deployment. The ARG is made up of the USS Kearsarge, USS Arlington, and USS Oak Hill. The Kearsarge and the Arlington both deployed from Naval Station Norfolk, while the Oak Hill deployed from Joint Expeditionary Base Little Creek-Fort Story.
