The City of Kenefick, TX Kenefick, Texas, located in Liberty County, has a population of 533. The population has increased 4 percent since 2000. The average temperature in January is 50° and 82° in July. The population is made up of 187 households, with an average household size of 2 people. Kenefick households include approximately 283 men and 250 women. In Kenefick, 54 percent of residents are married and of the 187 households, 73 percent are families with two or more people living in the home. Approximately 39 percent of homes have one or more children living at home. The median age of Kenefick residents is 33 years.
