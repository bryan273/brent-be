A PRI Card / Interface might be required to terminate the PRI circuit on the PBX. A PRI line can also be used to connect two PBX systems thereby providing 30 channels between them for interoperability. Advantages of PRI Lines: If thirty separate analog trunks are taken instead of one PRI line, 1  The cost of terminating all the thirty analog trunk lines becomes higher than terminating one PRI line. 2  There would be thirty rentals to be paid instead of one consolidated lower rental for a PRI line.
