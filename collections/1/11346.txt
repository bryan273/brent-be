Malcolm Winters is a fictional character from the American CBS soap opera The Young and the Restless.Originally portrayed by Shemar Moore for two intervals, between 1994 and 2005, Darius McCrary took over the role from December 29, 2009 to October 17, 2011.Moore reprised the role for a guest appearance in September 2014.is history includes a romance with Neil's wife, Drucilla Winters, his history as a photographer, and marriages to Olivia Winters and Sofia Dupre.
