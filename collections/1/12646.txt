Gallery: Element hotel at Bridge Street. A new extended-stay hotel at Bridge Street Town Centre is weeks away from opening to the public. Element Huntsville will debut April 4 on floors 7-11 of the Westin Huntsville. The 150-room property, which is being managed by Crescent Hotels & Resorts of Fairfax, Va., will operate in the upper five floors originally built as high-end condominiums.
