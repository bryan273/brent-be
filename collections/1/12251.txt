Detailed Walkthrough. Blacksmith Writs are available to anyone who completes the Blacksmith Certification quest. Every day, you may pick up the Blacksmith Writ quest from the Equipment Crafting Writs boards. These boards are located in the major cities of the five Alliance zones, as well as The Hollow City in Coldharbour, Belkarth in Craglorn and Orsinium in Wrothgar.
