Margarine (such as Flora) contains no bad fats, is cholestrol free and has a lower saturated fat content. Margarines are also sometimes fortified with other vitamins. Flora is a source of vitamins A, D and E, whereas Rama is a source of Vitamins A, D, E, B1, B2, B6, niacin and folic acid.oft margarine is a nutritious and safe product that has been part of suggested healthy diets for years. International health organisations such as the World Heart Federation, as well as many national health authorities, recommend it. Butter and margarine are very similar when it comes to what they contain.
