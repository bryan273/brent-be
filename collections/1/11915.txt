Iceland offers a variety of accommodation, including camping, hostels, bed and breakfast, farm stays, Air BnB as well as a variety of hotels, both good value hotels and luxury hotels. There are a few hotel chains in Iceland. Foss hotels have 12 hotels around the country. The newest one, Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon, is a 4 star hotel that opened in 2016 in a gorgeous location close to Jökulsárlón glacier lagoon.
