In the majority of cases there are no detectable signs or symptoms. When they do occur, usually in very young children, they may be severe, and can include: 1  Mouth or tongue lesions. 2  Blisters in the mouth. 3  Mouth ulcers. 4  Mouth or tongue pain. 5  Lip swelling. 6  Possible swallowing difficulties. 7  Sore throat.
