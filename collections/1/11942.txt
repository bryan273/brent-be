Buprenorphine is an opioid medication. An opioid is sometimes called a narcotic. Buprenorphine oral (sublingual, given under the tongue) is used to treat narcotic addiction. Buprenorphine sublingual tablets are not for use as a pain medication. Buprenorphine may also be used for purposes not listed in this medication guide.
