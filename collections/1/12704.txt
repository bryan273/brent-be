Janes/age fotostock/Getty Images. If you are raising goats for meat, you may wonder what breed is best for the small farmer. While you can, of course, raise any breed of goat for meat, specific breeds are focused on putting on weight versus producing milk, as well as other characteristics that make the breed more desirable for meat production. If you are raising goats for meat, you don't need to buy a show goat - in fact, you shouldn't.
