MD-everyone knows what these initials stand for. But you should be aware of the fact that it’s not illegal for MDs to refer to themselves as ‘cosmetic surgeons’ – even when they have had no specialized training. FRCSC-This stand for Fellow of the Royal College of Surgeons of Canada. To be certified in plastic surgery, doctors do a 5-year residency program after receiving their MD. Training involves on-going oral and practical evaluation. If successful, the doctor becomes FRCSC.
