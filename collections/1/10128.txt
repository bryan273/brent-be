A.Profile of Contra Costa County. Contra Costa County is the second largest Bay Area county, encompassing 800 square miles, including the urban shoreline along San Francisco Bay as well as agricultural land in the Delta Region. The county's cancer death rate is higher than the state average (based on preliminary age-adjusted rates from the Cancer Surveillance Section). 2  Among the 15 most populous counties in California, Contra Costa ranked second in incidence rate for breast, ovarian, and prostate cancers.
