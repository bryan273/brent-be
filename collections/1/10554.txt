You can get some pretty big pieces of pizza in America, but none larger than a slice from the 100-pound pie that was just crowned the largest in the world. According to the New York Daily News, a team of Texas caterers called The Dirt Road Cookers broke the Guinness Book of World Records for the largest pizza when they made a pie that was 46.64 square feet and 100 pounds.
