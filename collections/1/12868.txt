The Best Starter Cruisers - 5 Bikes To Seriously Consider. There was a time – and it was not that long ago – when a “big” motorcycle was considered to be anything over 750cc. Now it seems that starter bikes have taken over this category and for some people their first cruiser motorcycle has a big V-twin engine. Some will debate this list of best starter cruisers, but certainly it’s a list of five bikes that the staff at RideApart think you should seriously consider.
