Calculating Costs Per Inmate. According to the U.S. Bureau of Prisons, the average annual cost of incarceration in Federal prisons in 2010 was $28,284 per inmate. That cost is reduced at the Federal Community Corrections Centers; in 2010 the annual cost was $25,838.ccording to the Vera Institute of Justice, the average cost of housing an inmate in the U.S. was $31,286 in 2012.
