Nail Fungus. A fungal infection causes a toenail to become discolored. The nail usually turns a dark color from dirt or debris that has become trapped underneath the nail. Other symptoms of fungal infection include a foul smell, thickened nail, crumbled nail or a dull toenail that is misshapen.njury. Injury to the nail may cause discoloration. Mild trauma to the nail can cause white spots to form on the nail. These white spots usually grow out with the nail and are not serious.
