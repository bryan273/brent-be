There are four kinds of visas which permit a person to enter and/or stay in Sri Lanka. Visit Visa. A Visit Visa is an entry permit signifying the consent of the Sri Lankan Government for the admission of a foreign national to the country.The Visa contains details of the period of time and the condition/s of the stay. Sri Lankan visa is an endorsement on a passport or a similar document to facilitate the legal entry of non Sri Lankans into the country and to regulate the period of their stay and the conditions governing such stay.
