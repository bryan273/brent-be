Hillsboro man who fell to his death on Mt. Washington had spent summers on staff at Adventist camp. According to the Deschutes County Sheriff's Office, it appeared Brian Robak fell a significant distance from the top of Mt. Washington. Rescuers with the Oregon Army National Guard located Robak's body during an air search Saturday afternoon. The Deschutes County Sheriff's Office was notified at 3:32 a.m.
