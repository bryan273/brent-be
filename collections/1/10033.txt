Fill any voids in the bottom layer of soil and uniformly tamp the soil to a depth of 8. Place a geotextile fabric over the top of the compacted soil. Place and rake gravel to a depth of 4 on top of the geotextile fabric. Fill any voids in the top layer of gravel. Use a plate compactor to compact the gravel base. Place and rake sand to a depth of 1-1/2 on top of the compacted gravel.
