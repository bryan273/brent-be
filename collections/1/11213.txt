By Sandie Glass4 minute Read. I’ve seen red tomatoes, green tomatoes, and even yellow and purple tomatoes in the produce department, but for years I’d only seen red ketchup on the store shelf. Heinz ketchup is an iconic American brand, practically perfect in every way. Why mess with it? In 2000, that’s exactly what Heinz decided to do, when it turned the ketchup world upside-down when they began rolling our vibrant, crazy-colored ketchup in purple, pink, orange, teal, and blue varieties.
