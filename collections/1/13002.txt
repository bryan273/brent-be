She had already performed the original vocals on Pump Up The Jam and now finally the world could bear witness to her strutting deliverance of Get Up. By the end of 1990, Pump Up The Jam had reached pole position on the Billboard 12 singles dance chart. Eric Martin (4) AKA MC Eric also contributed vocally to Bogaert's first opus (This Beat Is Technotronic. After a short collaboration with Colin Case AKA Einstein (2) and Melissa on the single Turn It Up (1990), Technotronic's second album, Body To Body (1991), was released only in Europe.
