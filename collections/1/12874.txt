Photo by Jim Smerecak. Purchase color photos at www.BeeNews.com Three months ago, a new cycling studio opened in Western New York. Rebel Ride, 6449 Transit Road, East Amherst, offers strength and conditioning training, spinning classes and accessibility to most people, no matter what their ability level, according to owner Erika Brason. “This is something that I’m very passionate about, and it really started from scratch,” Brason said.
