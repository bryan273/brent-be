Diarrhea During Early Pregnancy. The diarrhea during early part of pregnancy (also called the morning sickness in many places) is a condition that is normally short lived. Nausea, vomiting and watery bowel movements need to be treated with care.fter the initial period of morning sickness is complete, diarrhea may stage a comeback due to many reasons many of which are related to pregnancy. 1  Change in Dietary Habits. 2  Exercise. 3  Bacteria and Viruses. 4  Pregnancy Hormones. 5  Parasites. 6  Other Reasons.
