anemone. n. 1. (Plants) any ranunculaceous woodland plant of the genus Anemone of N temperate regions, such as the white-flowered A. nemorosa (wood anemone or windflower).Some cultivated anemones have lilac, pale blue, pink, purple, or red flowers.See also pasqueflower Compare sea anemone.nemone. n. 1.
