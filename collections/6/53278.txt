Searcy County, Arkansas (AR) County population in 2014: 7,929 (all rural); it was 8,261 in 2000. County owner-occupied with a mortgage or a loan houses and condos in 2010: 1,096. County owner-occupied free and clear houses and condos in 2010: 1,633. County owner-occupied houses and condos in 2000: 2,737.
