Briggs cites Hozier, Alabama Shakes and Jack Garrett as influences, and it's easy to see their fingerprints on her work. Since then, Bishop Briggs has released a handful of singles and an EP, each with their own take on the unique mash-up of soul with dance beats. When we catch her on the phone, she's on her first headlining tour of the U.S and is still stunned by her own meteoric rise.
