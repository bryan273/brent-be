When you install Outlook, an Inbox repair tool (scanpst.exe) is also installed on your PC. The Inbox repair tool can resolve problems by scanning your Outlook data files, and repairing errors. To use the Inbox repair tool, exit Outlook, and then follow the steps in the following Office article: . If you are running Windows 10, Windows 7 or Windows Vista, click Start. 2  If you are running Windows XP, click Start, and then click Run. 3  Type Outlook.exe /safe, and then click OK. 4  If the problem is resolved, click Options on the File menu, and then click Add-Ins.
