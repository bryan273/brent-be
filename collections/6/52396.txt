BID, also b.i.d. or BD, is a medical abbreviation for bis in die, meaning to give medication twice a day. TID, also t.i.d., is a medical abbreviation for ter in die, meaning to give medication three times a day. QID also q.i.d, is a medical abbreviation which means to give the medication four times a day. PRN is a medical abbreviation for pro re nata, meaning to give medication as the situation demands or according to need. Qxh or Qxd once every x hours or once every x days. More abbreviations here.
