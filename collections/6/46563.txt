Trim fat from meat. Brown meat on all sides in hot oil in a 4- to 6-quart Dutch oven. Drain fat. Mix 3/4 cup water, Worcestershire sauce, bouillon, basil and salt and pepper to taste. Pour over roast. Bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 1 hour. Peel a strip of skin from center of each new potato or peel and quarter sweet potatoes. Add potatoes, carrots, onions and celery to pot.
