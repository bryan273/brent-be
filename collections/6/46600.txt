Raw pork can contain several bacteria and parasites, and raw pork sausage is no exception. When preparing pork sausage, take special care to avoid exposing yourself and your family to bacteria such as salmonella and staphylococcus aureus, and to a particular parasite known as trichinella spiralis.tep 5. Photo Credit Katy Jane Conlin/Demand Media. Broil, grill or bake pork sausage links. For baking, you should set your oven to 325 degrees F. Sausage links are safe to eat at an internal temperature of 160 degrees F as measured by a meat thermometer, or when no pink meat remains within the link.
