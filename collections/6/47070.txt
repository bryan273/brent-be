Definition. noun. (1) A polysaccharide consisting of a linear chain of β (1→4) linked D-glucose units: (C 6 H 10 O 5) n. (2) The fibrous carbohydrate found in the cell walls of green plants, some algae and oomycetes. It provides strength and rigidity to plant cells. Supplement. The cellulose is a straight chain polymer of carbohydrates.
