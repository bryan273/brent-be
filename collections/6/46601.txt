Sausage is a meat product generally consisting of pork, but also made of beef, veal, lamb, poultry, or a combination of several of these meats. Sausage meat is generally ground, mixed with other ingredients, such as rice, cereal, soybean flour, or dried milk solids, and seasoned with herbs and spices.o poach fresh sausage, bring water in a sauce pan to a boil and then add the uncooked sausages. Reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and cover. Cook for approximately 10 minutes and then finish cooking and browning them by frying, grilling or broiling.
