Foreign Currency and Currency Exchange Rates. You must express the amounts you report on your U.S. tax return in U.S. dollars. If you receive all or part of your income or pay some or all of your expenses in foreign currency, you must translate the foreign currency into U.S. dollars.f there is more than one exchange rate, use the one that most properly reflects your income. You can generally get exchange rates from banks and U.S. Embassies. If your functional currency is not the U.S. dollar, make all income tax determinations in your functional currency.
