Harper put on quite a show his rookie season, highlighted by a game where Harper would manage to record 25 points, 16 rebounds, 4 assists and 5 steals. Harper would average 22.9 points, 4.8 rebounds, 4.8 assists, 2.5 steals and 1 block that season. Although Harper did average a sloppy 4.2 turnovers during that season. Despite this performance (minus the turnovers), Harper was ranked second in rookie of the year voting to Chuck Person. In his sophomore season, Harper averaged 15.4 points, 3.9 rebounds, 4.9 assists, 2.1 steals and 0.9 blocks.
