How did the Tet Offensive affect public opinion about the Vietnam War? How did the Tet Offensive affect public opinion about the Vietnam War? On January 31, 1968 — the Vietnamese New Year, called Tet, — communist forces unleashed massive attacks on U.S. positions throughout Vietnam. The Tet Offensive, televised nightly in the U.S., shocked many Americans who previously had the idea the U.S. was easily taking care of the enemy.
