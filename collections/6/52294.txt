Buy 100 shares of GE and sell 50 shares of GE and another 50 shares of GE later in the day = 1 round trip. A round trip occurs in trading when you open and close a position in the same trading day. Ex. Buy 100 shares of GE and sell 100 shares of GE on the same day = 1 round trip Round trips are an important thing to consider as to not violate the rules related to pattern day trading.
