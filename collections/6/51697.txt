Sealed with a kiss! Heidi Klum greets estranged husband with peck on the cheek as they support children at soccer match. Heidi Klum may have moved on from her estranged husband, singer Seal, but after seven years of marriage she still has a soft spot for the Fly Like An Eagle crooner. On Saturday the 41-year-old supermodel met up with the British recording artist, 51, to support their children at their weekend soccer matches.
