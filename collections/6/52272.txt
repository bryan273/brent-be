Parking or standing is not allowed: 1  In front of a driveway. 2  Within 20 feet (6 m) of a crosswalk at an intersection. 3  Within 30 feet (10 m) of a traffic light, STOP sign or YIELD sign.  Within 20 feet (6 m) of a fire station driveway, or within 75 feet (23 m) on the opposite side of the road.
