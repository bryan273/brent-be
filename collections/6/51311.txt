Hecate is the daughter of the Titans Perses and Asteria. She is the Greek goddess of magic, sorcery, witchcraft, crossroads, trivial knowledge, and necromancy.According to The House of Hades, she is also the goddess of the Mist.ecate was a third-generation Titaness born to Asteria and Perses. She sided with the Olympians during the First Titan War, and was allowed to retain power after the Titans were defeated.
