Antidiuretic hormone analogues: Deamino-D-arginine vasopressin (DDAVP), oxytocin. Deamino-D-arginine vasopressin (DDAVP) and oxytocin prevent the excretion of water by the kidneys, thus can cause hyponatremia especially when administered with electrolyte-free water.
