Rock of Cashel Opening Hours. The Rock of Cashel is open year-round on this schedule: Mid-March to mid-June: 9:30 am. – 5:30 p.m. Mid-June to mid-September: 9 a.m. – 7 p.m. Mid-September to mid-October: 9 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. Mid-October to mid-March: 9 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. Note! The last admission of the day is 45 minutes before closing time.
