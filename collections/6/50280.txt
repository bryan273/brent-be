Exchanges and Rates. The amount of foreign currency necessary to trigger a report depends on the exchange rate at the time of the transaction. If you do a lot of business with tourists or foreign visitors, you might accept, say, Mexican pesos.anks can get an exemption from the reporting requirements for their business customers who do a lot of sales in cash, such as supermarkets or restaurants, and therefore frequently deposit more than $10,000. However, federal regulations push the reporting requirements down the line to those businesses.
