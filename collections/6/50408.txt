Belonging to the Cucumis genus (Cucumis melo var. cantalupensis 'Athena'), Athena is a variety of Cantaloupe. Athena is a variety of hybrid Cantaloupe . Highly disease resistant, especially resistant to mildews. This variety is an Fruit that typically grows as an Annual, which is defined as a plant that matures and completes its lifecycle over the course of a single year.
