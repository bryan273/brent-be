Vaginal lubrication - Vaginal discharge. 1  The lubricative fluid contains water, pyridine, squalene, urea, acetic acid, lactic acid, complex alcohols and glycols, ketones, and aldehydes. [1] 2  Vaginal fluid is slightly acidic and can become more acidic with certain sexually transmitted diseases.
