News: 7/6/2003. ASHANTI REMAKES I FOUND LOVIN. In 1990, Jeff Redd, a New Jack-Era artist signed to MCA Records released the single I Found Lovin, and the song connected with many fans of R&B music during that time period. 13 years later, Murder Inc's Ashanti pays homage to the track with her own version, which can be found on her latest LP, 'Chapter II'.
