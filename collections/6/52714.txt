Smith-Morris, Carolyn; Morales-Campos, Daisy; Alvarez, Edith Alejandra Castaneda; Turner, Matthew. Hispanic Journal of Behavioral Sciences, v35 n1 p35-60 Feb 2013. Research on core cultural values has been central to behavioral and clinical research in ethnic groups. Familismo is one such construct, theorized as the strong identification and attachment of Hispanic persons with their nuclear and extended families.
