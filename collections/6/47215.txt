It is your duty as a citizen of Broward County to serve when you have been summoned for jury service. You are not required to serve on jury duty more than once a year, so you will not receive a summons more than once in a calendar year. Sometimes you can go years without being summoned.Remember that being called for jury duty does not necessarily mean that you will actually sit on a jury.ou are not required to serve on jury duty more than once a year, so you will not receive a summons more than once in a calendar year.
