President Barack Obama took 71 percent of the Hispanic vote in 2012, up from 67 percent in 2008. African-Americans vote overwhelmingly for Democrats (Obama got 93 percent of the African-American vote in 2012, and John Kerry won 88 percent of that vote in 2004). Asians, who were the fastest-growing ethnic group between 2000-2010, have tended Democratic this century, with 73 percent voting for Obama in 2012.
