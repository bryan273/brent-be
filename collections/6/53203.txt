Standard Delivery includes an all-day time window from 6 a.m. to 8 p.m., we'll call you 30 minutes prior to arrival. The driver will place your items in an area at your home or jobsite accessible by delivery equipment. 2-hour or 4-hour time windows can be selected at an additional cost. Threshold delivery permits the driver to bring your order across a threshold closer to your home or job site, such as the garage or backyard. This service is available for an additional fee.
