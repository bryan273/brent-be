Look out for one or more of the following: 1  a hard lump on the front or side of a testicle. 2  swelling or enlargement of a testicle. 3  an increase in firmness of a testicle. 4  pain or discomfort in a testicle or in the scrotum (the sac that holds the testicles). 5  an unusual difference between one testicle and the other.
