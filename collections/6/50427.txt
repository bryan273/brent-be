Formica, or any other brand of household laminate, is most often used for kitchen counters and wall decoration. Laminate is easy to clean and relatively durable, although, being plastic, it can melt if exposed to excessive heat.elect the correct Formica or other laminate for your project. Most places that sell laminate will provide sample chips for you to take home and compare to the house's color scheme. Take several of these home and test for additional properties to help you decide.
