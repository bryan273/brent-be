All lizards can lose tails, but iguanas are among the few who can grow them back. John Foxx/Stockbyte/Getty Images. While not all lizards can regrow their tails if they lose them, there are several, including the green anole lizard, most salamander species, geckoes and iguanas, who possess this amazing ability.erves. Nerves seem to play a critical role in the regrowth of lizard tails. Lizards who have nerve damage at the base of the tail as their tails drop aren't able to regenerate the tails.
