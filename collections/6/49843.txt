Where To Donate Used Christmas Cards & Greeting Cards. Don't let those old cards sit around and become clutter. Find out where to donate used Christmas cards and greeting cards to worthwhile charities or other places in your community. At the end of each holiday or birthday season you are left with a big stack of well wishes from friends and family who've thoughtfully sent you and your family cards. While a few of these may be worth saving for their sentimentality the majority of them are really just going to become clutter in your home over time.
