The Breaker Blade is the largest sword in the game, beating the Blade of Grass. It has an average speed and the highest knockback of any sword in the game, making it useful for keeping enemies at bay. It is dropped by the Wall of Flesh. 01:35. Breaker Blade Terraria HERO. 1  Given its long reach, the Breaker Blade is very useful for farming some materials. 2  When combined with Titan Glove, this weapon's knockback becomes Insane, similar to Gungnir.  Despite its long range, it can only hit 1 enemy per swipe.
