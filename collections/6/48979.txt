This article is a Stub and is not complete. You can help Spectrobes Wiki by expanding it. Commander Grant, or simply Grant, is a character in the Spectrobes series. He is the head of the Nanairo Planetary Patrol. In the webisodes he is voiced by Kirk Thornton. Most of Commander Grant's early life remains unknown. As a young man, he worked as an officer for the Nanairo Planetary Patrol. During one mission, his ship flew into the Kaio system and crashed on the planet Wyterra. However, he was saved from his flaming ship by Kamtoga and Krux.
