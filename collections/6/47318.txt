Best Answer: Those are actually 3 different things, so I'll go over each one separately. ----OVA = Original Video Animation. In the simplest terms, an OVA is an anime that has never aired on TV (or never released in theaters), but instead went straight to the home video market. Often, one or more OVAs will be released to go along with an anime TV series. A prequel, a sequel, a spin-off, an alternate universe, a side story, etc...
