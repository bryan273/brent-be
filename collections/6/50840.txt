In Outlook 2007, go to the Tools menu, click  Trust Center , then choose  Add-ins  and click Go. In Outlook 2010 and Outlook 2013, switch to the File tab, click  Options , choose  Add-ins  and click Go. Now all you need to do is to untick the add-ins and close the dialog.hen open Windows Explorer and navigate to the C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office\OFFICE14 folder if you are using Outlook 2010. If you have Outlook 2013 installed, it will be C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office\OFFICE15. Double-click Scanpst.exe and click  Browse  to select the .pst or .ost file you want to check.
