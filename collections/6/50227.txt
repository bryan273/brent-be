Michael Phelps was born in Baltimore, Maryland, and raised in Towson, Maryland. He moved to Ann Arbor, Michigan to attend college and to continue working with his coach, Bob Bowman. In 2010, Michael was listed as living in Ann Arbor, Michigan. In 2012 Michael was reported as having bought a home in the Canton area of Baltimore, Maryland. Fanmail.biz gives the address of the Michael Phelps Foundation in Portland, Maine as his fan mail address.
