A Mason City Operations & Maintenance worker plows the sidewalks in East Park after a late season snow storm hit North Iowa late Wednesday and early Thursday. MASON CITY | A wide swath of at least 4-6 inches of snow fell in North Iowa late Wednesday and early Thursday. Some areas received much more. The heaviest snowfalls reported to the National Weather Service appeared to be some 60-70 miles west of Mason City in Kossuth and Palo Alto counties.
