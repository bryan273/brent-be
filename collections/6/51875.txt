Hallmark Channel To Develop Sherryl Woods’ Chesapeake Shores Novels Into Primetime Series. Hallmark Channel will develop Sheryl Woods’ Chesapeake Shores novels for a potential primetime series to launch in 2015. The deal, announced today by Harlequin, comes on the heels of an agreement between Harlequin MIRA and Woods that includes two new Chesapeake Shores books in 2015 to coincide with the possible series launch. Woods introduced the Chesapeake Shores series in 2009 with The Inn At Eagle Point.
