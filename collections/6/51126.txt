Leukocytosis, a WBC above 10,000, is usually due to an increase in one of the five types of white blood cells and is given the name of the cell that shows the primary increase. 1  Neutrophilic leukocytosis = neutrophilia. 2  Lymphocytic leukocytosis = lymphocytosis. 3  Eosinophilic leukocytosis = eosinophilia.
