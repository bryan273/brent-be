Congressman Yells 'You Lie' at Obama During Speech. WASHINGTON -- In an extraordinary breach of congressional decorum, a Republican lawmaker shouted You lie at President Barack Obama during his speech to Congress Wednesday. The incident came directly after Obama said, There are also those who claim that our reform effort will insure illegal immigrants. This, too, is false. You lie! Rep. Joe Wilson, R-S.C., shouted from his seat on the Republican side of the chamber.
