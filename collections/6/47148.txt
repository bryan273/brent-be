Long County Jail. Long County Jail is a County Jail used by the jurisdictions of Long to confine inmates for short periods while awaiting trial or processing. As inmates are staying for only a short period, it has fewer amenities than a jail or prison. Inmates do, however, have access to bathroom facilities and are provided with meals during their stay.
