Population of Rogers City, MI. Last official estimated population of Rogers City city (Presque Isle County**, Michigan state) was 2,747 (year 2014)[1]. This was 0.001% of total US population and 0.03% of total Michigan state population. Area of Rogers City city is 8.3 mi² (=21.6 km²)[6], in this year population density was 329.10 p/mi².
