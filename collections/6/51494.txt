Your legs are propelled by your hamstrings, quadriceps and calf muscles. Stretching your legs will help you to prevent injury and also to prevent muscle soreness after walking, running or cycling.his exercise shows you how to stretch your quads in a lunge position while sitting on the ball. However, you can also rest your hips on the ball, bend one heel toward your butt and grasp your ankle, pulling your heel close to your butt, to stretch your quads.
