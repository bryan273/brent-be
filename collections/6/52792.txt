If your car is new, most of the required paperwork will be handled by the dealership. If you buy a used car, you must not only submit a title transfer application, but depending on your state's requirements you might also have to provide an odometer reading, a vehicle identification number(VIN), and a bill of sale. This is a how to guide to transfer a vehicle's title in your state. It will explain filling out the title certificate to properly transfer ownership. The state-by-state pages may also provide downloadable title applications. You can find phone numbers, Web addresses, and DMV addresses.
