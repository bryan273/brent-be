Rachel Maddow. Rachel Maddow was born on April 1, 1973 in Castro Valley, California, USA as Rachel Anne Maddow. She is an actress, known for The Rachel Maddow Show (2008), The McVeigh Tapes: Confessions of an American Terrorist (2010) and The Assassination of Dr. Tiller (2010). She is married to Susan Mikula.
