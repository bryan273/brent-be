Directions. 1  Boil milk, add butter and sugar, and then cool. 2  Add yeast and milk and beat egg yolks in well with beater. 3  Add this mixture to flour.  Let rise for 2 1  hours. Roll out lightly on floured board to ⅜ inch thickness, and cut out round circles.  Lighly cover with cloth and let rise again about ½ hour.
