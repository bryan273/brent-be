Michael LaCapa. Born in Phoenix, Arizona, Michael Lacapa moved to Whiteriver, Arizona on the Fort Apache Indian Reservation at the age of one. He was the 2nd of eight children with ethnic roots from the Hopi, Tewa and Apache Tribes. Michael’s boyhood passion for art was “fine tuned” after earning a Bachelors Degree in Secondary Art Education for Arizona State University and graduate studies in printmaking for Northern Arizona University.
