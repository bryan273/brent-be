Age. The IRS allows you to claim an exemption for your child who is under 19 years of age. You might be able to continue claiming an exemption for your college-age child from age 19 through 23 years old, provided she meets the IRS' other tests as a qualifying child.ou can claim a $3,700 exemption if your college-age child qualifies as a dependent. The Internal Revenue Service allows you to reduce the amount of your taxable income based on the number of exemptions you claim.
