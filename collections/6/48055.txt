Common risperidone side effects may include: 1  headache; 2  dizziness, drowsiness, feeling tired; 3  tremors, twitching or uncontrollable muscle movements; 4  agitation, anxiety, restless feeling; 5  depressed mood; 6  dry mouth, upset stomach, diarrhea, constipation; 7  weight gain; or. 8  cold symptoms such as stuffy nose, sneezing, sore throat.
