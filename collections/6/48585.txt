Population of Bay County. Last official estimated population of Bay County was 169,294 (year 2014)[1]. This was 0.053% of total US population and 0.8% of total Florida state population. Area of Bay County is 1,033.2 mi² (=2675.9 km²), in this year population density was 163.86 p/mi². If population growth rate would be same as in period 2010-2014 (+0.07%/yr), Bay County population in 2017 would be 169,626 .
