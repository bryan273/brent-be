The border that separates the United States and Canada is 5,527 miles long, which includes a long stretch of Alaska and the United States and Mexico border, which is 2,013 miles long. From Hawaii to Maine, the distance totals 5,100 miles. West Quoddy Head, Maine, is the most eastern point, and Attu Island, Ala., is the most western point. The longest north-south distance in the contiguous United States is 1,650 miles from Maine to Florida. Both the most northern and southern points are not in the contiguous states. The southernmost point is Hawaii Island, and the most northern point is Point Barrow, Ala.
