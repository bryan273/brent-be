If you happen to find that you have a frozen bank account, there are a couple of ways to restore your account.Why Is It Frozen?The first thing to do is find out why your bank account is frozen.This is most likely because you have an unpaid debt. The easiest way to unfreeze your account is to pay your debt (a.k.a. “vacate the judgment”). 2  If your account was frozen because of a past-due collection, simply pay the debt and take proof of payment to your courthouse or bank.
