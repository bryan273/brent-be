As your metabolism adjusts to pregnancy, you can develop a number of digestive problems, including diarrhea. It usually doesn't mean anything is wrong.But it can be distressing. Diarrhea may be related to your prenatal vitamin, your attempts to eat better, or it could just be caused by a bug you caught.all Doctor If: 1  Diarrhea is serious or lasts longer than 24 hours. 2  You get dehydrated or dizzy. 3  You feel you want to try a medication to help. 4  Diarrhea contains blood or pus, or is black and tarry. 5  You have a fever or severe abdominal pain.
