The mammary gland of the dairy cow consists of four separate glands each with a teat. Milk which is synthesized in one gland cannot pass over to any of the other glands. The right and left side of the udder are also separated by a median ligament, while the front and the hind quarters are more diffusely separated.o produce 1 liter of milk 500 ltr. of blood have to pass through the udder. When the cow is producing 60 liters of milk per day, 30,000 liters of blood are circulating through the mammary gland.
