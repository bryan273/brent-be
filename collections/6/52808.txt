The World Is Yours  is a 1994 song by rapper Nas. It was the third single from his debut album Illmatic, released a month after the album.ongs that have sampled The World Is Yours include:  1  Dead Presidents  by Jay-Z.  2  Thief's Theme  by Nas.  3 Usual Suspects by Rick Ross featuring Nas and Kevin Cossom.  4 Jealousy Woes II by Eminem.
