The Mayflower Compact was drawn up with fair and equal laws, for the general good of the settlement and with the will of the majority. The Mayflower s passengers knew that the New World s earlier settlers failed due to a lack of government.They hashed out the content and eventually composed the Compact for the sake of their own survival.
