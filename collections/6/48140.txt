Lt. Joseph M. D'Accardi. Obituary Condolences. D'ACCARDI Lt. Joseph M. D'Accardi, U.S. Navy, 87, passed away Tuesday May 12, 2009. Papa Joe was a veteran of WWII and Korean War, one of the founding members of the North Florida Soaring Society, a member of the Quiet Birdmen, and retired agent of Massachusetts Mutual Insurance Co.
