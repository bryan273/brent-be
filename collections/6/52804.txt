. The World Is Yours  is a 1994 song by rapper Nas. It was the third single from his debut album Illmatic, released a month after the album. It is considered by some music critics one of the greatest hip-hop songs ever recorded; About.com ranked it seventh greatest rap song of all time.ongs that have sampled The World Is Yours include:  1  Dead Presidents  by Jay-Z.  2  Thief's Theme  by Nas.  3 Usual Suspects by Rick Ross featuring Nas and Kevin Cossom.  4 Jealousy Woes II by Eminem.
