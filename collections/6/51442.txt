The root word fic meaning ‘make’ is a very productive root in English and you should make yourself comfortable with it. 1  purification: process of ‘making’ pure. 2  certification: act of ‘making’ sure. 3  pacification: act of ‘making’ peace. 4  Pacific: ‘making’ peace. 5  artificial: ‘made’ with human skill. 6  magnificent: ‘made’ great.
