What is the difference between smut and rust? Answer Wiki. SMUTS are the fungal diseases of cereals and other members of grass family that cause the ears ( particularly the ovaries) to turn black or sooty. It is caused by a fungus of the order ustilaginales. the name given because of the black powdery appearance of infected host plant. Haustoria is present. They complete their life cycle with one host. e.g. 1.Loose smut of wheat :- host- triticum vulgare and pathogen- Ustilago tritici.
