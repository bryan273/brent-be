Make sure your health care providers and facilities are contracted with your plan. The most important thing you can do to prevent being balance billed is to find out in advance whether your health care providers, including hospitals, clinics, and other facilities, are contracted with your health plan.f the services are not covered, you will be responsible for all charges. Find out in advance how much you might be balance billed. If you know you are going to be treated by a provider not under contract with your health plan, you may be able to find out in advance how much you will have to pay.
