Canadian citizens using Canadian passports do not require a visa to enter Taiwan for tourism or business stays less than ninety (90) days. Canadians must have a confirmed departure ticket, and be arriving at either C.K.S.his cannot be confirmed in writing. Nationals of the following countries do not need a visa: European Union (EU); Australia (30 days), CANADA, Iceland, Japan, Korea Rep, Liechtenstein, Malaysia (30 days), Monaco, New Zealand, Norway, Singapore (30 days), Switzerland, UK, USA.
