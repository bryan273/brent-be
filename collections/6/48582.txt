Bay County, Florida (FL) County population in 2014: 178,985 (88% urban, 12% rural); it was 148,217 in 2000. County owner-occupied with a mortgage or a loan houses and condos in 2010: 27,480. County owner-occupied free and clear houses and condos in 2010: 15,727. County owner-occupied houses and condos in 2000: 40,892.
