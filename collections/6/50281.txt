These laws apply equally to U.S. currency and foreign currency. The amounts that trigger reports are expressed in dollar terms; the amounts of foreign currency necessary to require a report depend on the exchange rate.anks can get an exemption from the reporting requirements for their business customers who do a lot of sales in cash, such as supermarkets or restaurants, and therefore frequently deposit more than $10,000. However, federal regulations push the reporting requirements down the line to those businesses.
