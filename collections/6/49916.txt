General Gamestop Opening Hours. On Weekdays, the GameStop stores are normally open Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Some busy inner-city locations will stay open until 10 PM, however. In the Weekend most GameStop shops are open from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM on Saturday, and open from 11:00 PM and until at 6:00 PM, in general. Some busy stores stay open until 7 PM on Sundays, so call ahead. Typical Gamestop Hours of Operation.
